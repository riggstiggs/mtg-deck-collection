## Deck Strategy
**Archetype:** Jeskai (Blue/Red/White) Artifact Aggro
**Format:** Custom

*   **Goal:** This deck aims to win by creating a massive army of Golem tokens with "Simulacrum Synthesizer." The strategy is to flood the board with cheap artifacts to power up the Golems and then overwhelm the opponent with a wide, powerful board.
*   **How it Plays:** The deck plays a low-to-the-ground aggro game. The early turns are spent deploying cheap artifacts like "Clay-Fired Bricks" and "Spring-Loaded Sawblades." Once "Simulacrum Synthesizer" is in play, every subsequent historic spell (which includes all your artifacts) creates a Golem token that gets progressively larger. The deck uses its interaction to protect its board and push through damage.

*The key card that the entire deck is built around.*
