# Omnath Deck Guide: "Locus of Landfall"

## Commander Strategy
**Omnath, Locus of Creation**
*   **Archetype:** 4-Color (WURG) Landfall
*   **Goal:** This deck is a "value engine" that revolves around playing multiple lands per turn to generate overwhelming advantage from Omnath's abilities.
*   **How it Plays:** The early game is dedicated to ramping and playing cards that let you play extra lands. Once Omnath is on the field, each land drop becomes a huge event: the first gains you 4 life, the second adds four mana, and the third deals 4 damage to each opponent. The goal is to create a snowball effect of value that your opponents will struggle to keep up with.
