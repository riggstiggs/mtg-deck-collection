# Hearthhull, the Worldseed Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Hearthhull, the Worldseed**
*   **Role:** The Sacrifice Outlet & Damage Engine.
*   **Goal:** To sacrifice lands for card advantage while simultaneously draining your opponents' life and triggering powerful "Lands-Matter" payoffs.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Station:** Hearthhull is a Spacecraft (Artifact Creature — Spacecraft). It uses the *Station* mechanic to grow in power based on your other creatures, eventually becoming a massive 6/7 threat.
    2.  **Land Sacrifice:** Hearthhull's primary ability allows you to sacrifice a land to draw two cards and play an additional land. This keeps your hand full and fuels your graveyard.
    3.  **Life Drain:** Whenever you sacrifice a land (to Hearthhull or other effects like *Harrow*), each opponent loses 2 life. In a Jund Lands deck, this can quickly end the game.

## 1. The Land Sacrifice Synergy
*Cards that benefit from lands entering the graveyard or provide extra sacrifice outlets.*
*   **The Gitrog Monster:** Whenever a land is put into your graveyard from anywhere, you draw a card. With Hearthhull, sacrificing a land draws you three cards total.
*   **Titania, Protector of Argoth:** Creates a 5/3 Elemental token whenever a land hits your graveyard. This creates an army while you draw cards.
*   **Korvold, Fae-Cursed King:** Grows and draws cards whenever you sacrifice any permanent, including lands.
*   **Braids, Arisen Nightmare:** Another sacrifice outlet that punishes opponents for not matching your resource attrition.

## 2. Land Recursion & Graveyard Value
*Since you're sacrificing lands, you need ways to bring them back.*
*   **Soul of Windgrace / Lord Windgrace (Spirit):** Can pull lands from your graveyard directly back to the battlefield or to your hand.
*   **Splendid Reclamation:** The ultimate reset button. Put all lands from your graveyard onto the battlefield tapped.
*   **Worldsouls's Rage:** Deals damage to any target and puts X lands from your graveyard or library into play.
*   **Centaur Vinecrasher / Multani, Yavimaya's Avatar:** Creatures that grow larger for every land in your graveyard.

## 3. Landfall & Board Presence
*Rewarding you for the lands you play after sacrificing them.*
*   **Omnath, Locus of Rage:** Creates 5/5 Elementals whenever a land enters. Since Hearthhull lets you play extra lands, you can trigger this multiple times per turn.
*   **Rampaging Baloths:** Creates 4/4 Beasts on Landfall.
*   **Moraug, Fury of Akoum:** Grants extra combat steps on Landfall, allowing you to attack with your massive Spacecraft multiple times.
*   **Oracle of Mul Daya / Augur of Autumn:** Let you play lands from the top of your library to keep the engine moving.

## 4. Spacecraft & Artifact Support
*Unique cards from the "Edge of Eternities" theme.*
*   **Exploration Broodship:** Likely another Spacecraft or support vehicle that provides utility or mana.
*   **Eumidian Wastewaker / Evendo Brushrazer:** Creatures that interact with the "Station" mechanic or lands-matter theme.
*   **Planetary Annihilation:** A high-impact sorcery that likely wipes the board while benefiting from your sacrificed lands.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Drain Out:** Sacrifice multiple lands per turn with *Hearthhull* and *The Gitrog Monster* to drain opponents with Hearthhull's passive ability and *Mayhem Devil*.
*   **Elemental Swarm:** Use *Titania* and *Omnath* to create a board of 5/3s and 5/5s that opponents cannot answer.
*   **Commander Damage:** A 6/7 indestructible (if you have *Hammer of Purphoros*) Spacecraft can finish off players quickly.
