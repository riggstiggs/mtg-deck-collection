# Ulalek, Fused Atrocity Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Ulalek, Fused Atrocity**
*   **Role:** The Stack Multiplier & Tribal Powerhouse.
*   **Goal:** To cast Eldrazi spells and use Ulalek's unique triggered ability to copy everything on the stack, leading to an overwhelming board state and multiple "On Cast" triggers.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Casting Triggers:** Unlike many commanders, Ulalek cares about the *act* of casting. When you cast an Eldrazi, you can pay {C}{C} to copy *all* spells and *all* other triggered/activated abilities you control on the stack.
    2.  **On-Cast Value:** Eldrazi often have powerful "When you cast this spell" abilities (like *Artisan of Kozilek* reanimating a creature). Ulalek copies these triggers, allowing you to get double or even triple value.
    3.  **Devoid & Colorless Identity:** While Ulalek is colorless (Devoid), its mana cost includes all five colors, allowing you to play every Eldrazi ever printed.

## 1. The High-Impact Eldrazi
*Creatures that provide massive value when cast or copied.*
*   **Artisan of Kozilek:** When you cast it, you return a creature from your graveyard. With Ulalek, you can pay {C}{C} to get two 10/10s and two reanimations.
*   **World Breaker:** Exiles an artifact, enchantment, or land on cast. Ulalek doubles this disruption.
*   **Sire of Stagnation:** A massive draw engine that punishes opponents for playing lands.
*   **Ulamog's Dreadsire:** Creates 10/10 Eldrazi tokens. Copying this with Ulalek creates a truly terrifying board.
*   **Azlask, the Swelling Scourge:** Buffs your Eldrazi Scions and Spawn, turning your small tokens into lethal Annihilators.

## 2. Colorless Support & Ramping
*Generating the massive amounts of mana required to fuel the Hive.*
*   **Forsaken Monument:** The most important support piece. It makes your colorless lands tap for extra mana, buffs your creatures, and provides life gain.
*   **Morophon, the Boundless:** Reduces the colored mana cost of all Eldrazi to zero. This makes Ulalek's hybrid cost significantly easier to manage.
*   **Herald's Horn / Urza's Incubator:** Cost reducers that ensure you can cast multiple Eldrazi per turn.
*   **Mystic Forge:** Allows you to cast Eldrazi and artifacts from the top of your library, keeping the chain moving.

## 3. Token Generation (Scions & Spawn)
*Small creatures that provide mana and bodies for sacrifice.*
*   **Awakening Zone / Skittering Invasion:** Consistent sources of Eldrazi Spawn/Scions.
*   **Drowner of Hope:** Creates tokens on entry and can sacrifice them to tap down threats.
*   **Sifter of Skulls:** Generates a 1/1 Scion whenever a nontoken creature you control dies.

## 4. Board Control & Stack Manipulation
*   **All Is Dust:** The ultimate Eldrazi board wipe. It forces everyone to sacrifice colored permanents, usually leaving your entire board untouched.
*   **Eldrazi Confluence:** A versatile spell that can exile creatures, buff your own, or ramp you.
*   **Kozilek's Return:** An instant-speed wipe that triggers for 5 damage when you cast a high-CMC Eldrazi.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Annihilator Overrun:** Attack with multiple high-power Eldrazi. Even if they are blocked, the Annihilator triggers will strip opponents of their resources.
*   **Stack Explosion:** Use Ulalek to copy a high-impact spell like *Eldrazi Conscription* or *Return of the Wildspeaker* to end the game on the spot.
*   **Infinite Value:** With *Morophon* and *Ulalek*, you can often cast your entire library in a single turn cycle if you have enough colorless mana.
