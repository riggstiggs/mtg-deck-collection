# Omo, Queen of Vesuva Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Omo, Queen of Vesuva**
*   **Role:** The Type Fixer & Mana Accelerator.
*   **Goal:** To use "everything counters" to turn your lands and creatures into every possible subtype, unlocking powerful tribal and land-type synergies that are normally difficult to assemble.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Everything Counters:** Omo places counters that grant all types. On a land, this means it is simultaneously a **Gate, Locus, Urza's, Desert, Forest, Island, Mine, Tower,** etc.
    2.  **Mana Explosion:** By turning lands into *Locus* or *Urza's* types, you can generate 10-20 mana from just a few lands.
    3.  **Tribal Toolbox:** By turning creatures into every type, they benefit from any tribal lord or effect (e.g., *March from Velis Vel*).

## 1. The Land Toolbox (The "Everything" Lands)
*The primary way the deck wins is by abusing land subtypes.*
*   **The Locus Engine:** *Cloudpost*, *Glimmerpost*, and *Trenchpost* produce mana based on the number of Loci on the battlefield. With Omo, *every* land with a counter becomes a Locus, leading to exponential mana growth.
*   **The Urza's Tron:** *Urza's Mine*, *Urza's Tower*, and *Urza's Power Plant*. Omo completes the "Tron" set instantly by giving any one land all three types.
*   **The Gate Package:** *Basilisk Gate* and *Simic Guildgate*. Basilisk Gate provides a massive power boost to a creature based on the number of Gates you control.
*   **The Dark Depths Combo:** Use *Thespian's Stage* or *Copy Land* to copy *Dark Depths* (which is also a land Omo can fix) to create Marit Lage, a 20/20 indestructible flyer.

## 2. Creature Type Synergies
*Making your board "Everywhere" at once.*
*   **March from Velis Vel:** A sorcery that turns all your lands into creatures of every type. Since they are already every land type, they become a massive, versatile army.
*   **Sage of the Maze:** A creature that taps for mana based on your Gates. Since Omo makes your lands Gates, this becomes a massive mana dork.
*   **Rampant Frogantua:** A huge threat that grows with your lands and has Trample/Reach.

## 3. High-End Payoffs
*Using your massive mana to end the game.*
*   **Apex Devastator:** A 10/10 with Cascade, Cascade, Cascade, Cascade. You will often cast this for "free" using your Locus/Tron mana and flip 4 more spells off the top.
*   **Hydroid Krasis / Hydra Broodmaster:** X-spells that scale infinitely with your mana, drawing cards and creating a massive board.
*   **Finale of Revelation:** Draws you X cards and untaps 5 lands (which, if they are Loci, could be another 20+ mana).
*   **Avenger of Zendikar / Scute Swarm:** Landfall triggers that create an army of plants or insects as you play your utility lands.

## 4. Land Tutors & Recursion
*Ensuring you find your key pieces.*
*   **Expedition Map / Sylvan Scrying:** Search for your *Cloudpost* or *Dark Depths* immediately.
*   **Hour of Promise:** Finds *any* two lands. In this deck, it almost always finds two Loci or the Dark Depths pieces.
*   **Ramunap Excavator:** Lets you play lands from your graveyard, essential for reusing *Thespian's Stage* or *Vesuva*.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Locus Overrun:** Generate 20+ mana, cast *Apex Devastator* and *Avenger of Zendikar*, and overwhelm the table.
*   **Marit Lage:** Use the *Dark Depths* combo to create a 20/20 and swing for the win.
*   **Basilisk Gate Strike:** Give a creature +10/+10 or more using the Gate subtype and swing with unblockable or flying.
