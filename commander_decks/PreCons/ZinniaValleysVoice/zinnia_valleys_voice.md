# Zinnia, Valley's Voice Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Zinnia, Valley's Voice**
*   **Role:** The Token Manufacturer & 1-Power Lord.
*   **Goal:** To flood the battlefield with tiny (but powerful) 1/1 "Offspring" copies of your utility creatures, building a wide board that buffs Zinnia and overwhelms opponents through sheer "Enter the Battlefield" (ETB) value.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Offspring {2}:** Zinnia gives every creature spell you cast the Offspring ability. By paying {2} extra generic mana, you get a 1/1 token copy of that creature. This is incredibly powerful with creatures that have ETB abilities.
    2.  **Base Power 1:** Zinnia gets +1/+0 for every other creature you control with base power 1. Since all your Offspring tokens are 1/1s, Zinnia can quickly become a 10/4 or larger.
    3.  **Jeskai Value:** The Blue/Red/White color identity allows for excellent spell support, token doubling, and aggressive combat buffs.

## 1. High-Value Offspring Targets
*Creatures with "Enter the Battlefield" triggers that you want to double.*
*   **Cloudblazer / Inspiring Overseer:** Cast these with Offspring to draw multiple cards and gain significant life.
*   **Sun Titan / Restoration Angel:** Use Offspring to double up on your recursion or blinking effects, creating a feedback loop of value.
*   **Aether Channeler / Skyclave Apparition:** Powerful removal on bodies. Offspring allows you to handle two threats for the price of one spell plus {2}.
*   **Inferno Titan:** The "Fireball" Titan. Getting a 1/1 version of this still gives you the full 3-damage ETB and attack trigger.

## 2. The 1-Power Support
*Cards that benefit from or protect your 1/1 tokens and small creatures.*
*   **Tetsuko Umezawa, Fugitive:** Makes all your 1-power creatures unblockable. Since your Offspring tokens are base 1/1, your entire army becomes impossible to stop.
*   **Agate Instigator:** A Bloomburrow standout that deals damage to each opponent whenever another creature enters the battlefield under your control.
*   **Curiosity Crafter:** Draws you a card whenever a creature token (your Offspring) deals combat damage.
*   **Junk Winder:** Taps down opponents' permanents whenever a token enters, effectively locking them out of the game.

## 3. Token & Combat Payoffs
*Scaling your wide board into a win condition.*
*   **Devilish Valet:** Its power doubles every time a creature enters. Creating an Offspring token and the original creature in one turn can turn the Valet into a 16/3 or 32/3 trampler.
*   **Jazal Goldmane:** Spend mana to give your entire board +X/+X, where X is the number of creatures you control. This is the primary finisher for the deck.
*   **Combat Celebrant:** Offspring this to get two extra combat phases (if you can protect the 1/1 token).

## 4. Board Control & Recursion
*   **Dusk // Dawn:** A perfect wipe for this deck. *Dusk* destroys large creatures (which you don't have many of), while *Dawn* returns all your small creatures from the graveyard to your hand.
*   **Farewell / Time Wipe:** Essential board wipes to reset the game, noting that you can often rebuild faster through Offspring.
*   **Storm of Souls:** Reanimates your entire graveyard, turning them all into 1/1 flyers. This instantly triggers Zinnia's power boost and generates massive ETB value.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Unblockable Swarm:** Use *Tetsuko Umezawa* to swing through with a board of 1/1s and a massive *Zinnia*.
*   **Jazal Overrun:** Activate *Jazal Goldmane* with 10+ creatures out to give everyone +10/+10 and end the game.
*   **Valet One-Shot:** Use the *Devilish Valet* with a few token-generating spells or Offspring casts to one-shot an opponent with 64+ power.
