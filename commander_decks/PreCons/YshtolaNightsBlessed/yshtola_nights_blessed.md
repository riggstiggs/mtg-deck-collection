# Y'shtola, Night's Blessed Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Y'shtola, Night's Blessed**
*   **Role:** The Spellslinger Engine & Life Drain Finisher.
*   **Goal:** To control the game through powerful 3+ CMC noncreature spells, drawing cards and draining opponents' life until you can finish them with explosive magic.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Life Loss Reward:** At each end step, if a player lost 4 or more life (very easy to trigger with Y'shtola's own ability or combat), you draw a card.
    2.  **Aetheric Drain:** Whenever you cast a noncreature spell with mana value 3 or greater, Y'shtola deals 2 damage to each opponent and you gain 2 life. This turns every counterspell, board wipe, and utility artifact into a life-swing tool.
    3.  **Esper Control:** The Blue/Black/White color identity provides the best removal and card draw in the game.

## 1. The Scions of the Seventh Dawn (The Party)
*Iconic FFXIV characters providing diverse utility.*
*   **Alisaie & Alphinaud Leveilleur:** Likely provide spell-based value and protection, potentially working together to buff the team.
*   **Thancred Waters:** An evasive threat that likely provides removal or sabotage effects.
*   **Urianger Augurelt:** Provides card selection and potentially predicts/influences the top of the library.
*   **G'raha Tia, Scion Reborn:** A versatile legendary that can likely serve as a secondary win condition or provide a wide range of buffs.
*   **Tataru Taru:** Provides economic support (Treasure or cost reduction) to fuel your expensive spells.

## 2. The Ascians & Antagonists
*High-impact threats that dominate the late game.*
*   **Emet-Selch of the Third Seat:** A powerful legendary creature that likely interacts with the graveyard or provides massive board-control effects.
*   **Hermes, Overseer of Elpis:** Likely provides temporal manipulation or high-value card advantage.
*   **Fandaniel, Telophoroi Ascian:** A chaotic threat that punishes opponents for casting spells or attacking.

## 3. Job Actions & Tools
*Artifacts and enchantments representing FFXIV jobs and mechanics.*
*   **Blue Mage's Cane / Astrologian's Planisphere:** Artifacts that provide spell-based utility and scale with your Spellslinger theme.
*   **Dancer's Chakrams / Reaper's Scythe:** Equipment that buffs your creatures while providing life-drain or combat triggers.
*   **White Auracite:** An artifact that likely serves as a storage for energy or a piece of a larger victory condition.

## 4. Spellslinger Support
*Maximizing the value of your noncreature spells.*
*   **Archmage Emeritus:** Draws you a card every time you cast an instant or sorcery.
*   **Torrential Gearhulk:** Lets you recast a powerful instant from your graveyard for free.
*   **Sublime Epiphany / Dig Through Time:** High-impact spells that trigger Y'shtola's drain ability while providing massive card advantage.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Drain to Zero:** Use Y'shtola's passive ability and *Exsanguinate* to drain the entire table while keeping your own life total healthy.
*   **Scion Alpha Strike:** Buff your legendary creatures with *Champions from Beyond* and swing for the win.
*   **Ascian Control:** Lock the game down with *Emet-Selch* and *Propaganda* until your opponents are forced to concede to your superior resource management.
