# Ashling, the Limitless Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Ashling, the Limitless**
*   **Role:** The Value Multiplier & Cheat Engine.
*   **Goal:** To cast powerful Elemental creatures for cheap using the "Evoke" ability she grants them, and then double up on their "Enter the Battlefield" (ETB) triggers by creating token copies when they die.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Evoke {4}:** Ashling lets you cast *any* Elemental from your hand for just {4} generic mana. This is a huge discount for expensive creatures like *Maelstrom Wanderer* or *Omnath, Locus of Rage*.
    2.  **The Sacrifice Trigger:** When you cast for Evoke, the creature enters and is immediately sacrificed.
    3.  **The Clone:** When it dies, Ashling creates a token copy of it with Haste. This token enters the battlefield, triggering all ETB effects a *second* time.
    4.  **The Result:** For 4 mana, you get two rounds of ETB triggers and a hasty attacker (the token) that lasts until the end step. You can keep the token permanently if you pay {W}{U}{B}{R}{G}, but usually, the value is enough.

## 1. The Payoffs: High-Impact Elementals
*These are the creatures you want to "Evoke" for 4 mana to get massive value.*
*   **Maelstrom Wanderer:** Normally costs 8 mana. Evoke it for 4. It enters, Cascades twice. Dies. Token created. Token enters, Cascades *two more times*. That's 4 free spells for 4 mana!
*   **Omnath, Locus of Rage:** Landfall generator. Even if the token dies, you get a 5/5 Elemental token when the original dies (if you stack triggers right) or lightning bolts when they leave.
*   **Yarok, the Desecrated:** Doubles all ETB triggers. If you Evoke an Elemental with Yarok out, things get out of hand fast.
*   **Avenger of Zendikar:** Creates a massive army of plants. Evoking this gives you the plants twice.
*   **Mulldrifter:** The classic. Evoke for 4 (instead of its normal 3, oddly, but works with Ashling's ability to make a token) to draw 4 cards total.
*   **Titan of Industry:** A massive toolbox. Destroy artifacts, gain life, make tokens, or put counters on things.
*   **Fury / Endurance / Subterfuge / Grief:** The "Incarnation" elementals that already have Evoke. Ashling gives you extra value when they die.

## 2. The Elemental Tribe Support
*Cards that care about Elementals.*
*   **Horde of Notions:** The classic 5-color Elemental commander. Here, it acts as recursion, letting you replay your Evoked elementals from the graveyard.
*   **Risen Reef:** The value engine. Whenever an Elemental enters, you draw or ramp. With Ashling, you get double triggers.
*   **Incandescent Soulstoke:** Can cheat Elementals into play for {1}{R} and buff them.
*   **Smokebraider / Flamebraider:** Ramp specific to Elementals.

## 3. The "Lorwyn Eclipsed" Specials
*Unique cards from this specific precon theme.*
*   **Belonging / Impulsivity / Jubilation / Lamentation:** Likely new Incarnation or Elemental concepts that provide value on ETB or death.
*   **Mass of Mysteries / Subterfuge:** Interaction pieces on Elemental bodies.

## 4. Ramp & Fixing
*Since Ashling is a 5-color identity commander (due to her WUBRG ability), you need a robust mana base.*
*   **Chromatic Lantern / Dryad of the Ilysian Grove:** Fixes your mana perfectly.
*   **Nature's Lore / Three Visits / Farseek:** Fetches the specific colors you need (Triomes/Shocks).
*   **Bloom Tender / Faeburrow Elder:** Big mana dorks that tap for colors based on your permanents.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Overwhelming Value:** You will simply out-resource opponents by drawing cards and putting permanents into play faster than them.
*   **Combat Damage:** The tokens Ashling creates have Haste. If you Evoke a *Maelstrom Wanderer* or *Omnath*, you can swing for big damage immediately.
*   **Burn:** *Omnath, Locus of Rage* deals damage when Elementals die. Since you are sacrificing Elementals constantly for Evoke, this turns into a machine gun.
