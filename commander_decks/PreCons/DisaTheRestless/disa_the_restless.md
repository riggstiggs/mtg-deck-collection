# Disa the Restless Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Disa the Restless**
*   **Role:** The Tribal Enabler & Token Generator.
*   **Goal:** To fill your graveyard with Lhurgoyf cards (triggering free reanimation) and deal combat damage to create a swarm of Tarmogoyf tokens.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Library-to-Battlefield:** Whenever a Lhurgoyf permanent card is put into your graveyard from *anywhere* other than the battlefield (e.g., via *Stitcher's Supplier*, *Grisly Salvage*, or discarding it), Disa puts it directly onto the battlefield. This cheats mana costs and rewards self-mill.
    2.  **Tarmogoyf Generation:** Whenever one or more creatures you control deal combat damage to a player, you create a Tarmogoyf token. Since Tarmogoyf scales with the number of card types in all graveyards, these tokens often enter as 4/5s or larger.
    3.  **Jund Value:** The deck uses the classic Jund (BRG) philosophy of using the graveyard as a second hand and creatures as versatile tools.

## 1. The Lhurgoyf Pack
*The core creatures that scale with card types in graveyards.*
*   **Tarmogoyf (Tokens):** Created by Disa. The ultimate "beater" that gets bigger as the game progresses.
*   **Pyrogoyf:** Deals damage to a target whenever an Elemental or Lhurgoyf enters the battlefield, based on the Goyf's power.
*   **Barrowgoyf:** Has Lifelink and Deathtouch. When it deals combat damage, you mill cards and can put a creature from among them into your hand.
*   **Necrogoyf:** Has Lhurgoyf-based power and forces each player to discard a card on your upkeep.
*   **Polygoyf:** A Myriad-based Lhurgoyf that creates temporary copies of itself, triggering more ETB effects.

## 2. Graveyard Enablers (Self-Mill & Discard)
*These cards trigger Disa's ability to cheat Lhurgoyfs into play.*
*   **Stitcher's Supplier:** Mills three cards on ETB and death. If any are Lhurgoyfs, they enter for free.
*   **Grisly Salvage / Grapple with the Past:** Instant-speed mill that finds pieces and triggers Disa.
*   **Deadbridge Chant:** Mills ten cards on entry and provides recurring value every turn.
*   **Faithless Looting:** A cheap way to discard Lhurgoyfs stuck in your hand to trigger Disa.

## 3. The Jund Support
*Powerful non-tribal cards that support the strategy.*
*   **Coram, the Undertaker:** A massive threat that scales with the highest power in graveyards and provides card advantage from everyone's graveyard.
*   **Ziatora, the Incinerator:** A sacrifice outlet that turns your large Lhurgoyfs into direct damage and Treasures.
*   **The Reaver Cleaver:** Generates massive mana (Treasures) when your high-power creatures hit.
*   **Grist, the Hunger Tide:** A planeswalker that mills, creates tokens, and provides repeatable removal.

## 4. Payoffs & Finishers
*Closing out the game with overwhelming force.*
*   **Altar of the Goyf:** Gives your creatures Trample and massive stat boosts.
*   **Chandra's Ignition:** Target a huge Tarmogoyf token to wipe the board and burn your opponents for 6-10 damage each.
*   **Syr Konrad, the Grim:** Punishes opponents for every creature that hits or leaves your graveyard.
*   **Archon of Cruelty:** A top-tier reanimation target that swings the game through life drain and card advantage.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Goyf Swarm:** Use Disa to create 3+ Tarmogoyf tokens per turn cycle and overrun opponents with efficient stats.
*   **Direct Damage:** Use *Pyrogoyf* triggers and *Ziatora* flings to finish off low-life players.
*   **Graveyard Grind:** Outlast opponents through the sheer recursion power of *Disa*, *Coram*, and *Junji, the Midnight Sky*.
