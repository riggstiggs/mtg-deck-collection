# Auntie Ool, Cursewretch Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Auntie Ool, Cursewretch**
*   **Role:** The Engine & Finisher.
*   **Goal:** To manipulate -1/-1 counters across the board to generate card advantage and drain your opponents' life.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Ward - Blight 2:** Protects Auntie Ool by forcing opponents to weaken their own creatures just to target her.
    2.  **Card Draw:** Whenever -1/-1 counters are placed on *your* creatures, you draw a card. This turns self-inflicted penalties (like from *Blight* or *Devoted Druid*) into fuel.
    3.  **Life Drain:** Whenever -1/-1 counters are placed on *opponents'* creatures, they lose life. This makes removal and combat extremely punishing.

## 1. The Counter Generators
*These cards distribute -1/-1 counters to trigger Auntie Ool and weaken the board.*
*   **The Scorpion God:** A massive threat that puts counters on creatures and draws you cards when they die.
*   **Hapatra, Vizier of Poisons:** Creates 1/1 Deathtouch Snakes whenever you place a -1/-1 counter.
*   **Black Sun's Zenith / Soul Snuffers:** Mass -1/-1 counter placement that can drain your opponents for huge amounts in a single turn.
*   **Midnight Banshee:** Puts a -1/-1 counter on all non-black creatures every upkeep.
*   **Archfiend of Ifnir:** Puts counters on all opponents' creatures whenever you cycle or discard.

## 2. The Payoffs: Wither & Infection
*Cards that benefit from the presence of counters or the act of placing them.*
*   **Necroskitter:** Steals your opponents' creatures when they die with -1/-1 counters on them.
*   **Kulrath Knight:** Completely shuts down opponents' creatures if they have any counters on them (preventing them from attacking or blocking).
*   **Massacre Girl, Known Killer:** Gives your creatures Wither and draws you cards when creatures with counters die.
*   **Blowfly Infestation:** Creates a chain reaction where one creature dying puts a counter on another, potentially wiping the board and draining all opponents.

## 3. Self-Inflicted Value
*Cards that use -1/-1 counters as a resource, which Auntie Ool turns into card draw.*
*   **Devoted Druid:** Produces mana and puts counters on itself, drawing you cards with Auntie Ool out.
*   **Dusk Urchins:** Draws cards when it attacks or dies, accelerated by the counters you place on it.
*   **Channeler Initiate:** A mana dork that starts with counters you can remove for mana, or keep for Auntie Ool triggers.

## 4. Unique "Lorwyn Eclipsed" Additions
*Cards specific to this precon's theme.*
*   **The Reaper, King No More:** A powerful legendary creature that likely supports the -1/-1 theme through a "King of the Withered" style effect.
*   **Wickersmith's Tools / Sinister Gnarlbark:** Artifact and creature support for the Blight and Wither mechanics.
*   **Ferrafor, Young Yew / Village Pillagers:** New utility creatures from the Lorwyn return.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Drain Out:** Auntie Ool and *Zulaport Cutthroat* style effects will slowly deplete opponents as creatures are weakened and die.
*   **Board Lock:** Use *Kulrath Knight* to prevent opponents from interacting while you chip away.
*   **Mass Reanimation:** *Living Death* or *Aberrant Return* can bring back your entire engine after a wipe.
