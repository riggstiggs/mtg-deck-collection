# Tidus, Yuna's Guardian Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Tidus, Yuna's Guardian**
*   **Role:** The Counter Strategist & Proliferate Engine.
*   **Goal:** To manage and grow various types of counters across your creatures to unlock powerful abilities and generate massive card advantage.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Counter Manipulation:** At the beginning of combat, Tidus lets you move a counter from one creature to another. This is essential for moving +1/+1 counters to evasive threats or "saving" valuable counters from a creature about to leave the battlefield.
    2.  **Cheer (Proliferate):** Whenever your creatures with counters deal combat damage, Tidus lets you draw a card and Proliferate. This grows your entire board (and your Summons) while keeping your hand full.
    3.  **Bant Synergy:** Green/White/Blue provides the best support for counters, token generation, and protection.

## 1. The Guardians (The Party)
*Iconic characters from FFX that provide defensive and offensive utility.*
*   **Yuna, Grand Summoner:** The heart of the deck. She likely provides bonuses to "Summon" spells and creatures, potentially bringing them back or reducing their costs.
*   **Auron, Venerated Guardian:** Likely a powerful combatant that provides First Strike or Vigilance to the team.
*   **Wakka, Devoted Guardian:** Likely provides Reach or accurate targeting (removal/disruption).
*   **Lulu, Stern Guardian / Kimahri / Rikku:** Provide a mix of magic-based removal, protection, and resource theft.

## 2. The Summons (Aeons)
*High-impact creatures that represent Yuna's summons.*
*   **Summon: Valefor / Ixion / Yojimbo / Magus Sisters:** These are powerful entities that often enter with specific counters or have abilities that scale with Proliferate.
*   **Sin, Unending Cataclysm:** A game-ending threat that likely resets the board or punishes opponents for every counter on the battlefield.

## 3. The Sphere Grid & Leveling
*Cards that facilitate the counter and growth theme.*
*   **Sphere Grid:** An enchantment that likely allows you to spend mana or energy to place counters on your creatures, mimicking the FFX progression system.
*   **Hardened Scales / Together Forever:** Classic counter support that ensures every counter you place is twice as effective and your creatures are resilient.
*   **Blitzball Stadium:** An artifact or land that provides combat bonuses or generates tokens/counters through "Blitzball" matches.

## 4. Proliferate & Card Advantage
*Scaling your board into the late game.*
*   **Inexorable Tide:** Proliferates every time you cast a spell, quickly turning your small creatures into giants.
*   **Fathom Mage / Chasm Skulker:** Creatures that grow with counters and draw you cards, creating a feedback loop with Tidus.
*   **Walking Ballista:** A lethal outlet for your infinite counters.

## 5. Interaction & Protection
*   **Farewell / Damning Verdict:** Powerful board wipes, with *Damning Verdict* specifically sparing your creatures since they all have counters.
*   **Inspiring Call:** Protects your equipped/countered army and draws you cards.
*   **Endless Detour:** A versatile Simic-style removal that can handle any threat on the board or in a graveyard.

## Winning the Game
*   **Aeon Alpha Strike:** Use Tidus to move counters onto *Valefor* or *Ixion* and swing for lethal with Proliferate-backed stats.
*   **Sin's Cataclysm:** Deploy *Sin* to wipe the opponents' boards while your Guardians remain protected.
*   **Ballista Finish:** Proliferate *Walking Ballista* to 10+ counters and ping your opponents to death.
