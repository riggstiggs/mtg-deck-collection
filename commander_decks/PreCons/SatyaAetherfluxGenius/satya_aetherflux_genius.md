# Satya, Aetherflux Genius Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Satya, Aetherflux Genius**
*   **Role:** The Token Manufacturer & Energy Specialist.
*   **Goal:** To create temporary (or permanent) token copies of your most powerful creatures every time Satya attacks, while generating and spending "Energy" as a secondary resource.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Attack Trigger:** Whenever Satya attacks, you create a tapped and attacking token copy of another creature you control.
    2.  **Energy Generation:** Satya gives you {E}{E} (two energy counters) on attack.
    3.  **The Choice:** At the end step, you must sacrifice the token unless you pay Energy equal to its mana value. If you have a high-energy engine, you can keep your best creatures permanently.

## 1. High-Value Clone Targets
*Creatures with powerful "Enter the Battlefield" (ETB) or attack triggers.*
*   **Combustible Gearhulk:** When you copy this, you either draw three cards or deal massive damage to an opponent.
*   **Myr Battlesphere:** Creates four Myr tokens every time it's copied, quickly building an overwhelming board.
*   **Angel of Invention:** Provides a +1/+1 anthem and can create more tokens via Fabricate.
*   **Goldspan Dragon:** Copying this provides a hasty attacker that generates Treasures and doubles their value.
*   **Skyclave Apparition:** Repeatedly exile your opponents' nonland permanents by creating new copies of the Apparition.

## 2. The Energy Engine
*Cards that generate and spend Energy to fuel Satya and other payoffs.*
*   **Aetherworks Marvel:** One of the strongest energy payoffs. Spend {E}{E}{E}{E}{E}{E} to cast a card from the top six for free.
*   **Gonti's Aether Heart:** Generates energy whenever an artifact enters (including your tokens) and can be sacrificed for an extra turn.
*   **Aether Refinery:** Doubles all energy you gain, making it easy to keep even the most expensive token copies.
*   **Decoction Module:** Generates energy whenever a creature enters the battlefield.
*   **Whirler Virtuoso:** A classic outlet that turns energy into 1/1 Thopter tokens.

## 3. Synergy & Token Support
*Enhancing your tokens and board presence.*
*   **Brudiclad, Telchor Engineer:** A powerhouse in this deck. Brudiclad can turn all your 1/1 Myrs and Thopters into copies of the best token you created with Satya (e.g., a *Combustible Gearhulk* token).
*   **Legion Loyalty:** Gives all your creatures Myriad. When you attack, you create even more tokens, leading to an explosion of ETB triggers.
*   **Cayth, Famed Mechanist:** Supports both tokens (Populate) and counters (Proliferate), making her a perfect utility piece.
*   **Professional Face-Breaker:** Rewards your aggressive attacks with Treasure and card advantage.

## 4. Winning the Game
*   **Infinite Combats:** *Lightning Runner* can grant extra combat steps for {E}{E}{E}{E}{E}{E}{E}{E}. If Satya and other pieces generate enough energy, you can attack infinitely.
*   **Token Swarm:** Use *Brudiclad* or *Legion Loyalty* to overwhelm the table with high-power copies of *Myr Battlesphere* or *Gearhulks*.
*   **Aetherworks Marvel / Gonti's Heart:** Use your massive energy reserves to take extra turns or cast your biggest threats for free.

## 5. Interaction & Board Control
*   **Farewell / Austere Command:** Premier white board wipes to reset the game when behind.
*   **Localized Destruction:** A specialized wipe that likely interacts with energy or artifacts.
*   **Akroma's Will:** A premier finisher that gives your army protection and double strike to end the game on the spot.
