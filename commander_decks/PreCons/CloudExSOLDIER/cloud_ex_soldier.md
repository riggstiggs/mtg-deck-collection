# Cloud, Ex-SOLDIER Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Cloud, Ex-SOLDIER**
*   **Role:** The Voltron Engine & Treasure Generator.
*   **Goal:** To suit up Cloud (and other SOLDIERs) with equipment to draw massive amounts of cards and generate mana through Treasures.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Auto-Attach:** When Cloud enters, you can attach an equipment you control to him for free. This bypasses high equip costs (like *Colossus Hammer*).
    2.  **Card Draw:** Whenever Cloud attacks, you draw a card for *each* equipped attacking creature. This encourages going wide with equipment across multiple creatures, not just Cloud.
    3.  **The Limit Break (Treasures):** If Cloud's power is 7 or greater when he attacks, you create two Treasure tokens. This represents his high damage potential and provides the mana to cast more equipment or expensive spells like *Ultimate Magic: Meteor*.

## 1. The Armory (Equipment)
*High-impact gear to buff Cloud and trigger his abilities.*
*   **Colossus Hammer:** The best target for Cloud's auto-attach. +10/+10 for {1} mana, instantly putting Cloud at 14 power and triggering his Treasure ability.
*   **Behemoth Sledge:** Provides +2/+2, Lifelink, and Trample. Essential for keeping your life total high and ensuring damage gets through.
*   **Champion's Helm / Darksteel Plate / Mithril Coat:** Protection is key. These keep Cloud on the board by granting Hexproof or Indestructible.
*   **Sword of the Animist:** Ramps you every time Cloud attacks.
*   **Wrecking Ball Arm / Conformer Shuriken:** Specific Final Fantasy themed equipment with unique utility for the SOLDIER archetype.

## 2. The Avalanche & Friends (Support)
*Iconic Final Fantasy VII characters providing backup.*
*   **Sephiroth, Fallen Hero:** A powerful threat that likely punishes opponents or dominates combat.
*   **Tifa, Martial Artist:** Likely provides combat buffs or extra attacks.
*   **Aerith, Last Ancient:** Provides protection and potentially recursion for your fallen creatures.
*   **Barret / Red XIII / Vincent:** Utility creatures that provide removal, card selection, or direct damage.
*   **Zack Fair:** A legendary soldier who likely synergizes with Cloud or provides extra combat value.

## 3. Materia & Summons
*Spells and artifacts representing FF VII's magic system.*
*   **Ultimate Magic: Meteor:** A massive board wipe or finisher.
*   **Ultimate Magic: Holy:** Likely a powerful white removal or protection spell.
*   **Summoning Materia / Kujata:** Cards that let you cheat creatures into play or provide persistent value.
*   **SOLDIER Military Program:** An enchantment that buffs your Soldier creatures and supports the equipment theme.

## 4. Ramp & Fixing
*A Naya (RGW) deck needs consistent mana.*
*   **Nature's Lore / Cultivate / Rampant Growth:** Standard green ramp.
*   **Professional Face-Breaker:** Turns your Treasures into card draw.
*   **Puresteel Paladin:** Reduces equip costs to zero and draws cards whenever an equipment enters.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Commander Damage:** A 14/14 Cloud (with *Colossus Hammer*) ends games in two hits.
*   **Treasure Storm:** Generate enough Treasures to cast your entire hand or fuel a massive *Ultimate Magic: Meteor*.
*   **Equipped Army:** Go wide with equipped creatures like *Barret* and *Tifa*, drawing 3-5 cards per attack and overwhelming the board.
