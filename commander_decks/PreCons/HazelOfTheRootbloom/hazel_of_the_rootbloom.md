# Hazel of the Rootbloom Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Hazel of the Rootbloom**
*   **Role:** The Token Engine & Mana Battery.
*   **Goal:** To flood the board with various tokens (Squirrels, Food, Treasures) and use them to generate massive amounts of mana or copy your most powerful tokens.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Mana Generation:** Hazel can tap *any* untapped tokens you control to add mana. This turns non-mana tokens like Food, Clues, and Squirrels into a massive mana resource.
    2.  **Token Cloning:** At the beginning of your end step, Hazel copies a token you control. If you choose a Squirrel token, she creates *two* copies instead.
    3.  **Golgari Synergy:** The deck utilizes the black/green philosophy of sacrificing small creatures for value and using the graveyard to fuel token generation.

## 1. The Squirrel Hive
*The core of your army and Hazel's favorite cloning targets.*
*   **Chatterfang, Squirrel General:** The undisputed king of Squirrels. He creates a Squirrel whenever you create any other token and can sacrifice Squirrels to remove creatures.
*   **Scurry of Squirrels:** Creates tokens on combat damage and provides a consistent source of Squirrels for Hazel to double.
*   **Deep Forest Hermit:** Enters with four Squirrel tokens and buffs all other Squirrels.
*   **Chitterspitter:** A repeatable source of Squirrel tokens that grows them larger over time.
*   **Squirrel Nest:** Enchant a land to tap for a Squirrel every turn.

## 2. Token Value & Mana Fixers
*Cards that expand your token board and provide utility.*
*   **Academy Manufactor:** One of the strongest cards in the deck. Whenever you create a Clue, Food, or Treasure, you get one of each. This gives Hazel three times as many tokens to tap for mana.
*   **Beledros Witherbloom:** A massive threat that creates a Pest token every upkeep and can untap all your lands.
*   **Tireless Provisioner:** Creates Treasures or Food whenever a land enters, providing consistent token fuel.
*   **Sword of the Squeak:** A specialized equipment that gives the equipped creature a massive boost based on the number of tokens you control.

## 3. Aristocrat & Drain Effects
*Using your tokens' deaths to finish off opponents.*
*   **Zulaport Cutthroat / Nadier's Nightblade:** Drain your opponents whenever your tokens leave the battlefield.
*   **Poison-Tip Archer:** A defensive flyer that drains everyone whenever any creature dies.
*   **Morbid Opportunist:** Keeps your hand full by drawing cards whenever creatures die.
*   **Plumb the Forbidden:** A massive card draw spell that rewards you for sacrificing a board full of tokens.

## 4. Payoffs & Finishers
*Transforming your small tokens into game-ending threats.*
*   **Beastmaster Ascension:** Once active, your tiny Squirrels become 6/6 threats.
*   **End-Raze Forerunners:** A massive buff that grants Vigilance, Trample, and +2/+2 to your entire board.
*   **The Odd Acorn Gang:** A powerful Squirrel-themed threat from Bloomburrow that scales with your tribe.
*   **Swarmyard Massacre:** A board wipe that likely spares your Squirrels and Insects while destroying everything else.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Squirrel Overrun:** Build a massive army of Squirrels, double them with Hazel, and swing for the win with *Beastmaster Ascension*.
*   **Token Drain:** Use sacrifice outlets like *Woe Strider* or *Chatterfang* to trigger your aristocrat effects and drain opponents to zero.
*   **Infinite Value:** Use *Hazel* and *Academy Manufactor* to generate so much mana and card draw that you can cast your entire deck.
