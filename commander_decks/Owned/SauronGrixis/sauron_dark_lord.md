# Deck Guide: Sauron, the Dark Lord (Grixis Amass)

## Commander Strategy
**Sauron, the Dark Lord**
*   **Archetype:** Grixis Midrange / Amass / Ring Temptation
*   **Bracket:** 2 (Core)
*   **Core Goal:** Utilize the "Ring Tempts You" mechanic to trigger Sauron's discard-draw-4 ability. Build a massive Orc Army and protect your commander with built-in Ward and targeted equipment. The deck thrives on incremental value and a resilient command zone.

---

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🗡️ The Commanders & The Army
*   **Sauron, the Dark Lord:** The heart of the deck. Provides protection (Ward), consistent token generation (Amass), and an incredible card-draw engine that triggers whenever you are tempted by the Ring.
*   **Saruman, the White Hand:** A secondary engine that Amasses whenever you cast *any* noncreature spell, making your Army grow even when you're just casting removal or ramp.
*   **Lord of the Nazgûl:** A massive late-game threat. He makes your spells cheaper and turns every instant/sorcery into a 9/9 Wraith with Menace once you've assembled enough Wraiths.
*   **Witch-king of Angmar:** A powerful defensive piece. He punishes opponents for attacking you and can become Indestructible by discarding a card (synergizing with Sauron's draw).
*   **Mauhúr, Uruk-hai Captain:** Doubles the number of +1/+1 counters placed on your Orc Army.
*   **Warg Rider:** An aggressive threat that grants your Orc Army **Myriad**, allowing you to attack everyone at once.
*   **Gothmog, Morgul Lieutenant:** Grants your tokens (including the Orc Army) Deathtouch, making them nearly impossible to block profitably.
*   **Grishnákh, Brash Instigator:** A "Threaten" effect on a stick. He steals an opponent's creature based on your Army's power.
*   **March from the Black Gate:** A persistent enchantment that Amasses every time you attack with your Army.
*   **Mordor Muster:** A cheap way to Amass and draw a card early in the game.
*   **Shadow of the Enemy:** A thematic reanimation spell that lets you cast creatures from your opponents' graveyards that you've milled or destroyed.

### 💍 The Ring & Protection
*   **Palantír of Orthanc:** A value engine that forces opponents to choose between letting you draw or taking damage/milling.
*   **Call of the Ring:** The most consistent source of Ring Temptation in the deck. Triggers Sauron every turn.
*   **In the Darkness Bind Them:** A Saga that Amasses, tempts you, and eventually steals a creature from every opponent.
*   **Birthday Escape:** A simple 1-mana cantrip that triggers Sauron's draw ability.
*   **Ringsight:** A powerful tutor that also triggers a Ring temptation.
*   **Ring of Evos Isle:** Budget protection that grants Hexproof to Sauron.
*   **Lightning Greaves:** Essential for protecting Sauron and letting him or the Orc Army attack immediately.
*   **Inherited Envelope:** Mana fixing that provides a one-time Ring temptation.
*   **Claim the Precious:** Efficient creature removal that also tempts you.
*   **Bombadil's Song:** Instant-speed protection that grants Hexproof and tempts you.
*   **Soothing of Smeagol:** A bounce spell to handle problematic permanents while triggering your engine.

### 💎 Ramp & Mana Fixing
*   **Relic of Sauron:** High-flavor rock that Amasses and filters your hand.
*   **Sol Ring / Arcane Signet / Talismans / Signets:** The efficient core of your mana acceleration.
*   **Commander's Sphere / Mind Stone:** Rocks that can be sacrificed later for card draw.

### 📚 Card Advantage & Selection
*   **Windfall:** Especially powerful after you've emptied your hand via Sauron's discard trigger.
*   **Fact or Fiction / Night's Whisper / Frantic Search:** Standard efficient draw and selection.
*   **Faithless Looting / Consider / Opt / Preordain:** Cheap cantrips to smooth out your early turns and trigger Saruman.
*   **Treasure Cruise / Deep Analysis / Expressive Iteration:** High-value draw for the mid-to-late game.

### ⚔️ Disruption & Board Control
*   **Terminate / Bedevil / Go for the Throat / Bitter Triumph:** Efficient Grixis removal for creatures and artifacts.
*   **Counterspell / Arcane Denial / Negate / Drown in the Loch:** A suite of protection to stop game-ending spells.
*   **Chaos Warp / Abrade / Pongify:** Flexible removal for various threat types.
*   **Blasphemous Act / Toxic Deluge:** Premier board wipes to reset the game.
*   **One Ring to Rule Them All:** A thematic board wipe that eventually drains your opponents.
*   **Languish / Summons of Saruman:** Mid-range wipes that favor your high-toughness Army and Sauron.
*   **Extract from Darkness:** A mill/reanimate hybrid that can steal a threat from any graveyard.

## 📈 Upgrade Path (Top 10 Recommendations)

1.  **Mithril Coat** (Replace: *Ring of Evos Isle*) - Instant-speed indestructible protection is a massive upgrade over Hexproof.
2.  **Raise the Palisade** (Replace: *Languish*) - A perfect asymmetrical board wipe for an Orc-heavy deck.
3.  **Phyrexian Arena** (Replace: *Deep Analysis*) - Provides the consistent, turn-by-turn draw that Bracket 2 decks thrive on.
4.  **Herald's Horn** (Replace: *Commander's Sphere*) - Reduces the cost of your Orcs/Wraiths and provides pseudo-draw.
5.  **Witch-king, Bringer of Ruin** (Replace: *Shadow of the Enemy*) - A more reliable, repeatable removal engine and a massive flying threat.
6.  **Swan Song** (Replace: *Negate*) - The most efficient counterspell for protecting your commander.
7.  **Baleful Strix** (Replace: *Dunland Crebain*) - An elite defensive dork that draws a card on entry.
8.  **Oubliette** (Replace: *Go for the Throat*) - The best way to handle an opponent's commander by removing it without letting it return to the command zone.
9.  **Kindred Discovery** (Replace: *Fact or Fiction*) - If named "Orc" or "Wraith," this provides an overwhelming amount of card draw.
10. **Cyclonic Rift** (Replace: *One Ring to Rule Them All*) - The ultimate blue board reset. (Note: Only add if your pod moves toward high Bracket 3).
