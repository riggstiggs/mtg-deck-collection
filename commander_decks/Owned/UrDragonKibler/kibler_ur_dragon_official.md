# Deck Guide: The Ur-Dragon (Official Brian Kibler List)

## Commander Strategy
**The Ur-Dragon**
*   **Archetype:** Dragon Tribal / Midrange / Ramp
*   **Bracket:** 2-3 (Optimized)
*   **Official List:** [Moxfield Deck Link](https://moxfield.com/decks/LgXkxNpBH0qzcd5MvGUO0w)
*   **Core Goal:** Utilize Eminence to ramp into powerful Dragons and massive value engines. This is Brian Kibler's personal list, focused on high-quality Dragons, robust mana fixing, and explosive finishes.

---

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🐉 Creatures (31 Cards)
*   **Ancient Copper Dragon:** Massive treasure generation for explosive turns.
*   **Birds of Paradise:** Essential 1-drop mana fixing.
*   **Bloom Tender:** High-yield mana dork in a 5-color deck.
*   **Delighted Halfling:** Mana and uncounterable protection for legendary spells.
*   **Dragonlord Dromoka:** Shuts down interaction on your turn.
*   **Dragonlord Kolaghan:** Grants Haste to the entire team.
*   **Dragonlord Silumgar:** Steals a key creature or planeswalker.
*   **Galazeth Prismari:** Turns artifacts into mana.
*   **Goldlust Triad:** Dragon tribal synergy.
*   **Goldspan Dragon:** Accelerates mana through treasures.
*   **Hellkite Courser:** Cheats The Ur-Dragon into play for immediate value.
*   **Klauth, Unrivaled Ancient:** Generates massive mana during combat.
*   **Lathliss, Dragon Queen:** Floods the board with 5/5 tokens.
*   **Magda, Brazen Outlaw:** Treasure synergy and Dragon tutoring.
*   **Miirym, Sentinel Wyrm:** Doubles every Dragon that enters.
*   **Morophon, the Boundless:** Reduces Dragon costs significantly.
*   **Old Gnawbone:** Generates treasures on combat damage.
*   **Oracle of Mul Daya:** Land acceleration and top-deck information.
*   **Protector of the Wastes:** Defensive utility.
*   **Roaming Throne:** Doubles all Dragon triggered abilities.
*   **Sarkhan, Soul Aflame:** Cost reduction and transformation utility.
*   **Scavenger Regent:** Growth and graveyard hate.
*   **Scion of Draco:** Buffs the board based on basic land types.
*   **Shadrix Silverquill:** Versatile political tool and token generator.
*   **Silumgar, the Drifting Death:** Board wipe protection and token control.
*   **Terror of the Peaks:** Direct damage engine.
*   **Tiamat:** The ultimate Dragon tutor.
*   **Ureni of the Unwritten:** Tribal utility.
*   **Vaevictis Asmadi, the Dire:** Permanent removal and chaotic value.
*   **Zurgo and Ojutai:** Haste, protection, and card selection.

### 🧙 Planeswalkers (1 Card)
*   **Sarkhan Unbroken:** Ramp, card draw, and an ultimate that wins the game.

### 🪄 Sorceries (7 Cards)
*   **Crux of Fate:** One-sided board wipe for Dragons.
*   **Escape to the Wilds:** Explosive card advantage and extra land drops.
*   **Explore:** Cantrip ramp.
*   **Farseek:** Essential 5-color land fixing.
*   **Nature's Lore:** Fetches untapped duals/forests.
*   **Skyshroud Claim:** Fetches two Forests (duals) untapped.
*   **Three Visits:** Fetches untapped duals/forests.

### ⚡ Instants (8 Cards)
*   **Brainstorm:** Top-deck manipulation.
*   **Cyclonic Rift:** The premier blue board bounce.
*   **Heroic Intervention:** Protection against board wipes.
*   **Return of the Wildspeaker:** Massive card draw or game-ending buff.
*   **Smuggler's Surprise:** Versatile protection and creature cheating.
*   **Stinging Study:** Card draw proportional to the commander's high CMC.
*   **Stubborn Denial:** Cheap counterspell with Ferocious synergy.
*   **Swan Song:** Reliable low-cost counterspell.

### 💎 Artifacts (9 Cards)
*   **Arcane Signet:** Staple 5-color fixing.
*   **Coalition Relic:** High-quality mana storage and fixing.
*   **Dragon's Hoard:** Ramp and card draw on Dragon ETB.
*   **Fellwar Stone:** Efficient 2-mana rock.
*   **Gruul Signet:** Guild-based fixing.
*   **Selesnya Signet:** Guild-based fixing.
*   **Simic Signet:** Guild-based fixing.
*   **Sol Ring:** The format staple.
*   **The Great Henge:** Massive mana, life gain, and card draw engine.

### 🌈 Enchantments (8 Cards)
*   **Dragon Tempest:** Haste and direct damage engine.
*   **Garruk's Uprising:** Card draw and Trample for big creatures.
*   **Mirari's Wake:** Mana doubling and anthem effect.
*   **Rhythm of the Wild:** Haste and protection from counterspells.
*   **Smothering Tithe:** The gold standard for mana taxation.
*   **Sylvan Library:** Premier card selection engine.
*   **Temur Ascendancy:** Haste and card draw on creature ETB.
*   **Up the Beanstalk:** Recurrent card draw for expensive spells.

### ⛰️ Lands (36 Cards)
*   **10 Dual Lands:** (Badlands, Bayou, Plateau, Savannah, Scrubland, Taiga, Tropical Island, Tundra, Underground Sea, Volcanic Island)
*   **10 Fetch Lands:** (Arid Mesa, Bloodstained Mire, Flooded Strand, Marsh Flats, Misty Rainforest, Polluted Delta, Scalding Tarn, Verdant Catacombs, Windswept Heath, Wooded Foothills)
*   **10 Shock/Check/Utility:** (Breeding Pool, Overgrown Tomb, Stomping Ground, Temple Garden, Ketria Triome, Hinterland Harbor, Rootbound Crag, Sulfur Falls, Sunpetal Grove, Cavern of Souls)
*   **6 Special Utility:** (Haven of the Spirit Dragon, Maelstrom of the Spirit Dragon, Reflecting Pool, Command Tower, Forest, Mountain)
