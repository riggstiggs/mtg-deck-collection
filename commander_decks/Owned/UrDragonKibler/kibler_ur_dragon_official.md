# Deck Guide: The Ur-Dragon (Official Brian Kibler List)

## Commander Strategy
**The Ur-Dragon**
*   **Archetype:** Dragon Tribal / Midrange / Ramp
*   **Bracket:** 2-3 (Optimized)
*   **Official List:** [Moxfield Deck Link](https://moxfield.com/decks/LgXkxNpBH0qzcd5MvGUO0w)
*   **Core Goal:** Utilize Eminence to ramp into powerful Dragons and massive value engines. This is Brian Kibler's personal list, focused on high-quality Dragons, robust mana fixing, and explosive finishes.

---
