# Deck Guide: Bruce Banner, the Incredible Hulk (Gamma Smash)

![Bruce Banner](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/e/0/e0dbbdcf-84e1-494f-8b8c-0a094f603fa9.jpg)

## Commander Strategy
**Bruce Banner // The Incredible Hulk** ({U} // {2}{R}{R}{G}{G})
*   **Archetype:** Temur (U/R/G) +1/+1 Counters Midrange / Gamma Tribal
*   **Bracket:** 3
*   **Core Goal:** Deploy Banner turn 1 as a card-advantage engine, build a wide board of counter-hungry Gamma Heroes, and multiply +1/+1 counters until the team swings for lethal trample damage. The flipped Hulk is a resilient 8/8 finisher whose Enrage snowballs counters and — with a self-pinger — chains extra combats.

### The Two Faces
Banner front is {U}, a 1/1 that taps for `{X}{X}: Draw X cards` (a repeatable, mana-hungry draw engine) and flips for `{2}{R}{R}{G}{G}` at sorcery speed. The Incredible Hulk back is an 8/8 with reach and trample and **Enrage — whenever he's dealt damage, put a +1/+1 counter on him; if he's attacking, untap him and take an additional combat phase.** Early game you use Banner to draw and develop; mid-game you flip into the Hulk as a beater that grows every time he takes damage.

### The Counter Engine
The deck is a +1/+1 counters shell first. Four counter-boosters — **Hulk, Strongest There Is** (doubles counters on each Gamma creature every upkeep), **Doc Samson** (+1 to every counter placement), **Hardened Scales**, and **Branching Evolution** — stack multiplicatively on a board of Gamma Heroes that natively generate counters (Hulk Brutal Brawler, Abomination Irradiated Brute, She-Hulk Wallbreaker, Verdurous Gearhulk, the Sagas). **Kalonian Hydra** doubles counters on the *whole team* on attack, and **Herald of Secret Streams** makes every counter-creature unblockable to close games.

### The Enrage / Extra-Combat Line (soft wincon)
**Caltrops** deals 1 damage to each attacking creature — including your own Hulk. When the flipped Hulk attacks, Caltrops pings him → Enrage adds a counter and grants an additional combat → he attacks again → Caltrops pings again → another combat. That is a near-unbounded extra-combat loop, but it is gated behind flipping the commander (1 + 6 mana) plus a 3-mana artifact, so it is a fair, late-game finish rather than a fast combo. **Red Hulk** and **Hulk's Thunderclap** are secondary self-damage enablers that also feed Enrage. This line is a *bonus* axis — the deck wins primarily through counters + trample.

### The Fling Package (alternate wincon)
The most on-theme finish: pump the Hulk (or a Kalonian-doubled Hero) and hurl it. **Fling** and **Soul's Fire** deal damage equal to the creature's power to any target — a doubled Hulk throws for 15–30 to a face. **Chandra's Ignition** turns that same creature into a board wipe *and* burns every opponent for its power at once, often lethal to the whole table. This is reach that closes games the counters plan has already set up, and it doubles as removal in a pinch.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 👑 Commander (1 Card)
*   **Bruce Banner // The Incredible Hulk:** Turn-1 {U} draw engine that flips into an 8/8 reach/trample Enrage finisher. The centerpiece of both the counters plan and the extra-combat line.

### 💚 Gamma & Hero Creatures — Counter Generators & Payoffs (18 Cards)
*   **Hulk, Strongest There Is:** Upkeep — double the +1/+1 counters on each Gamma creature you control. The marquee tribal doubler; every Gamma on board balloons each turn.
*   **Doc Samson, Super Psychiatrist:** Adds +1 to every counter you place (a permanent Hardened Scales) *and* taps for mana equal to his power — doubler plus ramp on one body.
*   **Hulk, Brutal Brawler:** Attacks each combat; whenever he attacks, put a +1/+1 counter on each *other* creature you control — a repeatable team-wide counter engine.
*   **Hulk, Gamma Goliath:** Reach, trample; reduces other creatures' Power-up costs by {3}; Power-up for five counters on himself — enables the set's Power-up abilities cheaply.
*   **Abomination, Irradiated Brute:** Whenever a Gamma or Villain you control deals combat damage to a player, put that many counters on Abomination — turns connects into a growing threat.
*   **Gamma Grotesque:** Vigilance; Power-up puts three counters on it, then draws a card for each counter-creature you control — a burst of counters and card draw.
*   **Red Hulk:** Enrage — dealt damage → counter, then deals damage equal to his counters to any target. A self-pinging removal/reach engine that synergizes with Caltrops.
*   **Professor Hulk:** Trample; whenever he deals combat damage to a player, draw that many cards — a 6/6 that refills your hand as counters make him bigger.
*   **She-Hulk, Wallbreaker:** Other Heroes gain trample; whenever a Hero is blocked, put a counter on it per blocker — punishes blocks and spreads trample across the Hero board.
*   **She-Hulk, Jennifer Walters:** Trample; {2}{R}, sac a land: draw a card and put a counter on her — repeatable draw + growth.
*   **She-Hulk, Jade Defender:** Reach, trample; Power-up destroys an artifact or enchantment and adds a counter — flexible removal on a body.
*   **Brawn, Amadeus Cho:** ETB draw a card; Power-up adds a counter for each card in hand — pairs with the deck's heavy draw for an explosive single-target pump.
*   **Abomination, Terrifying Titan:** Trample; Power-up adds a counter and fights an opponent's creature — repeatable-feeling fight removal that also grows.
*   **Hulkling, Burgeoning Bruiser:** Vigilance; whenever a bigger creature enters, put a counter on Hulkling — snowballs alongside the deck's fat Gamma drops.
*   **Abomination, World Ravager:** 10/10 menace, trample with Mayhem recursion — the top-end beater and a resilient threat that comes back from the graveyard.
*   **Verdurous Gearhulk:** Trample; ETB distribute four +1/+1 counters — instant board growth, doubled/boosted by the counter engine.
*   **Pyrewood Gearhulk:** Exact-Temur 7/7 ({2}{R}{R}{G}{G}); ETB the team gets +2/+2, vigilance, menace, and damage can't be prevented — an alpha-strike enabler on the Hulk's own colors.
*   **The Thing, Ben Grimm:** 7/7 trample Hero; whenever any Heroes you control deal damage to a player, put two +1/+1 counters on him — triggers off nearly every combat (most of the board is Heroes) and the counters are multiplied by the doublers. A thematic Fantastic Four bruiser and a self-snowballing threat.

### 🟢 Hulk-Named Synergy Creatures (2 Cards)
*   **Bloom Hulk:** ETB proliferate — adds a counter to everything already growing across the board.
*   **Hulking Raptor:** Ward {2}; adds {G}{G} each first main phase — a durable ramp body that also blocks well while you set up.

### ⚡ Counters Engine & Payoffs (6 Cards)
*   **Kalonian Hydra:** Enters with four counters; on attack, double the counters on *every* creature you control — a game-ending team pump alongside the Hulks.
*   **Herald of Secret Streams:** Creatures you control with +1/+1 counters can't be blocked — the primary way to push through a stalled board.
*   **Evolution Sage:** Landfall — proliferate whenever a land enters; with fetches and land ramp this fires repeatedly.
*   **Rishkar, Peema Renegade:** ETB two counters; every counter-creature taps for {G} — counters that double as a mana engine.
*   **Hardened Scales:** +1 to every +1/+1 counter placement — cheap, low-flash multiplier.
*   **Branching Evolution:** Doubles every +1/+1 counter you place — the strongest fair multiplier in the deck.

### 🧬 Counter Recursion (1 Card)
*   **The Ozolith:** Collects counters from dying creatures and reassigns them at combat — insurance against removal that keeps your investment on the board.

### 💥 The Enrage Package (2 Cards)
*   **Caltrops:** Deals 1 damage to each attacking creature — pings your own attacking Hulk to trigger Enrage (counter + extra combat), the deck's soft combat wincon.
*   **Hulk's Thunderclap:** Behold a Gamma creature; a creature you control deals damage equal to its power to another target — flexible removal that can also ping your own Hulk to trigger Enrage.

### 🤢 The Fling Package — Reach Finishers (3 Cards)
*   **Fling:** {1}{R} instant; sacrifice a creature, deal damage equal to its power to any target — a doubled Hulk becomes a 15–30 point burst kill at instant speed.
*   **Soul's Fire:** {2}{R} instant; a creature you control deals damage equal to its power to any target — the same reach as Fling but *without* sacrificing, so the Hulk stays on board.
*   **Chandra's Ignition:** {3}{R}{R}; your creature deals damage equal to its power to each other creature AND each opponent — a board wipe plus multiplayer burn that is frequently lethal to the whole table off a big Hulk.

### ⚡ Ramp (11 Cards)
*   **Sol Ring:** Universal two-mana accelerant.
*   **Arcane Signet:** {1}, {T}: Add one mana in your commander's identity — efficient fixing for demanding pips.
*   **Birds of Paradise:** {T}: Add one mana of any color — turn-1 fixing for the RR/GG/UU requirements.
*   **Incubation Druid:** Taps for one mana (three with a counter) — a mana dork that scales with the counter theme.
*   **Gyre Sage:** Evolve grows it; taps for {G} per counter — explosive ramp on a counters board.
*   **Bloom Tender:** Taps for one mana per color among your permanents — often two-to-three mana in this multicolor shell.
*   **Farseek:** Fetches a Steam Vents / Breeding Pool / Stomping Ground (or any dual with a basic type) — targeted fixing.
*   **Three Visits:** Fetches a Forest onto the battlefield — untapped green ramp.
*   **Cultivate:** Fetch two basics, one to the battlefield — ramp and fixing that smooths every color.
*   **Kodama's Reach:** Same as Cultivate — redundancy for the green base that powers the {2}{R}{R}{G}{G} flip.
*   **Restorative Technique:** Fetch a basic land (tapped), gain 2 life, and put a +1/+1 counter on a creature — ramp that also advances the counter plan; the counter is multiplied by the doublers and triggers Evolution Sage on the land drop.

### 📖 Card Advantage (6 Cards)
*   **The Great Henge:** Cost-reduced by your biggest creature; taps for {G}{G} + life and draws a card whenever a nontoken creature enters — the premier engine in a fat-creature counters deck.
*   **Rishkar's Expertise:** Draw cards equal to your greatest power, then cast a spell ≤5 for free — a massive refuel off any big Hulk.
*   **Guardian Project:** Draw whenever a nontoken creature with a unique name enters — the deck is nearly all singleton legends, so this fires constantly.
*   **Garruk's Uprising:** Team gains trample; draw whenever a 4-power creature enters — trample enabler plus steady draw.
*   **Beast Whisperer:** Draw whenever you cast a creature spell — flat card advantage across the creature suite.
*   **Toski, Bearer of Secrets:** Indestructible, can't be countered, attacks each combat; draw whenever any creature deals combat damage to a player — a resilient, wide-board draw engine.

### 🎯 Targeted Disruption (7 Cards)
*   **Beast Within:** Destroy any permanent — the most flexible answer in Temur.
*   **Chaos Warp:** Shuffle any permanent away — hits things green and red normally can't.
*   **Pongify:** Destroy target creature for {U} — cheap instant removal.
*   **Epic Fight:** {2}{G} sorcery — choose one or both: double a creature's power/toughness, and/or your creature fights an opponent's. Flexible fight removal that doubles as a combat trick and a *Fling multiplier* (double the Hulk's power, then throw it).
*   **Lightning Bolt:** 3 damage to any target — efficient early interaction or reach.
*   **Abrade:** 3 damage to a creature or destroy an artifact — flexible answer to rocks and equipment.
*   **Ram Through:** Your creature deals its power to an opponent's; trample carries excess to the player — removal that scales with your counters.

### 🛡️ Mass Disruption & Protection (2 Cards)
*Note: Mass removal is intentionally light (one one-sided bounce) — symmetric wraths fight this deck's board-centric plan. Compensated by seven targeted answers, a protection spell, and Chandra's Ignition as a semi-one-sided finisher.*
*   **Cyclonic Rift:** Overload returns every nonland permanent your opponents control to hand — a one-sided reset that clears blockers for a lethal swing. (Only Game Changer in the deck.)
*   **Tyvar's Stand:** {X}{G} — target creature gets +X/+X and gains hexproof and indestructible; can't be countered. Single-target protection that saves the Hulk through a wipe or removal, and doubles as a combat trick / Fling-enabler / lethal pump.

### 🏔️ Lands (37 Cards)

#### Utility Lands
*   **Command Tower:** Adds any color in the commander's identity.
*   **Plaza of Heroes:** Taps {C}; taps any color to cast a legendary spell (nearly the whole deck) and any color among your legendary permanents; can exile itself to give a legendary creature hexproof + indestructible — flexible fixing plus a built-in shield for the Hulk.
*   **Exotic Orchard:** Taps for any color an opponent's land can produce — effectively a Temur land in most pods.
*   **Rogue's Passage:** {4}, {T}: A creature is unblockable — pushes the Hulk or a giant Hero through.
*   **Karn's Bastion:** {4}, {T}: Proliferate — a repeatable, colorless counter engine on a land.
*   **Kessig Wolf Run:** {X}{R}{G}: +X/+0 and trample — a mana sink that turns excess mana into damage.

#### Dual / Fixing Lands
*   **Ketria Triome:** Taps for U/R/G; fetchable by Farseek — perfect three-color fixing.
*   **Steam Vents / Breeding Pool / Stomping Ground:** Shock lands covering all three color pairs; enter untapped for 2 life.
*   **Rootbound Crag / Hinterland Harbor / Sulfur Falls:** Check lands — untapped with a matching basic type.
*   **Karplusan Forest / Shivan Reef:** Pain lands — untapped fixing for the demanding pips.
*   **Rejuvenating Springs:** G/U dual, untapped with two-plus opponents (always in Commander) — no life loss; an upgrade over the pain-land G/U slot.
*   **Cinder Glade:** R/G dual, untapped with two-plus basics — supports the {2}{R}{R}{G}{G} flip and the red-heavy Fling package.
*   **Scorched Geyser:** U/R dual, untapped with two-plus basics — shores up blue (our lightest color) and red for the flip; replaced Cavern of Souls on value.
*   **Frontier Bivouac:** Temur tri-land (enters tapped).
*   **Spire Garden:** R/G dual, untapped with two-plus opponents (always in Commander).

#### Basic Fetch
*   **Fabled Passage:** Sacrifice to fetch any basic (tapped; untaps with 4+ lands) — flexible fixing that also triggers Evolution Sage landfall. Replaces the premium fetch lands (kept for other decks).

#### Basic Lands
*   7x Forest
*   5x Mountain
*   4x Island

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
*   **Hardened Scales effects (Kami of Whispered Hopes, Innkeeper's Talent):** More counter multipliers if the deck wants a higher ceiling — held back to keep the Bracket-3 power feel.
*   **Berserkers' Onslaught / Xenagos, God of Revels:** Double-strike or power-doubling to make the Fling / Chandra's Ignition finish even more explosive.
*   **Fabricate / Whir of Invention:** Tutors for Caltrops to make the extra-combat line more consistent (raises the power level toward Bracket 4).
*   **Kalonian Behemoth / bigger Gamma top-end:** Additional evasive finishers if the board stalls against go-wide decks.
*   **Doubling Season / Vorinclex, Monstrous Raider:** The premium doublers, deliberately left out for Bracket 3 — the obvious upgrade if the pod scales up.

    *   **In:** Scorched Geyser
    *   **Out:** Cavern of Souls
    *   **Reason:** Cavern of Souls (~$47) was the deck's softest-value card — the creatures don't need to be uncounterable, and Command Tower + Plaza of Heroes + Exotic Orchard already handle flexible/legendary fixing. Scorched Geyser (U/R, ~$0.84) adds a source in blue (the lightest color) and red (flip + Fling package), reliably untapped given 16 basics. Saves ~$46 with negligible consistency loss. Still 37 lands, 100 cards.
*   **2026-07-04:** Swapped board-wide protection for a cheaper, proactive single-target protection.
    *   **In:** Tyvar's Stand
    *   **Out:** Heroic Intervention
    *   **Reason:** Heroic Intervention (~$14) is best-in-class board-wide protection but a purely reactive insurance card the deck can survive without — The Ozolith, Toski, Abomination World Ravager recursion, Plaza of Heroes, and heavy card draw already provide wipe resilience. Tyvar's Stand covers the key moment (save the Hulk through a wipe or removal via hexproof + indestructible) at ~$0.48, and unlike Heroic it's a card you cast proactively as a +X/+X combat trick, Fling-enabler, or lethal pump. Trade-off is single-target vs board-wide, acceptable for a Bracket 3 pod.
*   **2026-07-04:** Land base overhaul — cut premium fetches, added Marvel duals + legendary fixing.
    *   **In:** Plaza of Heroes, Fabled Passage, Rejuvenating Springs, Cinder Glade; +1 basic Mountain
    *   **Out:** Misty Rainforest, Scalding Tarn, Wooded Foothills, Yavimaya Coast; −1 basic Forest
    *   **Reason:** Removed the three premium fetch lands (reserved for other decks) in favor of msc dual lands. Plaza of Heroes is exceptional in this legend-dense deck — any-color fixing for the Hulks plus a hexproof/indestructible shield for the commander. Fabled Passage is a cheap fetch that still triggers Evolution Sage landfall. Rejuvenating Springs replaces Yavimaya Coast as a G/U source with no life loss (always untapped in multiplayer). Cinder Glade shifts a slot toward R/G to support the {2}{R}{R}{G}{G} flip and the red-heavy Fling package; basics rebalanced 8/4/4 → 7/5/4 for the same reason. Still 37 lands, still 100 cards.
*   **2026-07-04:** Added the Fling reach package and on-theme ramp; retired the Simic Ascendancy axis.
    *   **In:** Restorative Technique, Fling, Chandra's Ignition, Soul's Fire
    *   **Out:** Hunt the Weak, Simic Ascendancy, Leader Super-Genius, Return of the Wildspeaker
    *   **Reason:** Replaced the Simic Ascendancy alt-win with a flavor-tight "throw the Hulk" finisher — Fling / Soul's Fire deal a doubled Hulk's power to any target, and Chandra's Ignition is a semi-one-sided board wipe plus multiplayer burn. Restorative Technique swaps in for Hunt the Weak as ramp that also places a (doubler-multiplied) counter and triggers Evolution Sage, keeping both Cultivate-effects intact. Leader Super-Genius (eased UU strain) and Return of the Wildspeaker (surplus card draw) funded the two extra package slots. Still 100 cards, still Bracket 3 (only Game Changer remains Cyclonic Rift).
