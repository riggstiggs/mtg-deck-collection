# Ms. Bumbleflower Deck Guide

## Commander Strategy
**Ms. Bumbleflower**
*   **Role:** The Political Negotiator & Card Engine.
*   **Goal:** To navigate the game through "Group Hug" politics, giving opponents resources while building a massive advantage for yourself through +1/+1 counters and explosive card draw.
*   **The Engine:**
    1.  **Spellcasting Trigger:** Whenever you cast a spell, an opponent draws a card, and you put a +1/+1 counter and Flying on a creature.
    2.  **Double Down:** If you cast a second spell in a turn, you draw two cards. This incentivizes playing cheap spells or instant-speed interaction to trigger her twice every turn cycle.
    3.  **Bant Control:** Use white/blue/green's best defensive tools to ensure that while your opponents have many cards, they cannot use them to kill you.

## 1. The Group Hug & Politics
*Cards that benefit everyone but you most of all.*
*   **Kwain, Itinerant Meddler / Selvala, Explorer Returned:** Provide card draw and life/mana to the table, making you the "friend" at the pod.
*   **Ghirapur Orrery / Rites of Flourishing:** Speed up the game by allowing everyone to play more lands and draw more cards.
*   **Secret Rendezvous / Tempt with Bunnies:** Targeted political tools to help a struggling opponent or bribe someone into a truce.
*   **Tenuous Truce:** An enchantment that ensures you and an opponent draw cards together until someone breaks the peace.

## 2. Card Draw Payoffs
*Turning the high volume of draw into a win condition.*
*   **Psychosis Crawler:** Drains all opponents whenever you draw a card. With Ms. Bumbleflower drawing you two cards per turn, this adds up fast.
*   **Chasm Skulker:** Grows larger with every card you draw and explodes into a swarm of squids when it dies.
*   **Body of Knowledge:** Its power/toughness matches your hand size, which will often be 10+ cards.
*   **Triskaidekaphile:** A secondary win condition if you have exactly 13 cards in hand (very easy in this deck).

## 3. The Counter Strategy
*Using the +1/+1 counters from Ms. Bumbleflower to dominate the board.*
*   **Simic Ascendancy:** A primary win condition. As Ms. Bumbleflower places counters, you build up "Growth Counters" and win the game.
*   **Managorger Hydra / Forgotten Ancient:** These creatures grow passively as everyone casts the cards you're helping them draw.
*   **Kalonian Hydra:** Doubles all counters on your creatures when it attacks, turning Ms. Bumbleflower's buffs into a lethal force.
*   **Steelburr Champion:** A resilient threat that grows as opponents cast spells.

## 4. Protection & Survival
*Ensuring you aren't the first target of the resources you're giving away.*
*   **Baird, Steward of Argive / Ghostly Prison (effects):** Force opponents to pay mana to attack you.
*   **Perch Protection:** A powerful phase-out effect that also creates bird tokens to block.
*   **Spore Frog:** A repeatable fog effect that discourages aggression.
*   **Illusionist's Gambit:** Redirects an entire attacking army away from you and toward another opponent.

## 5. Winning the Game
*   **Alternate Win Cons:** Use *Simic Ascendancy*, *Triskaidekaphile*, or *Twenty-Toed Toad* to win without needing to deal damage.
*   **Flying Beatdown:** Use Ms. Bumbleflower's ability to give your massive *Body of Knowledge* or *Kalonian Hydra* Flying and swing for lethal.
*   **Drain Out:** *Psychosis Crawler* can finish the table once you start casting 2-3 spells per turn.
