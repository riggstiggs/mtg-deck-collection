# Deck Guide: Ramses, Assassin Lord (Shadow Brotherhood)

![Ramses, Assassin Lord](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/9/e/9e5f168c-4f56-4d06-bef1-db10fdfbe657.jpg?1674174868)

## Commander Strategy
**Ramses, Assassin Lord** (2UB)
*   **Archetype:** Dimir (U/B) Assassin Tribal / Alt-Win Condition
*   **Bracket:** 3
*   **Core Goal:** Build a board of evasive, deathtouch-laden Assassins that discourage blocks and threaten opponents every combat. Attack a player to "tag" them with Ramses' win trigger, then close through life drain (Exsanguinate, Torment of Hailfire), combat life-halving (Unstoppable Slasher + Wound Reflection), or accumulated pressure. Ramses immediately wins if a tagged player loses the game by any means that turn.

### The Kill Trigger
Ramses' win condition reads: "Whenever a player loses the game, if they were attacked this turn by an Assassin you controlled, you win the game." The kill source is irrelevant — the player just needs to have been attacked by one of your Assassins and then die, from any cause, before end of turn. Primary kill lines:
- **Drain Closer:** Attack a player, resolve Exsanguinate or Torment of Hailfire targeting them.
- **Slasher + Wound Reflection:** Unstoppable Slasher deals combat damage → player loses half their life → Wound Reflection doubles that loss at end of turn → player dies.
- **Commander Damage:** Ramses himself is a 4/4 deathtouch lord. Repeated unblocked hits accumulate toward 21 commander damage.

### The Deathtouch Board
Multiple Assassins natively have deathtouch, and Poison-Blade Mentor spreads deathtouch to any attacker on each swing. This turns the whole board into a political deterrent — opponents are reluctant to attack into even a single Assassin. The deck exploits this to swing freely while the table is locked down.

### The Cipher Engine
Three cipher spells (Whispering Madness, Undercity Plague, Hidden Strings) encode on evasive Assassins (Hookblade Veteran, Midnight Assassin, Shadowmage Infiltrator) and trigger on every successful combat hit. Whispering Madness wheels hands repeatedly; Undercity Plague drains life, strips cards, and eats permanents; Hidden Strings untaps mana rocks or taps blockers.

### The Freerunning Discount
Ramses dealing combat damage, or any other Assassin hitting a player, unlocks Freerunning on Achilles Davenport and Merciless Harlequin — reducing their costs to {U}{B} and {1}{B} respectively. Ezio Auditore da Firenze in the 99 extends this further, giving every Assassin spell you cast Freerunning {B}{B} after any successful hit.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 👑 Commander (1 Card)
*   **Ramses, Assassin Lord:** 4/4 deathtouch lord — other Assassins get +1/+1. Wins the game immediately if a player you attacked with an Assassin dies that turn from any source. Deck is built around enabling and accelerating this trigger.

### ⚡ Ramp (8 Cards)
*   **Sol Ring:** Universal two-mana accelerant.
*   **Arcane Signet:** Produces U or B — the most efficient color-fixing rock.
*   **Dimir Signet:** {1}, {T}: Add {U}{B} — pairs perfectly with the color identity.
*   **Talisman of Dominance:** Produces colorless or U/B at 1 life — efficient early mana.
*   **Thought Vessel:** Colorless ramp plus no maximum hand size — important on high-draw turns with Whispering Madness or Vanquisher's Banner.
*   **Mind Stone:** Colorless ramp early; sacrifices into a card draw in the late game.
*   **Commander's Sphere:** Produces any color in commander's identity; sacrifices for a card — flexible and convertible.
*   **Dark Ritual:** Add {B}{B}{B} — critical burst mana for Exsanguinate or Torment finishes; also enables turn-1 or turn-2 acceleration.

### 📖 Card Advantage (4 Cards)
*   **Phyrexian Arena:** Draw a card per upkeep at the cost of 1 life — primary repeatable draw engine.
*   **Sign in Blood:** Draw two, lose 2 life — burst draw; can target an opponent at 2 life to close the game.
*   **Night's Whisper:** Draw two, lose 2 life — easier to cast than Sign in Blood in the early turns.
*   **Read the Bones:** Scry 2 then draw two, lose 2 life — the scry makes this the strongest of the three draw spells.

### 🗡️ Legendary Assassins (5 Cards)
*   **Ezio, Blade of Vengeance:** 5/5 deathtouch — draws a card whenever any Assassin you control deals combat damage to a player. Primary value engine; turns the whole Assassin board into a card draw machine.
*   **Etrata, the Silencer:** Can't be blocked; on combat damage exiles a creature and places a hit counter; target player loses the game at 3 hit counters. Secondary alternate win condition alongside Ramses.
*   **Achilles Davenport:** Backup lord — other Assassins get +1/+1; Menace; Freerunning {U}{B} reduces him to 2 mana after any Assassin connects.
*   **Desmond Miles:** Menace; grows +1/+0 for each other Assassin you control and each Assassin in your graveyard — scales hard with the tribal engine; surveil X on combat damage synergizes directly with Darkblade Agent's deathtouch + draw trigger.
*   **Basim Ibn Ishaq:** Draws a card whenever you cast a historic spell (artifacts, legendaries, sagas); becomes unblockable the same turn. Grows with +1/+1 counters from combat hits.

### 🔪 Removal Assassins (5 Cards)
*   **Royal Assassin:** {T}: Destroy target tapped creature — classic thematic removal, pairs with King's Assassin to orchestrate targeted kills.
*   **King's Assassin:** Destroy target tapped creature, activatable before attackers are declared — taps a threat before it can swing, then Royal Assassin cleans it up on your turn.
*   **Callidus Assassin:** Flash; enters as a copy of any creature on the battlefield and immediately destroys one creature that player controls — flexible removal that also adds an Assassin body.
*   **Scarblade Elite:** {T}, Exile an Assassin from your graveyard: Destroy target creature — repeatable removal that converts graveyard Assassins into action. Grows the graveyard fuel pool over time.
*   **Dark Impostor:** {4}{B}{B}: Exile target creature and put a +1/+1 counter on this — gains all activated abilities of every exiled creature; snowballs into a massive, multi-ability threat.

### 💀 Value Assassins (6 Cards)
*   **Unstoppable Slasher:** Deathtouch; whenever it deals combat damage to a player, that player loses half their life rounded up; self-recurring on death (returns as a tapped 2/3 with stun counters). Core kill enabler alongside Wound Reflection.
*   **Thorn of the Black Rose:** Deathtouch; ETB makes you Monarch — consistent end-step card draw as long as you keep the crown.
*   **Termination Facilitator:** Puts a Bounty counter on a creature or planeswalker; draws a card when that bounty permanent dies — removal incentive plus card draw on one body.
*   **Poison-Blade Mentor:** Deathtouch; whenever this attacks, another target Assassin you control gains deathtouch until end of turn — spreads the deterrent across the entire attacking force.
*   **Guildsworn Prowler:** Deathtouch; when it dies while not blocking, draw a card — cheap chip threat that replaces itself on death.
*   **Merciless Harlequin:** Freerunning {1}{B}; ETB draw a card and lose 1 life — becomes a {1}{B} card-drawing 2/1 after any combat hit.

### 🦅 Evasion / Cipher Targets (6 Cards)
*   **Midnight Assassin:** Flying + deathtouch — reliable evasive attacker and primary cipher host in the early game.
*   **Darkblade Agent:** Gains deathtouch and "whenever deals combat damage draw a card" as long as you've surveilled this turn — pairs with Read the Bones and Basim.
*   **Brotherhood Spy:** Unblockable at the start of your combat whenever you control a legendary Assassin — since Ramses is always available from the command zone, this is effectively always-on unblockable; more reliable cipher host than Hookblade Veteran.
*   **Aven Heartstabber:** Flying; becomes a 3/3 flying deathtouch creature when 5+ mana values are in your graveyard — escalates into a serious threat by mid-game.
*   **Vein Ripper:** Flying; Ward — sacrifice a creature; whenever any creature dies, target opponent loses 2 life and you gain 2 life — powerful sustained drain engine in a board-wipe-heavy deck.
*   **Shadowmage Infiltrator:** Fear (can't be blocked except by artifacts or black creatures); whenever it deals combat damage to a player, you may draw a card — reliable evasive draw engine.

### 🎯 Plan Cards (15 Cards)

#### Tribal Engine
*   **Door of Destinies:** Whenever you cast an Assassin spell, put a charge counter on this; Assassins you control get +1/+1 per counter — one-sided lord that snowballs with every Assassin cast.
*   **Vanquisher's Banner:** Assassins you control get +1/+1; whenever you cast an Assassin spell, draw a card — combines lord and draw in one artifact.
*   **Herald's Horn:** Assassin spells cost {1} less; at the beginning of your upkeep, reveal the top card and draw it if it's an Assassin — cost reduction plus potential card advantage.
*   **Crypt Ghast:** Whenever you tap a Swamp for mana, add an additional {B}; Extort — with Urborg in play, this doubles all mana production. Extort drains each opponent a little every spell you cast.

#### Evasion
*   **Cover of Darkness:** Choose Assassin — all Assassins have Fear; can't be blocked except by artifacts and black creatures — massive evasion upgrade for the entire team.
*   **Whispersilk Cloak:** Equipped creature can't be blocked and has Shroud — guarantees cipher spells trigger each combat; also protects Ramses from targeting.
*   **Trailblazer's Boots:** Equipped creature has nonbasic landwalk — unblockable against virtually every Commander player since nearly all decks run nonbasic lands.

#### Commander Protection
*   **Lightning Greaves:** Haste + Shroud, Equip {0} — attach immediately when Ramses enters to protect him before opponents can respond.
*   **Swiftfoot Boots:** Haste + Hexproof, Equip {1} — Hexproof allows Ramses to still be targeted by your own spells (e.g., equip other equipment) unlike Shroud.

#### Kill Enablers
*   **Wound Reflection:** At each end step, each opponent loses life equal to the life they lost this turn — with Unstoppable Slasher, a player loses half their life from combat damage, then that same amount again from Wound Reflection; instant death.
*   **Exsanguinate:** Each opponent loses X life, you gain that much — primary kill finisher; cast after attacking a player with any Assassin to trigger Ramses' win condition.
*   **Torment of Hailfire:** Repeat X times: each opponent loses 3 life or sacrifices a nonland permanent or discards a card — alternate finisher that simultaneously strips resources.

#### Cipher Package
*   **Whispering Madness:** Each player discards their hand and draws equal to the most discarded; Cipher — encodes on an evasive Assassin; every hit thereafter wheels the table and refills your hand.
*   **Undercity Plague:** Target player loses 1 life, discards a card, sacrifices a permanent; Cipher — relentless pressure on a flying Assassin; each hit dismantles an opponent's hand and board.
*   **Hidden Strings:** Tap or untap up to two target permanents twice; Cipher — untap mana rocks for bonus mana each combat or tap blockers before attacking.

### 🔍 Targeted Disruption (7 Cards)
*   **Feed the Swarm:** Destroy target creature or enchantment — black's answer to enchantments; essential in a format with many problematic enchantments.
*   **Go for the Throat:** Destroy target nonartifact creature — efficient instant removal.
*   **Snuff Out:** Destroy target nonblack creature; pay 4 life if you control a Swamp — effectively free in this deck with Urborg in the mana base.
*   **Hero's Downfall:** Destroy target creature or planeswalker — instant-speed answer to both threats.
*   **Counterspell:** Counter target spell — hard counter for anything dangerous.
*   **Arcane Denial:** Counter target spell; opponent draws 2 — soft counter with a political softener; excellent in multiplayer.
*   **Negate:** Counter target noncreature spell — stops board wipes, Cyclonic Rifts, and game-ending spells.

### 💥 Mass Disruption (5 Cards)
*Note: One below the template's 6-count, compensated by five creature-based removal pieces (Royal Assassin, King's Assassin, Callidus Assassin, Scarblade Elite, Dark Impostor).*
*   **Damnation:** Destroy all creatures — symmetric wrath; best played after attacking to trigger Ramses or before your rebuild.
*   **Toxic Deluge:** Pay X life; all creatures get -X/-X — scales precisely to the threat level; the most flexible wrath in the deck.
*   **Extinction Event:** Choose odd or even; exile all creatures with that mana value — exile-based, avoids indestructible, often asymmetric.
*   **Black Sun's Zenith:** Put X -1/-1 counters on each creature; reshuffles — scalable and recurring; can be surgically precise.
*   **Kindred Dominance:** Choose a creature type; destroy all creatures not of the chosen type — choose Assassin for a completely one-sided board wipe that leaves your entire team intact.

### 🏔️ Lands (38 Cards)

#### Utility Lands
*   **Command Tower:** Produces U or B — essential dual.
*   **Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth:** All lands become Swamps — enables Cabal Coffers and Crypt Ghast to leverage the full mana base; lets Snuff Out activate for free.
*   **Cabal Coffers:** Taps for B per Swamp you control — with Urborg, generates massive black mana for Exsanguinate and Torment finishes.
*   **Rogue's Passage:** {4}, {T}: Target creature is unblockable this turn — guarantees Ramses or a cipher host connects when needed.
*   **Otawara, Soaring City:** Channel — return target permanent to owner's hand at instant speed — counterspell-proof interaction that bypasses indestructible.
*   **Takenuma, Abandoned Mire:** Channel — mill 3 and return a legendary creature or planeswalker to hand — recurs Ramses or a fallen Legendary Assassin.
*   **Exotic Orchard:** Produces any color a land an opponent controls can produce — essentially a free dual in multiplayer Commander.

#### Dual Lands
*   **Watery Grave:** Shock land — enters untapped at 2 life; produces U or B.
*   **Morphic Pool:** Enters untapped with two or more opponents (always in Commander) — free dual.
*   **Darkslick Shores:** Enters untapped if you control two or fewer other lands — untapped on turns 1 and 2.
*   **Tainted Isle:** Enters untapped; produces {U} or {B} if you control a Swamp — with Urborg in play this is always an untapped dual; reliable even without it given 9 basic Swamps.
*   **Sunken Hollow:** Enters untapped if you control two or more basic lands — with 17 basics, enters untapped on virtually every draw.
*   **Choked Estuary:** Enters untapped if you reveal an Island or Swamp from hand — near-guaranteed untapped with the high basic count.
*   **Underground River:** Pain land — produces U or B at 1 damage; colorless alternative.
*   **Sunken Ruins:** Filter land — {U/B}, {T}: Add {U}{U}, {U}{B}, or {B}{B} — excellent color fixing and filtering.
*   **Drowned Catacomb:** Check land — enters untapped if you control an Island or Swamp (almost always).
*   **Darkwater Catacombs:** {1}, {T}: Add {U}{B} — reliable but slightly slow dual.
*   **Clearwater Pathway:** Enters as either U or B side — flexible pathway land.
*   **River of Tears:** Produces {U} normally; produces {B} instead if you played a land this turn — nuanced but reliable.

#### Fetch Lands
*   **Polluted Delta:** Fetches Island or Swamp — the only fetch that can find either basic type; premier Dimir fetch.
*   **Misty Rainforest:** Fetches Island — secondary fetch for deck thinning.

#### Basic Lands
*   8x Island
*   9x Swamp

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
*   **Jet Medallion (2):** Black spells cost {1} less — pervasive discount across the entire creature suite; strong upgrade path if the deck needs more speed.
*   **Black Market (3BB):** Charge counter per creature death, adds {B} per counter at main phase — grows exponentially with board wipes and Royal/King's Assassin activations; consider over Commander's Sphere.
*   **Coat of Arms (5):** +1/+1 per other creature sharing a type — higher ceiling than Door of Destinies in the short term, but symmetric. Only worthwhile if your playgroup shifts away from tribal themes.
*   **Nirkana Revenant (4BB):** Doubles Swamp mana with Urborg; a second Crypt Ghast effect — overkill unless you need extreme late-game ramp for massive Exsanguinate finishes.
*   **Culling the Weak (B):** Sacrifice a creature, add {B}{B}{B}{B} — surprise Exsanguinate enabler in the same turn you attack; burst kill from nowhere.
*   **Mana Vault (1):** Taps for {C}{C}{C} — premium accelerant; high price tag but the best early ramp upgrade available.

## 📜 Deck Changelog
*   **2026-05-09:** Fix color identity violation; upgrade cipher host.
    *   **In:** Desmond Miles, Brotherhood Spy
    *   **Out:** Ezio Auditore da Firenze, Hookblade Veteran
    *   **Reason:** Ezio Auditore's WUBRG ability in rules text makes his color identity 5-color — illegal in Dimir. Desmond Miles fills the Menace slot, scales with the Assassin count (including graveyard), and the surveil-on-damage synergizes with Darkblade Agent. Brotherhood Spy is a strict upgrade over Hookblade Veteran as a cipher host — effectively always unblockable in this deck since Ramses in the command zone satisfies the legendary Assassin condition.
*   **2026-05-09:** Land base overhaul — cut 3 off-color fetches, added 3 untapped Dimir duals, flipped basic Island/Swamp split to favor black.
    *   **In:** Tainted Isle, Sunken Hollow, Choked Estuary; 1 additional basic Swamp
    *   **Out:** Bloodstained Mire, Flooded Strand, Scalding Tarn; 1 basic Island
    *   **Reason:** 5 fetches is excessive for a 2-color deck with a single shock land. Bloodstained Mire/Flooded Strand/Scalding Tarn can each only find one basic type, providing no advantage over the duals they replaced. Tainted Isle/Sunken Hollow/Choked Estuary all enter untapped reliably given the high basic count. Basic split flipped to 9 Swamp / 8 Island to better support the heavy black pip requirements (Kindred Dominance, Exsanguinate, Crypt Ghast).
*   **2026-05-09:** Initial deck creation — Ramses Assassin Lord, Shadow Brotherhood (Dimir Assassin Tribal, Bracket 3).
    *   **In:** Full 99-card list.
    *   **Out:** N/A
    *   **Reason:** New deck build. Core strategy: attack to tag a player with Ramses' win trigger, close via Exsanguinate/Torment of Hailfire drain or Unstoppable Slasher + Wound Reflection life-halving. Cipher engine on evasive Assassins for sustained card advantage.
