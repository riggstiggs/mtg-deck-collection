# Deck Guide: Henzie "Toolbox" Torre — "Budget Blitz"

![Henzie "Toolbox" Torre](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/e/e/ee228dcc-3170-4c24-80bc-28bcee07cb43.jpg?1673481644)

## Commander Strategy
**Henzie "Toolbox" Torre**
*   **Archetype:** Jund (Black/Red/Green) Blitz / Sacrifice / Reanimation
*   **Bracket:** 2
*   **Core Goal:** Cast Henzie as early as turn 2–3, then Blitz massive creatures every turn at a steep discount. Blitz grants haste and draws a card on death, turning every high-CMC bomb into a high-impact one-shot draw spell. Each time Henzie is recast from the command zone his cost reduction grows — by the third cast, 7–8 mana threats enter for as little as 1–2 mana. Chain ETBs, death triggers, and recursion spells to bury opponents in cascading value.

### 🏎️ How to Pilot the Blitz
1.  **Ramp Early (Turns 1–2):** Lead with a one-mana dork. A turn-1 Llanowar Elves into turn-2 Henzie is the ideal opening.
2.  **Blitz High-Value Targets (Turns 3–5):** Prioritize creatures with ETB removal (Plaguecrafter, Ravenous Chupacabra) to clear blockers, then follow up with big payoffs (Sepulchral Primordial, Molten Primordial) to steal games.
3.  **Chain Recursion:** Use the card drawn from each Blitz death to find Living Death, Victimize, or Dread Return — then refuel the graveyard with another Blitz cycle.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🐉 The Blitz Brigade — Forced Sacrifice (5 Cards)
*Creatures that force opponents to sacrifice permanents on entry — clears hexproof and indestructible threats, and chains with Butcher of Malakir for repeated opponent board pressure.*

*   **Plaguecrafter:** 3/2 for {2}{B}. ETB — each player sacrifices a creature or planeswalker, or discards a card. The highest-impact forced-sacrifice ETB in the deck. Blitzed for even less with Henzie, it clears the most dangerous threat on every opponent's board simultaneously.
*   **Demon's Disciple:** 1/1 for {1}{B}. ETB — each player sacrifices a creature. The cheapest forced-sacrifice effect available. At 2 mana (less with Henzie), it's a reliable early answer to indestructible and hexproof creatures that other removal can't touch.
*   **Gravelighter:** 2/2 for {2}{B}. ETB — each opponent sacrifices a creature or discards a card. When it dies, draw a card. Draws itself on death so Blitzing it is free card advantage on top of the forced-sacrifice effect — one of the most efficient Blitz targets at this cost.
*   **Ravenous Chupacabra:** 2/2 for {3}{B}. ETB — destroy target creature. Hard, unconditional removal attached to a body. Blitzed, it removes one key threat and draws a replacement card in the same turn.
*   **Noxious Gearhulk:** 5/4 menace for {4}{B}{B}. ETB — destroy target creature and gain life equal to its toughness. Menace makes it difficult to block in the rare case it survives for combat. The life gain is relevant against aggressive tables.

### 🐉 The Blitz Brigade — ETB Value Creatures (9 Cards)
*High-CMC creatures whose ETBs alone justify their Blitz cost — enter, trigger, draw a card, repeat.*

*   **Solemn Simulacrum:** 2/2 for {4}. ETB — search for a basic land and put it onto the battlefield tapped. Dies — draw a card. Every Blitz generates a land fetch and a card draw. The most consistently efficient value creature in the deck.
*   **Timeless Witness:** 3/2 for {3}{G}. ETB — return any card from your graveyard to your hand. Recovers a countered Henzie, a destroyed value engine, or your best Blitz bomb. Eternalize later for a second activation that doesn't require Henzie's tax.
*   **Thragtusk:** 5/3 for {4}{G}. ETB — gain 5 life. When it leaves the battlefield, create a 3/3 Beast token. Even when Blitzed and sacrificed at end of turn it leaves behind a 3/3 blocker and 5 life — more value than most five-drops generate in an entire game.
*   **Inferno Titan:** 6/6 for {4}{R}{R}. ETB or whenever it attacks — deal 3 damage divided among any targets. Kills a small creature and pings a player, or machine-guns three tokens on entry. A powerful removal spell stapled to a massive body.
*   **Etali, Primal Storm:** 6/6 for {4}{R}{R}{G}. Whenever Etali attacks, exile the top card of each opponent's library and cast it for free. With Henzie's reduction, Blitzing Etali turns a 7-mana creature into a few-mana all-in value play — it ETBs with haste, attacks immediately, steals up to three spells, then sacrifices itself drawing a card.
*   **Sepulchral Primordial:** 5/4 intimidate for {5}{B}{B}. ETB — for each opponent, put a creature card from their graveyard onto the battlefield under your control. Reanimates one of each opponent's best threats to your side in a single entry. One of the highest-impact Blitz targets in the deck.
*   **Molten Primordial:** 5/4 haste for {5}{R}{R}. ETB — gain control of up to one creature from each opponent until end of turn; those creatures gain haste and must attack if able. Steals opponents' biggest blockers, swings them at their owners or other opponents, and clears paths for Henzie.
*   **Woodfall Primus:** 6/6 trample for {5}{G}{G}{G}. ETB — destroy target noncreature permanent (artifact, enchantment, or land). Has Persist — returns from death with a -1/-1 counter. The Persist trigger fires on the Blitz sacrifice, destroying two permanents from a single Blitz entry.
*   **Daemogoth Woe-Eater:** A massive body for its mana cost. Its drawback synergizes with this deck's sacrifice theme — feeding it a mana dork or an end-of-turn Blitz token triggers Butcher of Malakir and all other death-payoff effects. A threat that demands immediate answers.

### 🐉 The Blitz Brigade — Death Trigger Creatures (6 Cards)
*Creatures whose primary value comes from their death trigger — maximized by Blitz's automatic end-of-turn sacrifice.*

*   **Seedguide Ash:** 3/3 for {4}{G}. When it dies, search for up to 3 Forests and put them onto the battlefield tapped. A single Blitz sacrifice generates three land drops — massive acceleration toward future Henzie casts and commander tax payments.
*   **Gamekeeper:** 2/2 for {3}{G}. When it dies, exile it then reveal cards from the top of your library until you reveal a creature card — put that creature onto the battlefield. A free creature cheat triggered automatically by every Blitz sacrifice, potentially dropping an 8-drop into play for free.
*   **Mitotic Slime:** 4/4 for {4}{G}. When it dies, create two 2/2 Ooze tokens. When each of those dies, create two 1/1 Ooze tokens. One Blitz sacrifice generates a board of six tokens as blockers and future sacrifice fodder — with Butcher of Malakir online, this cascades into multiple forced opponent sacrifices.
*   **Kura, the Boundless Sky:** 4/4 flying for {4}{G}. When it dies, choose one: search for up to 3 Forests, or create a 6/6 green Spirit token. The land option ramps aggressively into future Henzie recasts; the token option creates a massive permanent blocker that outlasts the Blitz.
*   **Junji, the Midnight Sky:** 5/5 flying for {3}{B}{B}. When it dies, choose one: each opponent discards 2 cards and loses 2 life, or return target creature card from any graveyard to the battlefield under your control. The reanimation mode recovers any Blitz bomb sent to the graveyard earlier in the game.
*   **Atsushi, the Blazing Sky:** 4/4 flying for {3}{R}{R}. When it dies, choose one: create 3 Treasure tokens, or exile the top 4 cards of your library and cast them this turn. The Treasure mode provides instant burst mana for a second Blitz in the same turn; the exile mode turns the death into a massive impulsive draw.

### 🐉 The Blitz Brigade — Giant Threats (3 Cards)
*High-power bodies whose size alone generates value through combat and on-entry effects.*

*   **Giant Adephage:** 7/7 trample for {5}{G}{G}. When it deals combat damage to a player, create a token copy of it. With Blitz, it enters with haste, attacks immediately, and if it connects creates a permanent 7/7 trample token. Even if sacrificed at end of turn the token persists as a long-term threat.
*   **Treeshaker Chimera:** 9/5 flying for {5}{G}{G}. When it attacks, tap all creatures target player controls. An air-superiority enforcer that taps down an entire opponent's board — clearing all defenders for Henzie and your other Blitzers in the same combat step it enters.
*   **First Responder:** A haste-enabled Blitz threat from Streets of New Capenna. Enters with haste to provide immediate attack or ETB value, then draws a card when sacrificed via Blitz at end of turn.

### ♻️ Feldon & the Graveyard Engine (1 Card)
*   **Feldon of the Third Path:** 2/3 for {1}{R}{R}. Tap — create a token copy of a creature card in your graveyard until end of turn with haste; sacrifice at end of turn. Feldon is a one-card Blitz engine — each turn he reanimates your best Blitz bomb from the graveyard for another ETB trigger, completely bypassing Henzie's escalating commander tax.

### ⚡ Sacrifice Synergies & Combat Engines (4 Cards)
*Permanents that convert end-of-turn Blitz sacrifices into damage, Treasures, or forced opponent sacrifices.*

*   **Ziatora, the Incinerator:** 6/6 trample for {3}{B}{R}{G}. At the beginning of combat, sacrifice a creature to deal damage equal to its power to any target and create 3 Treasure tokens. Sacrifice the Blitz creature to Ziatora *before* the Blitz trigger resolves — dealing 5–11 damage and generating 3 Treasures from a creature you were going to sacrifice anyway.
*   **Butcher of Malakir:** 5/4 flying for {5}{B}{B}. Whenever a creature you control dies, each opponent sacrifices a creature. The creature version of Grave Pact — stacks with every Blitz sacrifice and every Demon's Disciple or Plaguecrafter trigger. Multiple Blitz sacrifices in one turn forces opponents to sacrifice multiple times in a single end step.
*   **Ognis, the Dragon's Lash:** 4/3 haste for {2}{B}{R}{G}. Whenever a creature you control with haste attacks, create a Treasure token. Every Blitz creature (which enters with haste by definition) generates a free Treasure on attack — burst mana to cast additional Blitz creatures in the same turn.
*   **Kodama of the East Tree:** 4/4 reach for {4}{G}{G}. Whenever another permanent enters the battlefield under your control, you may put a permanent card with equal or lesser mana value from your hand into play. Every land drop, Treasure token, or creature entry cascades into a free permanent — connecting Ognis's Treasures and Blitz entries into free cards from hand each trigger.

### 🔥 Value Engines (9 Cards)
*Permanents that generate ongoing advantage from creatures entering or dying — the infrastructure layer of the Blitz engine.*

*   **Garruk's Uprising:** When a creature with power 4 or greater ETBs, draw a card. Gives all your creatures trample. Every Blitz entry draws a card from this enchantment *and* a card from the Blitz sacrifice — double card advantage per creature. Trample on Etali and the primordials ensures combat damage connects through token blockers.
*   **Industrial Advancement:** At the beginning of your end step, you may sacrifice a creature. If you do, look at the top X cards of your library equal to its mana value and put a creature from among them onto the battlefield. Sacrifice the Blitz creature to Industrial Advancement to cheat the next bomb directly from your library — a self-sustaining Blitz chain.
*   **Evolutionary Leap:** {1}{G}, Sacrifice a creature: reveal cards from your library until you reveal a creature, put it in your hand. Converts an end-of-turn Blitz sacrifice into a tutored creature search — insurance against running out of Blitz targets.
*   **Riveteers Ascendancy:** Whenever a nontoken creature you control dies, look at the top three cards and you may put a creature card of lesser power into play. Every Blitz sacrifice becomes a permanent creature cheat from the top three — high-value recursion that completely bypasses Henzie's commander tax.
*   **Deathreap Ritual:** Whenever a creature dies, if mana was spent during this phase, draw a card. Draws a card on every Blitz sacrifice *on top of* the Blitz draw trigger — two cards per Blitz creature when both are online.
*   **Moldervine Reclamation:** Whenever a creature you control dies, draw a card and gain 1 life. Straight one-for-one replacement draw on every Blitz sacrifice. Also draws on opponent sacrifices triggered by Plaguecrafter and Butcher of Malakir chains.
*   **Warstorm Surge:** Whenever a creature enters the battlefield under your control, it deals damage equal to its power to any target. Every Blitz entry deals direct damage — a 6/6 ETB kills a 6-toughness blocker or deals 6 to face. With multiple Blitz triggers per turn this closes out games without ever connecting with combat.
*   **Rhythm of the Wild:** Creatures you control can't be countered. Each nontoken creature gets Riot — choose +1/+1 counters or haste. Henzie already provides haste via Blitz, so Riot usually means +1/+1 counters on Henzie himself to grow him into a meaningful blocker. Counterspell protection prevents opponents from stopping key Blitz entries on the stack.
*   **Cauldron of Souls:** Tap — give any number of target creatures Persist until end of turn. Activate in response to the Blitz end-of-turn sacrifice trigger. The creature dies, Persist returns it with a -1/-1 counter, and its ETB fires *again* before the counter matters. A free second ETB trigger from every Blitz creature in the deck.

### 🃏 Card Draw — Big Refuel Spells (3 Cards)
*Mass draw spells that refuel the hand after a Blitz chain empties it.*

*   **Greater Good:** {3}{G} enchantment. Sacrifice a creature: draw cards equal to its power, then discard 3. With an 11-power Daemogoth or a 7-power Giant Adephage, draws 7–11 cards and fills the graveyard for Living Death. Activate in response to the Blitz sacrifice trigger for a massive hand reload.
*   **Life's Legacy:** {1}{G} sorcery. Sacrifice a creature: draw cards equal to its power. The cheapest mass draw in the deck — sacrifice a 7/7 Giant Adephage for 7 cards at sorcery speed mid-turn.
*   **Momentous Fall:** {2}{G}{G} instant. Sacrifice a creature: draw cards equal to its power and gain life equal to its toughness. Instant-speed — use in response to targeted removal to sacrifice the creature before it's destroyed and draw a massive hand replacement.

### ⚰️ Recursion & Reanimation (4 Cards)
*Mass reanimation and bounce to rebuild after board wipes or to recycle key Blitz targets.*

*   **Living Death:** {3}{B}{B} sorcery. Each player exiles all creature cards from their graveyard, then all players sacrifice all creatures they control, then each player puts all cards they exiled this way onto the battlefield. The game-winning reset — after a full Blitz cycle fills your graveyard with bombs, Living Death puts them all back simultaneously while clearing opponents' boards.
*   **Victimize:** {2}{B} sorcery. Sacrifice a creature: return up to two target creature cards from your graveyard to the battlefield. Sacrifice a spent mana dork or the Blitz token to return two Blitz bombs directly from the graveyard in one efficient spell.
*   **Dread Return:** {2}{B}{B} sorcery. Return target creature from a graveyard to the battlefield. Flashback — sacrifice three creatures. The flashback cost is nearly free in this deck — sacrifice three spent mana dorks or Blitz tokens to reanimate the best creature in any graveyard at no mana cost.
*   **Temur Sabertooth:** 4/3 for {2}{G}{G}. {1}{G}: Return a creature you control to its owner's hand. Bounces ETB creatures back to hand for another cast and another ETB trigger — bypasses Henzie's commander tax by replaying a Blitz bomb from hand instead. Also protects key permanents from removal by bouncing them in response to destroy effects.

### 🌿 Mana Dorks (6 Cards)
*One-mana accelerants enabling a turn-2 Henzie — the backbone of the fast-start game plan.*

*   **Elves of Deep Shadow:** Taps for {B} or {G}, paying 1 life for black mana. Provides critical early black fixing for Demon's Disciple, Plaguecrafter, Gravelighter, and Junji while accelerating toward Henzie.
*   **Elvish Mystic:** The classic one-drop green dork. Puts Henzie out a full turn ahead of schedule.
*   **Fyndhorn Elves:** Functionally identical to Llanowar Elves — redundancy ensures a turn-1 dork is consistently available across opening hands.
*   **Llanowar Elves:** The original one-drop accelerant. With multiple one-mana dorks available, turn-2 Henzie is a consistent line rather than an exceptional draw.
*   **Paradise Druid:** Taps for any color with 1 life for off-color. Fixes all three colors on turn one and has hexproof while untapped — protected from targeted removal as long as it isn't tapped.
*   **Sakura-Tribe Elder:** Blocks an early aggressor, then sacrifices to search for a basic land. Ramp stapled to a chump blocker — slows fast starts while accelerating toward Henzie.

### 🌱 Ramp Spells (3 Cards)
*   **Farseek:** Find any Forest-typed dual land (Cinder Glade, Smoldering Marsh, etc.) and put it in play tapped. Best two-mana ramp in this land base — fetches color-fixing duals rather than only basics.
*   **Nature's Lore:** Find any Forest-typed land and put it in play *untapped*. Faster than Farseek on the turn it resolves — a Forest-typed dual enters untapped and immediately produces mana.
*   **Rampant Growth:** Find any basic land, put it in play tapped. Reliable and universal.

### 🪨 Mana Rocks & Cost Reduction (3 Cards)
*   **Arcane Signet:** Two mana, taps for any color in Henzie's identity. Covers B, R, and G equally — the most flexible accelerant after Sol Ring.
*   **Sol Ring:** One mana for two. The most important turn-1 play in the format.
*   **Goreclaw, Terror of Qal Sisma:** 4/3 for {3}{G}. Creatures with power 4 or greater cost {2} less to cast. Henzie's Blitz reduction already scales down costs — Goreclaw stacks on top, enabling near-zero-cost Blitz on some of the biggest threats. When those creatures attack they gain +1/+1 and trample.

### ⚔️ Removal & Interaction (7 Cards)
*   **Beast Within:** Destroy any permanent, its owner gets a 3/3 Beast token. Unconditional instant-speed answer — hits artifacts, enchantments, planeswalkers, lands, and creatures. A 3/3 Beast is irrelevant in a board state full of 6/6 and 7/7 Blitz threats.
*   **Chaos Warp:** Shuffle target permanent into its owner's library, its owner reveals the top card — if it's a permanent it enters. Red's answer to problematic enchantments and indestructible permanents that other removal can't handle.
*   **Terminate:** {B}{R} instant — destroy target creature. It can't be regenerated. The cheapest unconditional creature removal in the format. Two-mana instant-speed answer to any creature threat.
*   **Putrefy:** {1}{B}{G} instant — destroy target artifact or creature. It can't be regenerated. Covers both creatures and artifacts at three mana — hits Sol Ring, Rhystic Study, or any creature with the same card.
*   **Blasphemous Act:** Costs {1} with enough creatures in play — deals 13 damage to all creatures. The best red board wipe in the format. Resets the table when opponents develop faster than your Blitz engine.
*   **Rakdos Charm:** {B}{R} instant. Choose one: exile target graveyard; destroy target artifact; each creature an opponent controls deals 1 damage to its controller. A modular three-in-one — graveyard hate, artifact removal, or token sweeper on the same card.
*   **Golgari Charm:** {B}{G} instant. Choose one: all creatures get -1/-1 until end of turn; regenerate each creature you control; destroy target enchantment. Sweeps token boards, saves your creatures from a mass removal spell, or destroys a key enchantment.

### 🏔️ Lands (36 Cards)
*   **Command Tower:** Taps for any color in Henzie's identity. Auto-include.
*   **Path of Ancestry:** Taps for any color in the deck's identity. When used to cast a creature, scry 1. In a creature-heavy Blitz deck, the scry 1 fires every turn.
*   **Kessig Wolf Run:** {X}{R}{G}, tap: target creature gets +X/+0 and trample until end of turn. Converts Henzie or any large Blitz creature into a one-shot lethal threat when opponents tap out to deal with other threats.
*   **Mosswort Bridge:** Hideaway — exile a card face-down. {4} — if you control a creature with power 10 or greater, cast the exiled card for free. With Daemogoth Woe-Eater or Giant Adephage on board, the condition is easily satisfied.
*   **Temple of the False God:** Taps for {C}{C} once you control five or more lands. Free ramp mid-game — covers half of Henzie's mana cost or funds a Blitz activation.
*   **Savage Lands:** Tri-land that taps for {B}, {R}, or {G}, enters tapped. Perfect three-color fixing at the cost of one turn of tempo.
*   **Riveteers Overlook:** Jund tri-land from Streets of New Capenna that enters tapped and fixes all three colors.
*   **Cinder Glade:** Forest and Mountain — enters untapped with two or more basic lands. Near-always untapped in this 17-basic land base.
*   **Smoldering Marsh:** Swamp and Mountain — enters untapped if you control a Swamp or Mountain. Consistent Rakdos fixing.
*   **Exotic Orchard:** Taps for any color an opponent can produce. In a four-player pod reliably produces B, R, and G.
*   **Foreboding Ruins:** Enters untapped if you reveal a Swamp or Mountain or control one. Budget Rakdos dual.
*   **Game Trail:** Enters untapped if you reveal a Forest or Mountain. With 17 basics, almost always an untapped Gruul dual.
*   **Necroblossom Snarl:** Enters untapped if you reveal a Swamp or Forest. Golgari fixing.
*   **Llanowar Wastes:** Taps for {B} or {G} with 1 damage. Effectively an untapped dual with negligible cost.
*   **Karplusan Forest:** Taps for {R} or {G} with 1 damage. Gruul pain land — near-identical to Llanowar Wastes.
*   **Sulfurous Springs:** Taps for {B} or {R} with 1 damage. Completes the pain land cycle for Rakdos fixing.
*   **Temple of Malady:** Enters tapped, scry 1. Golgari color fixing with deck filtering.
*   **Temple of Abandon:** Enters tapped, scry 1. Gruul color fixing with deck filtering.
*   **Temple of Malice:** Enters tapped, scry 1. Rakdos color fixing with deck filtering.
*   **7 Forest / 5 Swamp / 5 Mountain:** 17 basics provide consistent targets for Farseek, Nature's Lore, Rampant Growth, Sakura-Tribe Elder, and Seedguide Ash.

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
*   **Grave Pact ({1}{B}{B}{B}):** The enchantment version of Butcher of Malakir. Stacks with Butcher for double forced-sacrifice triggers on every Blitz death — opponents can never maintain a board state.
*   **Phyrexian Altar ({3}):** Sacrifice a creature: add one mana of any color. Converts end-of-turn Blitz sacrifices into free colored mana for the next Blitz activation — near-infinite value loop with Henzie's cost reduction.
*   **Ashnod's Altar ({3}):** Sacrifice a creature: add {C}{C}. Colorless version of Phyrexian Altar — cheaper to acquire, still generates burst mana from every Blitz death.
*   **Birthing Pod ({3}{G/P}):** Pay 1 life, tap, sacrifice a creature: search your library for a creature with mana value 1 greater and put it into play. A Blitz chain through the Pod tutors creatures in order while firing their ETBs — Henzie's best long-game value engine at a budget price.
*   **Bolas's Citadel ({3}{B}{B}{B}):** Look at the top card of your library at any time; play lands and cast spells from the top by paying life instead of mana. After Blitz cycles fill the graveyard and Life's Legacy / Greater Good reload the hand, the Citadel chains spells off the top of the deck paying life instead of mana.

### ⚠️ Bracket Notes
*   This deck is comfortably Bracket 2. Grave Pact and Phyrexian Altar push it toward the high end of Bracket 2 — discuss with your pod before adding both simultaneously.
*   No Game Changers are currently included.
