# Deck Guide: Sauron, the Budget Dark Lord

## Commander Strategy
**Sauron, the Dark Lord**
*   **Archetype:** Grixis (UBR) Midrange / Amass / Ring Temptation
*   **Bracket:** 2 (Core)
*   **Goal:** Use the "Ring Tempts You" mechanic to consistently trigger Sauron's card-draw ability (discarding your hand and drawing 4) while building a massive Orc Army token.
*   **Resilience:** Sauron's **Ward—Sacrifice a legendary artifact or legendary creature** makes him incredibly difficult to remove in a Bracket 2 environment. Even against Elesh Norn, your Orc Army token grows with +1/+1 counters, allowing it to easily outscale the -2/-2 debuff.

## 1. The Army: Amass & Synergy (10)
*Cards that build your Orc Army or benefit from its size.*
*   Mauhúr, Uruk-hai Captain (Doubles counters)  
*   Warg Rider (Grants Myriad to the Army)  
*   Gothmog, Morgul Lieutenant (Grants Deathtouch to all tokens)  
*   Saruman the White (Amass on noncreature spells)  
*   Grishnákh, Brash Instigator (Steals a creature based on Army power)  
*   Mordor Muster (Cheap Amass + Draw)  
*   March from the Black Gate (Persistent Amass on attack)  
*   Gloin, Dwarf Emissary (Generates Treasures)  
*   Dunland Crebain (Flying Amass)  
*   Gorbag of Minas Morgul (Sacrifice Orcs for mana/draw)  

## 2. The Ring: Temptation Engine (10)
*Triggers Sauron's "Wheel" ability and buffs your Ring-bearer.*
*   Call of the Ring (Consistent upkeep temptation)  
*   Birthday Escape (Cheap cantrip + temptation)  
*   Inherited Envelope (Ramp + temptation)  
*   Claim the Precious (Removal + temptation)  
*   Ringsight (Tutor + temptation)  
*   One Ring to Rule Them All (Board wipe + temptation)  
*   Bombadil's Song (Protection + temptation)  
*   Soothing of Smeagol (Bounce + temptation)  
*   Nazgûl (Only 1 copy for budget/bracket reasons - Deathtouch + temptation)  
*   The One Ring (NOTE: Replaced with *Ring of Evos Isle* for budget/Bracket 2 compliance)
