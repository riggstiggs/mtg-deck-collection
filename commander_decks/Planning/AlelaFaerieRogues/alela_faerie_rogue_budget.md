# Alela, Cunning Conqueror: Faerie & Rogue Tribal (Budget)

This deck is built for Christina, focusing on **Faerie and Rogue tribal synergies** with a heavy emphasis on **Flash and Goad mechanics**. It adheres to the "New Era" template (38/10/12/12/6/31) and is designed for **Bracket 2** gameplay with a total cost under $100.

## 🏹 Strategy & Playstyle

*   **Stick and Protect:** Alela wants you to cast spells on *each* of your opponents' turns. This creates 1/1 Faerie Rogue tokens and triggers her goad ability.
*   **Political Goading:** Use your tokens to poke opponents. Alela forces their largest threats to attack each other, keeping the heat off you.
*   **The Finisher:** **Shadow Puppeteers** is the secret weapon. It turns your swarm of 1/1 tokens into 4/4 Dragons with Flying, ending the game quickly.
*   **Tempo Interaction:** You have a high count of instant-speed removal and counterspells to disrupt opponents while simultaneously advancing your board state.

## 🛠️ Notable Budget Swaps

| Original (Expensive) | Budget Replacement | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bitterblossom** | **Alela / Feywild Visitor** | Alela already generates tokens; extra $17 isn't needed. |
| **Deadly Rollick** | **Rapid Hybridization** | $20 vs $1. Instant speed removal at a fraction of the cost. |
| **Black Market Connections**| **Coastal Piracy** | $15 vs $1. We have enough tokens to draw cards via combat. |
| **Morphic Pool** | **Tainted Isle / Sunken Hollow** | $15 vs $1. Great budget duals that enter untapped often. |
| **Sword of Feast & Famine** | **Sword of the Animist** | $40 vs $5. Provides ramp instead of untap, better for budget. |
| **Faerie Mastermind** | **Nymris, Oona's Trickster** | $12 vs $0.25. Similar value engine for flash decks. |
