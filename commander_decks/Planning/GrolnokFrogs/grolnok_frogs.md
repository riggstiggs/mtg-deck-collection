# Deck Guide: Grolnok, the Omnivore (The Frog Pond)

![Grolnok, the Omnivore](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/5/3/531cace5-98df-4291-af18-ace91a5a40dc.jpg)

## Commander Strategy
**Grolnok, the Omnivore** ({2}{G}{U})

- **Archetype:** Simic Tribal Mill/Value Engine
- **Bracket:** 2
- **Core Goal:** Mill your own library with attacking Frogs (and Changeling/type-lord enablers making everything a Frog), then play lands and cast spells from exile via Grolnok's croak counter ability. The deck wins through incremental card advantage and a board of flash threats, counterspells on creatures, and removal-enchantments that keep opponents off balance.

### The Frog Engine
Every attacking Frog mills three cards, exiling permanents with croak counters. Type-lords (Arcane Adaptation, Maskwood Nexus, Leyline of Transformation, Runed Stalactite) and Changelings make every creature a Frog, turning the whole board into a mill/exile engine. Doc Aurlock reduces costs on spells cast from exile, making the rebuy effect even more explosive.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🐸 Commander (1 Card)
- **Grolnok, the Omnivore** ({2}{G}{U}): Mills 3 on each Frog attack; exiles milled permanents with croak counters for free casting later.

### 🐸 Frogs & Frog Payoffs (17 Cards)
- **Clement, the Worrywort** ({1}{G}{U}): Taps Frogs for {G}/{U} to cast creature spells; bounces your own creatures for replay value.
- **Clifftop Lookout** ({2}{G}): Ramps by putting a land onto the battlefield when it enters.
- **Chomping Changeling** ({2}{G}): Changeling; destroys an artifact or enchantment on entry.
- **Dreamdew Entrancer** ({2}{G}{U}): Taps and stun-counters a threat; draws two cards if targeting your own creature.
- **Frog Butler** ({1}{G}): Deathtouch mana dork; produces any color.
- **Froghemoth** ({3}{G}{G}): Trample/haste; exiles cards from opponents' graveyards and grows on combat damage.
- **Long River Lurker** ({2}{U}): Ward {1}; gives another creature unblockability and blink-resets it for ETB value.
- **Poison Dart Frog** ({1}{G}): Reach mana dork; can gain deathtouch as a combat trick.
- **Rampant Frogantua** ({2}{G}): Trample; mills and drops lands from combat damage; gets massive if a player is eliminated.
- **Spore Frog** ({G}): Fog on a stick; sacrifices to prevent all combat damage.
- **Risen Reef** ({1}{G}{U}): Every Elemental (or Frog via type-lords) entering puts a land into play or draws a card.

### 🔄 Changeling / Type Enablers (8 Cards)
- **Amoeboid Changeling** ({1}{U}): Changeling; grants or strips all creature types from any target at instant speed — use to make opponents' creatures Frogs for Grolnok triggers, or strip types as interaction.
- **Bloodline Pretender** ({3}): Changeling artifact; grows with each creature of the chosen type entering.
- **Chameleon Colossus** ({2}{G}{G}): Changeling with protection from black; doubles its own power for a turn.
- **Hakbal of the Surging Soul** ({2}{G}{U}): All Merfolk (read: all creatures via type-lords) explore each combat; puts lands into play when he attacks.
- **Masked Vandal** ({1}{G}): Changeling; exiles a graveyard card to destroy an artifact or enchantment.
- **Mutable Explorer** ({2}{G}): Changeling; creates a tapped Mutavault token for additional Frog attacks.
- **Mutavault**: Activates as a 2/2 with all creature types — counts as a Frog for Grolnok.

### 🔮 Type-Lord Enablers (4 Cards)
- **Arcane Adaptation** ({2}{U}): Names Frog; all your creatures become Frogs permanently.
- **Leyline of Transformation** ({2}{U}{U}): Choosse Frog at setup; potentially in opening hand for free. Makes all your creatures Frogs.
- **Maskwood Nexus** ({4}): All creatures you control are every type; also generates Changeling tokens.
- **Runed Stalactite** ({1}): Equip {2}; equipped creature becomes every creature type.

### ⚡ Flash Interaction / Counterspells (6 Cards)
- **Fear of Impostors** ({1}{U}{U}): Flash creature that counters a spell and manifests dread for the opponent.
- **Frilled Mystic** ({G}{G}{U}{U}): Flash Elf Lizard Wizard; counters a spell on entry.
- **Mystic Snake** ({1}{G}{U}{U}): Flash; counters target spell on entry — classic Simic counterspell creature.
- **Overcharged Amalgam** ({2}{U}{U}): Flash flying Zombie; exploits a creature to counter a spell, ability, or triggered ability.
- **Venser, Shaper Savant** ({2}{U}{U}): Flash; bounces any spell or permanent — flexible catch-all.
- **Vodalian Hexcatcher** ({1}{U}): Flash; buffs Merfolk (all creatures via type-lords); sacrifice a Merfolk to counter a noncreature spell for {1}.

### 🗡️ Targeted Removal / Control Enchantments (7 Cards)
- **Burden of Proof** ({1}{U}): Flash Aura; shrinks non-Detective targets to 1/1 that can't block Detectives.
- **Eaten by Piranhas** ({1}{U}): Flash Aura; strips all abilities and reduces to a 1/1 black Skeleton.
- **Imprisoned in the Moon** ({2}{U}): Turns any creature, land, or planeswalker into a colorless land — hard permanent removal.
- **Lignify** ({1}{G}): Turns a creature into a 0/4 Treefolk with no abilities — cheap permanent answer.
- **Noggle the Mind** ({1}{U}): Flash Aura; strips abilities and turns target into a 1/1 colorless Noggle.
- **Unable to Scream** ({U}): Strips abilities and turns creature into a 0/2 Toy artifact — cheapest answer.
- **Utter Insignificance** ({1}{U}): Flash Aura; strips abilities and shrinks to 1/1; can later exile the creature for {2}{C}.
- **Witness Protection** ({U}): Strips all abilities, types, and name — turns target into a vanilla 1/1 Citizen.

### 🎯 Utility / Support (5 Cards)
- **Chalk Outline** ({3}{G}): Creates Detective tokens and Clue tokens whenever creature cards leave your graveyard — synergy with Grolnok's milling.
- **Confession Dial** ({3}): Surveils 3 on entry; gives legendary creatures in your graveyard escape until EOT.
- **Cresting Mosasaurus** ({6}{U}{U}): Emerge {6}{U}; bounces all non-Dinosaurs (your creatures are Frogs/Dinosaurs via type-lords) — one-sided board reset.
- **Desecrated Tomb** ({3}): Creates a 1/1 flying Bat whenever creature cards leave your graveyard — pairs with Grolnok's exile engine.
- **Doc Aurlock, Grizzled Genius** ({G}{U}): Reduces costs of spells cast from exile or graveyard by {2} — directly fuels the croak counter engine.
- **Hylda's Crown of Winter** ({3}): Taps creatures; mass draw by sacrificing when opponents have many tapped creatures.
- **Kellan, Inquisitive Prodigy** ({2}{G}{U}): Adventure card; provides card selection and a solid Frog-eligible body.
- **Willow Geist** ({G}): Grows whenever cards leave your graveyard; gains life on death — incidental payoff for milling.
- **Rooftop Storm** ({5}{U}): Makes Zombie spells free — primarily here if type-lords make your creatures Zombies; notable synergy with Overcharged Amalgam and the Zombie package.
- **Perpetual Timepiece** ({2}): Mills 2 per tap; can shuffle graveyard back into library as insurance against decking yourself.
- **Hedge Shredder** ({2}{G}{G}): Vehicle that mills on attacks and puts land cards from the graveyard onto the battlefield.
- **Tlincalli Hunter** ({5}{G}{G}): Adventure half retrieves a creature; large trample body as top-end.

### 🌱 Ramp (11 Cards)
- **Arcane Signet** ({2}): Produces {G} or {U}.
- **Clement, the Worrywort** (see Frogs): Taps Frogs as mana dorks.
- **Fertile Ground** ({1}{G}): Enchants a land to produce two mana or any color.
- **Frog Butler** (see Frogs): Any-color mana dork.
- **Poison Dart Frog** (see Frogs): Any-color mana dork.
- **Sakura-Tribe Elder** ({1}{G}): Sacrifices to tutor a basic land to the battlefield.
- **Simic Signet** ({2}): Produces {G}{U}.
- **Sol Ring** ({1}): Produces {C}{C}.
- **Talisman of Curiosity** ({2}): Produces {G} or {U}.
- **Tangled Florahedron** ({1}{G}): MDFC — creature mana dork or tapped Forest land.
- **Utopia Sprawl** ({G}): Enchants a Forest; produces two mana of chosen colors.
- **Wild Growth** ({G}): Enchants a land to produce one additional mana.
- **Wolfwillow Haven** ({1}{G}): Enchants a land for extra {G}; later sacrifices to make a 2/2 Wolf.

### 🗺️ Lands (36 Cards)
- **Alchemist's Refuge**: Flash enabler land — lets you cast spells from exile at instant speed.
- **Argoth, Sanctum of Nature**: Creates Bear tokens and mills; enters untapped with a legendary green creature.
- **Command Tower**: Produces {G} or {U}.
- **Conduit Pylons**: Desert; surveils 1 on entry; any-color mana for {1}.
- **Dryad Arbor**: Forest creature land; a Frog via type-lords.
- **Flooded Grove**: Filter land; produces {G}{G}, {U}{U}, or {G}{U}.
- **Gallifrey Council Chamber**: Surveils 1 on entry; produces any color for Time Lord/Alien spells (relevant with Amoeboid Changeling making things Time Lords... mostly a flavor land).
- **Hidden Grotto**: Surveils 1 on entry; any-color mana for {1}.
- **Hinterland Harbor**: Check land; untaps alongside Forest or Island.
- **Kishla Village**: Enters tapped without Island/Swamp; produces {G}; surveils 2 for {3}{G}.
- **Lilypad Village**: Produces {U} for creature spells; surveils 2 when a Frog/Bird/Otter/Rat entered this turn.
- **Myriad Landscape**: Tutors two basic lands of the same type to the battlefield.
- **Mutavault**: See Changeling section.
- **Oakhollow Village**: Produces {G} for creature spells; places +1/+1 counters on Frogs/etc. that entered this turn.
- **Ominous Cemetery**: Tucks a creature into an opponent's library for {5}{T} + exile.
- **Path of Ancestry**: Tribal scry land for Frogs (and every other type via Changelings).
- **Simic Growth Chamber**: Bounces a land; produces {G}{U}.
- **Tocasia's Dig Site**: Surveils 1 for {3}.
- **Trenzalore Clocktower**: Produces {U} and accumulates time counters; resets hand/library for {1}{U} + 12 counters (requires Time Lord — mostly incidental).
- **Vineglimmer Snarl**: Check land; untaps with Forest or Island in hand.
- **Yavimaya Coast**: Pain land; produces {G}, {U}, or {C} with 1 damage.
- **9 Forest** / **6 Island**: Basic lands.
- **The Everflowing Well**: MDFC legendary artifact // artifact land.

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
- **Kindred Discovery** ({3}{U}{U}): Draw a card whenever a Frog enters or attacks — obscene card advantage with this many Frogs.
- **Amphin Mutagen** ({4}{U}{U}): Turns all non-Frog creatures into 4/4 Frogs — one-sided board transformation.
- **Croak and Carry** ({U}): Frog-specific bounce or counter — worth checking recent Bloomburrow printings.
- **Yuriko, the Tiger's Shadow**: If ever splashing or for inspiration — not legal here, but a comparable "attack triggers" commander to study.
- **Kodama's Reach** ({2}{G}): More reliable ramp at sorcery speed.
- **Counterspell** ({U}{U}): Hard counter to replace one of the softer control auras.
