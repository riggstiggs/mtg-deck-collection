# Potential Future Commanders

This file tracks commanders that are under consideration for future builds, categorized by intended pilot.

## 👑 For Jamie (Wife)
*Ideas focusing on unique mechanics, aesthetics, or interactive playstyles.*

*   **Zimone Engine** ({G}{U}): **Active Planning Project.** A Simic Landfall "Feedback Loop" focused on high-speed ramp and token generation.
*   **Bess, Soul Nourisher** ({1}{G}{W}): **1/1 Weenie Tribal.** Grows herself and your entire board of 1/1 creatures whenever you create them. Extremely strong with Selesnya token generators.
*   **Zur, Eternal Schemer** ({W}{U}{B}): **Enchantment Creatures.** Turns non-Aura enchantments into creatures with Lifelink, Deathtouch, and Hexproof. High resilience and "sneaky" aggression.
*   **Oloro, Ageless Ascetic** ({3}{W}{U}{B}): **Passive Life Gain.** Provides value even from the Command Zone. Classic Esper control/drain shell.
*   **Hakbal of the Surging Soul** ({2}{G}{U}): **Merfolk Explore Tribal.** Every Merfolk explores at the start of combat; lands from hand hit the battlefield when Hakbal attacks. Strong Simic value engine with tribal upside.
*   **Mr. House, President and CEO** ({R}{W}{B}): **Dice Rolling / Treasures.** Mardu artifact-creature that generates Robot and Treasure tokens from dice rolls, then snowballs by spending Treasures to roll more dice. Universes Beyond (Fallout).
*   **Arabella, Abandoned Doll** ({R}{W}): **Wide Weenie Aggro.** Deals damage to all opponents and gains life equal to the number of creatures you control with power 2 or less when she attacks. Strong with go-wide token strategies. Universes Beyond (Duskmourn).
*   **The Wandering Minstrel** ({G}{U}): **Town Synergy / Lands.** All your lands enter untapped; generates 2/2 Elemental tokens and a massive board pump with enough Towns. Universes Beyond (Final Fantasy).
*   **Mathise, Surge Channeler** ({2}{U}): **Wild Magic / Dice Rolling.** (Secret Lair alt-art: Simon, Wild Magic Sorcerer.) Rolls a d20 whenever you cast a big instant or sorcery — shared draw, personal draw, or a free copy on a nat 20. Spellslinger with chaos upside.
*   **Adrix and Nev, Twincasters** ({2}{G}{U}): **Token Multiplication.** Doubles all tokens created. Extremely explosive Simic value engine.
*   **Kynaios and Tiro of Meletis** ({R}{G}{W}{U}): **Group Hug / Diplomacy.** Accelerates the whole table while providing massive card draw and land drops for the pilot.
*   **Ovika, Enigma Goliath** ({5}{U}{R}): **Spells-into-Goblins.** Creates massive amounts of Phyrexian Goblins for every noncreature spell cast.
*   **Brenard, Ginger Sculptor** ({1}{G}{W}{U}): **Food/Golems.** Splicer/Artifact synergy that turns dying nontoken creatures into 1/1 Food Golem copies.

## 🗡️ For Mark
*Ideas focusing on high-complexity, land synergy, or group-slug mechanics.*

*   **Vivi Ornitier** ({1}{U}{R}): **Final Fantasy Secret Lair.** Explosive spellslinging and potentially Black Mage/artifact synergy.

*   **Thalia and the Gitrog Monster** ({W}{B}{G}): **Abzan Hate-Bears / Landfall.** Combines resource denial with massive land-based card draw. (Currently in Planning)
