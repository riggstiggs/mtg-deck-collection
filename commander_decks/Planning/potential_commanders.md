# Potential Future Commanders

This file tracks commanders that are under consideration for future builds, categorized by intended pilot.

## 👑 For Jamie (Wife)
*Ideas focusing on unique mechanics, aesthetics, or interactive playstyles.*

*   **Zur, Eternal Schemer** ({W}{U}{B}): **Enchantment Creatures.** Turns non-Aura enchantments into creatures with Lifelink, Deathtouch, and Hexproof. High resilience and "sneaky" aggression.
*   **Oloro, Ageless Ascetic** ({1}{W}{U}{B}): **Passive Life Gain.** Provides value even from the Command Zone. Classic Esper control/drain shell.
*   **Adrix and Nev, Twincasters** ({2}{G}{U}): **Token Multiplication.** Doubles all tokens created. Extremely explosive Simic value engine.
*   **Kynaios and Tiro of Meletis** ({R}{G}{W}{U}): **Group Hug / Diplomacy.** Accelerates the whole table while providing massive card draw and land drops for the pilot.
*   **Ovika, Enigma Goliath** ({5}{U}{R}): **Spells-into-Goblins.** Creates massive amounts of Phyrexian Goblins for every noncreature spell cast.
*   **Brenard, Ginger Sculptor** ({1}{G}{W}{U}): **Food/Golems.** Splicer/Artifact synergy that turns dying nontoken creatures into 1/1 Food Golem copies.

## 🗡️ For Mark
*Ideas focusing on high-complexity, land synergy, or group-slug mechanics.*

*   **Vivi Ornitier** ({1}{U}{R}): **Final Fantasy Secret Lair.** Explosive spellslinging and potentially Black Mage/artifact synergy.
*   **Nekusar, the Mindrazer** ({2}{U}{B}{R}): **Group Slug / Wheels.** Punishes opponents for drawing cards. Highly synergistic with the Grixis wheel package already in the collection.
*   **Omnath, Locus of Creation** ({W}{U}{R}{G}): **Landfall Mastery.** (Currently in Planning) High-power 4-color value engine.
*   **Thalia and the Gitrog Monster** ({W}{B}{G}): **Abzan Hate-Bears / Landfall.** Combines resource denial with massive land-based card draw. (Currently in Planning)
