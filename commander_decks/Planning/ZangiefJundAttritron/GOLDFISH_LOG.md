# Goldfish Simulation Log: Zangief, the Red Cyclone (The Siberian Blizzard)

## 🏆 Goldfish Game 1 (The Deathtouch Avalanche)
**Starting Hand:** Ohran Frostfang, Luxury Suite, Chandra's Ignition, Halana and Alena, Partners, Smoldering Marsh, Hunter's Talent, Swamp.
**Draws:** Sulfurous Springs, Bojuka Bog, Forest, Nemesis Mask, Rishkar's Expertise, Kessig Wolf Run, Damnation, Spire Garden, Gisa Glorious Resurrector, Gimli's Reckless Might.

*   **Turn 1-3:** Slow start, missed land drop on T3 due to tap-land. 
*   **Turn 4:** Halana and Alena.
*   **Turn 5:** Nemesis Mask played.
*   **Turn 6:** Zangief (9/6 with Haste from Halana).
*   **Turn 7:** Equipped Nemesis Mask. Zangief (11/8) forced a block and sacrifice. Draw 1 from Hunter's Talent.
*   **Turn 8:** Ohran Frostfang. Attack with Deathtouch/Lure Zangief (13/10). Draw 2.
*   **Turn 9:** Rishkar's Expertise. Draw 13 cards. Free cast Gisa.
*   **Turn 10:** Chandra's Ignition on Zangief (13/10). One-sided wipe. Gisa steals the board.
*   **Turn Comment:** Handled a slow start well. The power of Nemesis Mask + Zangief's base stats is enough to stabilize any game.

## 🏎️ Goldfish Game 2 (The Early Engine)
**Starting Hand:** Gisa Glorious Resurrector, Opal Palace, Sol Ring, Hunter's Insight, Rishkar's Expertise, Chaos Warp, Three Visits.
**Draws:** Nemesis Mask, General Marhault Elsdragon, Stomping Ground, Mountain, Mossfire Valley, Rootbound Crag, Morbid Opportunist.

*   **Turn 1:** Sol Ring.
*   **Turn 2:** Three Visits for Stomping Ground.
*   **Turn 3:** Zangief. (Turn 3 Commander!).
*   **Turn 4:** General Marhault Elsdragon. Zangief attacks.
*   **Turn 5:** Nemesis Mask + Equip. Zangief (10/7) forces massive blocks.
*   **Turn 6:** Rishkar's Expertise. Draw 10 cards. Free cast Gisa.
*   **Turn 7+:** Morbid Opportunist and Chevill join the fray. Total dominance.
*   **Turn Comment:** Ideal Sol Ring start. The deck reached "Game Over" state by Turn 5.

## 🐻 Goldfish Game 3 (The Inevitable Attrition)
**Starting Hand:** Exotic Orchard, Toski Bearer of Secrets, Forest, Xenagos God of Revels, Dragonskull Summit, High Market, Chevill Bane of Monsters.
**Draws:** Terminate, Forest, Mountain, Gimli's Reckless Might, Birds of Paradise, Rogue's Passage, Blood Artist, Luxury Suite, Blasphemous Act.

*   **Turn 2:** Chevill.
*   **Turn 4:** Toski.
*   **Turn 5:** Gimli's Reckless Might + Birds. Draw 2 from Toski.
*   **Turn 6:** Xenagos.
*   **Turn 7:** Zangief (14/11 with Haste/Power Double). Draw 3 from Toski.
*   **Turn 8:** Karlach. Two combat phases. Zangief hits for 14 then 28. Draw 8 cards.
*   **Turn Comment:** No early ramp but the Toski draw engine kept the hand full until Xenagos and Karlach turned Zangief into a one-shot machine.
