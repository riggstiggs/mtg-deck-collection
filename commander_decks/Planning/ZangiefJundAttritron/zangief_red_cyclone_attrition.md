# Deck Guide: Zangief, the Red Cyclone (The Siberian Blizzard)

## Commander Strategy
**Zangief, the Red Cyclone**
*   **Archetype:** Jund Forced-Combat / Attrition
*   **Bracket:** 2 (Core)
*   **Core Goal:** Utilize Zangief's "Indestructible on your turn" and "Excess Damage" triggers to force opponents into losing their nonland permanents. By combining Deathtouch and Trample, we ensure every block results in a sacrifice trigger.

---

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🗡️ Forced Combat & Lure (5 Cards)
*   **Nemesis Mask:** **MVP.** Forces every creature to block Zangief, clearing the path and triggering multiple sacrifices.  
*   **Lure:** Constant pressure to force blockers every turn.  
*   **Roar of Challenge:** Forces all creatures to block; Zangief is already indestructible on your turn.  
*   **Gimli's Reckless Might:** Grants Haste and punishes blockers by giving them -1/-1 counters.  
*   **Geode Rager:** Goads opponents' creatures, forcing them to attack elsewhere and leaving them open for your crackback.  

### 🛡️ Keyword Mastery & Stats (6 Cards)
*   **Basilisk Collar:** Deathtouch makes 1 damage lethal; the rest is excess. Lifelink keeps you in the game.  
*   **Shadowspear:** Trample for excess damage + Lifelink + Removal of Hexproof/Indestructible.  
*   **Saryth, the Viper's Fang:** Grants Deathtouch to attacking creatures and Hexproof to untapped ones.  
*   **Bow of Nylea:** Grants Deathtouch to attackers and provides versatile utility (counters, life, damage).  
*   **Mr. Orfeo, the Boulder:** Doubles Zangief's power on attack (base 14 power).  
*   **Xenagos, God of Revels:** Grants Haste and doubles power, making Zangief a one-shot threat.  

### 🥊 Combat Synergy & Finishers (5 Cards)
*   **General Marhault Elsdragon:** Grants +3/+3 for each creature blocking Zangief. Massive excess damage potential.  
*   **Karlach, Fury of Avernus:** Grants First Strike and an additional combat phase.  
*   **Halana and Alena, Partners:** Permanently buffs Zangief with +1/+1 counters and grants Haste.  
*   **Anzrag, the Quake-Mole:** Provides additional combat phases if he is blocked.  
*   **Gisa, Glorious Resurrector:** Steals the creatures Zangief kills or forces to be sacrificed.  

### 💉 Card Advantage & Anti-Stall (12 Cards)
*   **Hunter's Insight:** Draw cards equal to damage dealt (usually 7+).  
*   **Rishkar's Expertise:** Draws 7 cards and lets you cast a 5-drop for free.  
*   **Return of the Wildspeaker:** Burst draw or a massive board buff.  
*   **Garruk's Uprising:** Grants Trample and draws a card on entry and whenever a 4+ power creature enters.  
*   **Neyith of the Dire Hunt:** Draws cards when fighting or being blocked; doubles power.  
*   **Morbid Opportunist:** Consistent draw whenever a creature dies (including forced sacrifices).  
*   **Ohran Frostfang:** Grants Deathtouch and draws cards when creatures deal combat damage.  
*   **Toski, Bearer of Secrets:** Indestructible draw engine for every creature that hits.  
*   **Chevill, Bane of Monsters:** Deathtouch blocker that draws and gains life on "Bounty" deaths.  
*   **Infiltration Lens:** Draws 2 cards every time Zangief is blocked.  
*   **Hunter's Talent:** Fight spell that scales into a draw engine and power buff.  
*   **Greater Good:** Emergency sacrifice outlet to draw 7 cards if Zangief is targeted for exile.

## 🚀 High-Impact Considerations
*These cards are not currently in the deck but are top-tier candidates for future upgrades based on recent technical analysis.*

*   **Falthis, Shadowcat Familiar (2B):** Grants Deathtouch and Menace. Menace is arguably the best keyword for Zangief as it guarantees multiple blockers, making excess damage triggers nearly automatic.
    *   **Suggested Swap:** *Saryth, the Viper's Fang*. While Saryth provides protection, Falthis's Menace is a more aggressive and synergistic enabler for the Commander's triggers.
*   **Lizard Blades (1R):** Grants Double Strike. This allows Zangief to potentially trigger his "Excess Damage" sacrifice ability twice in a single combat (once in First Strike, once in regular).
    *   **Suggested Swap:** *Geode Rager*. Goad is powerful, but Double Strike is a force-multiplier for Zangief's core engine.
*   **Massacre Wurm (3BBB):** A one-sided board wipe for small creatures that punishes opponents for every death. Fits the "Attrition" theme perfectly while sparing Zangief.
    *   **Suggested Swap:** *Damnation*. Swapping a generic wipe for a synergistic one increases the deck's win-condition density.
*   **The Great Henge (7GG):** The ultimate Jund staple. Usually costs only {GG} with Zangief out. Provides ramp, lifegain, and draws a card every time a creature enters.
    *   **Suggested Swap:** *Greater Good*. Both are power-based draw engines, but The Great Henge provides significantly more cumulative value and board stabilization.
