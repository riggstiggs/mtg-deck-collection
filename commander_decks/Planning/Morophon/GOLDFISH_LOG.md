### Key Observations
- The deck hit Morophon by T7 in all 5 games with zero mulligans. Three of five games hit T5 — two full turns ahead of the goal. The mana base and ramp package are performing at a high level.
- Mana rocks were the primary accelerant in 4 of 5 games. Urza's Incubator was the standout — it functioned as both a mana accelerant and a cost reducer, enabling a T6 Morophon in G2 despite zero elf ramp. Fellwar Stone and Chromatic Lantern showed up in G3 and G4 respectively and delivered similar results.
- Creature count at Morophon's casting was modest (avg 2.2). This is expected and acceptable — the early turns are spent developing mana infrastructure, not flooding the board. Morophon landing with even 1-2 creatures in play is a strong position since it unlocks free or near-free changeling casts from that point.
- Guardian Project delivered meaningfully in Game 1 — it fired twice on T6 (Realmwalker + Bloodline Pretender entering) and drew the Hallowed Fountain that became the exact 7th mana source for Morophon on T7. Without those draws, Morophon would have been delayed to T8. The draw engine is working.
- Bloodline Pretender accumulated +1/+1 counters in multiple games as changelings and Shapeshifters entered the battlefield. It's a reliable value piece that scales throughout the game.

### Recommendations
- **No changes warranted by this data.** The deck's mana development is consistent and ahead of the T7 target in 3/5 games. The ramp package (mana rocks + elf ramp) is diverse enough that no single piece is required.
- **Goblin Warchief was stranded in G1** — no red mana was available by T7. This isn't a concern (it will always be castable eventually) but confirms the deck won't always have red mana accessible in the first 7 turns.
- **Monitor Cairn Wanderer's keyword acquisition.** In G2 it entered with no keywords (no creatures in graveyard) — in a real pod this resolves itself quickly, but it's worth noting the card can be a vanilla 4/4 in the early game.
- **Door of Destinies came online T4 in G3** and already had a charge counter when Morophon landed. This is ideal timing — it will snowball rapidly once post-Morophon changeling casts begin.
