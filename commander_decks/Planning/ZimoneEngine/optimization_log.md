# Zimone Engine: Optimization & Budget Log

This document tracks the evolution of Christina's Zimone deck from the initial concept to the final Optimized and Budget versions.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 1: Strategic Optimization
*Goal: Refining the "Feedback Loop" and removing off-plan mechanics.*

| Original Card | Optimized Replacement | Reason for Swap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zimone, Paradox Sculptor** | **Zendikar's Roil** | Manifest Dread felt off-plan. Roil provides consistent 2/2 tokens on every landfall. |
| **Zimone, Mystery Unraveler** | **Case of the Locked Hothouse** | Swapped a clunky manifest trigger for a powerful top-deck engine and extra land plays. |
| **Zimone's Hypothesis** | **Spelunking** | Swapped a one-time draw spell for a permanent speed boost (untapped lands). |
| **Tireless Tracker** | **Six** | Tracker was mana-intensive. Six provides a superior, permanent graveyard recursion engine. |
| **Fact or Fiction** | **Conduit of Worlds** | (In Optimized version) Swapped one-time burst draw for permanent graveyard resilience. |

---

## 💰 Phase 2: Budget Optimization
*Goal: Reduce the $500 price tag while keeping the "Power Spike" cards.*

### 🗺️ The Mana Base (Total Savings: ~$160)
| Premium Card | Budget Replacement | Savings | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boseiju, Who Endures** | **Terramorphic Expanse** | $44.90 | Boseiju is a luxury. Expanse provides double landfall triggers for cents. |
| **Prismatic Vista** | **Evolving Wilds** | $37.88 | Maintains the double-trigger mechanic at a fraction of the cost. |
| **Misty Rainforest** | **Myriad Landscape** | $29.14 | Landscape provides an explosive "triple landfall" turn for $0.25. |
| **Oboro, Palace in the Clouds** | **Trade Routes** | $15.12 | Trade Routes is more flexible; it can return *any* land to hand, not just itself. |
| **Breeding Pool** | **Simic Guildgate** | $12.43 | Standard budget dual land swap. |
| **Rejuvenating Springs** | **Vineglimmer Snarl** | $9.86 | Standard budget dual land swap. |

### 🌿 The Engine & Win-Cons (Total Savings: ~$82)
| Premium Card | Budget Replacement | Savings | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Exploration** | **Growth Spiral** | $31.79 | Growth Spiral provides draw and a trigger at instant speed. |
| **Cyclonic Rift** | **Fact or Fiction** | $29.77 | Rift is a "Game Changer." Restoring Christina's requested FoF fills the graveyard. |
| **Parallel Lives** | **Second Harvest** | $14.15 | Swapped a static doubler for a surprise "Instant" doubling finish. |
| **Greensleeves, Maro-Sorcerer** | **Blighted Woodland** | $13.45 | Swapped a non-essential token maker to fix the deck's land count. |
| **Guardian Project** | **Lifecrafter's Bestiary** | $11.01 | Bestiary provides scry and draw while being much cheaper to acquire. |
| **Reshape the Earth** | **Boundless Realms** | $6.01 | Boundless is easier to cast (7 mana vs 9) and offers similar game-ending power. |

---

## 📊 Final Result
*   **Optimized Version:** ~$500 (Bracket 3). Maximum speed and consistency.
*   **Budget Version:** ~$244 (Bracket 2). Retains **Doubling Season** and **Ancient Greenwarden** as high-impact "Power Spikes" while being over 50% cheaper.
