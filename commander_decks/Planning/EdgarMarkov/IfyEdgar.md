# Deck Guide: Edgar Markov — Ify's List

![Edgar Markov](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/5/8/5831da5c-da05-49b8-8d74-b16d6b3bde62.jpg?1735288696)

## Commander Strategy
**Edgar Markov** (3RWB)
*   **Archetype:** Mardu (W/B/R) Vampire Tribal / Go-Wide Aggro
*   **Bracket:** 2
*   **Source:** Ify Nwadiwe
*   **Core Goal:** Flood the board with Vampires using Edgar's Eminence, grow them with Cathars' Crusade and Metallic Mimic, and close with tribal lords and combat tricks. Exquisite Blood + Sanguine Bond provides a 2-card infinite drain win condition. Lower power and budget than the primary EdgarMarkov list — no tutors, no premium draw engines, basics-heavy mana base.

---

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 👑 Commander (1 Card)
*   **Edgar Markov:** Eminence token engine. Stays in the command zone.

### ⚡ Ramp (4 Cards)
*   **Sol Ring:** Universal accelerant.
*   **Arcane Signet:** Two-mana rock that produces any color in Edgar's identity.
*   **Mardu Banner:** Three-mana rock tapping for R, W, or B. Sacable for a card draw.
*   **Patchwork Banner:** Three-mana rock tapping for any color; also gives +1/+1 to Vampires. Doubles as a tribal lord.

### 📖 Card Advantage (5 Cards)
*   **Florian, Voldaren Scion:** First strike + impulse draw for each 1 damage dealt in combat.
*   **Welcoming Vampire:** Draws a card the first time each turn a creature with power 2 or less enters.
*   **Wedding Announcement:** Over three upkeeps: creates a 1/1 Soldier token, draws a card, then transforms into a +1/+1 anthem for all your creatures.
*   **Markov Purifier:** 2/3 lifelink; at end of step, if you gained life this turn, pay {2} to draw a card.
*   **Baron Bertram Graywater:** Whenever tokens you control enter, create a 1/1 Vampire token with lifelink (once per turn); {1}{B}: sacrifice a creature or artifact to draw a card.

### 🔍 Tutors (0 Cards)
*None in this list.*

### ⚔️ Targeted Disruption (6 Cards)
*   **Path to Exile:** White's best single-target removal.
*   **Go for the Throat:** Destroys any nonartifact creature.
*   **Hero's Downfall:** Instant-speed creature or planeswalker removal.
*   **Infernal Grasp:** Destroy any creature; pay 2 life.
*   **Abrade:** Flexible — 3 damage to a creature or destroy an artifact.
*   **Bound by Moonsilver:** Aura that locks down a creature; can pivot targets by sacrificing another permanent.

### 💥 Mass Disruption (2 Cards)
*   **The Meathook Massacre:** X-cost board wipe; each creature you control dying drains opponents, each opponent creature dying gains you life. Scales from clearing tokens to a full wipe.
*   **Ruinous Ultimatum:** Destroy all nonland permanents opponents control. Backbreaking but costs WWWBBBR.

### 🧛 Vampires — Low Curve (13 Cards)
*   **Voldaren Epicure:** R, 1/1. Deals 1 damage to each opponent on ETB; creates a Blood token.
*   **Restless Bloodseeker:** 1B, 2/1 lifelink. Transforms into Bloodsoaked Reveler (4/1 menace, creates Blood token on attack) when you gain life at end of turn.
*   **Cemetery Gatekeeper:** 1R, 2/1 first strike. Imprints a card type — opponents lose 2 life whenever they play that type.
*   **Vampire Socialite:** 1B, 2/2. Enters with a counter if an opponent lost life this turn; grants entering creatures that counter too.
*   **Scourge of the Undercity:** 1B, 2/1 lifelink. On ETB, gives another creature lifelink until end of turn.
*   **Stromkirk Bloodthief:** 2B, 2/2. At end of step, if an opponent lost life this turn, put a +1/+1 counter on target Vampire.
*   **Bloodtithe Harvester:** BR, 3/2. Creates a Blood token on ETB; tap + sacrifice: target creature gets -X/-X where X is twice your Blood token count.
*   **Arrogant Outlaw:** 2B, 3/2. On ETB, if an opponent lost life this turn, each opponent loses 2 life and you gain 2.
*   **Gluttonous Guest:** 2B, 1/4. Creates a Blood token on ETB; whenever you sacrifice a Blood token, gain 1 life.
*   **Odric, Blood-Cursed:** 1RW, 3/3. On ETB, creates X Blood tokens, where X is the number of distinct keyword abilities among your creatures.
*   **Markov Retribution:** 2R (sorcery). Creatures get +1/+0 until end of turn, or target Vampire deals damage equal to its power to another creature. Flexible tribal combat trick.
*   **Vampire's Kiss:** 1B (sorcery). Target player loses 2 life, you gain 2 life; creates two Blood tokens.
*   **Chromatic Star:** 1 artifact. Sacrifice to add one mana of any color; draws a card when it hits the graveyard.

### 🧛 Vampires — Mid Curve (16 Cards)
*   **Anje, Maid of Dishonor:** 2BR, 4/5. Whenever Anje and/or another Vampire enters, create one Blood token (once per turn). Pay {2} + sacrifice a creature or Blood token: each opponent loses 2 life and you gain 2.
*   **Astarion, the Decadent:** 4WB, 4/4 deathtouch + lifelink. At end of step, choose to have a target opponent lose life equal to life they lost this turn, or you gain life equal to life you gained this turn.
*   **Drana, Liberator of Malakir:** 2B (flying, first strike). When she deals combat damage, put a +1/+1 counter on each attacking creature.
*   **Edgar, Charmed Groom:** 2WB, 3/4 lifelink. At end of step, if you gained life, transform into Edgar Markov's Coffin — an artifact that creates a 1/1 Vampire token each upkeep and transforms back when a Vampire dies.
*   **Florian, Voldaren Scion:** 2BR, 3/2 first strike. Impulse draw on combat damage.
*   **Henrika Domnathi:** 2BB, 1/3. Choose modes at the start of combat — grant flying, sac for draw+life, or deal 2 to self. Choosing all three transforms into Henrika, Infernal Seer (4/5 flying that drains on creature death).
*   **Metallic Mimic:** 2, 2/1. Chooses a creature type (Vampire); each creature of that type entering gets a +1/+1 counter.
*   **Odric, Lunarch Marshal:** 3W, 3/3. At the start of each combat, all your creatures gain every keyword ability held by any creature you control until end of turn.
*   **Sorin the Mirthless:** 2BB, planeswalker. +1: draw a card, lose life equal to its CMC. −2: create a 2/3 Vampire Knight token with flying and lifelink. −7: give all creatures +1/+0 and lifelink until end of turn.
*   **Voldaren Bloodcaster:** 1B, 1/1 flying. Creates a Blood token on ETB; transforms into Bloodbat Summoner (3/3 flying that creates 2/2 Bat tokens) when you've sacrificed enough Blood tokens.
*   **Bloodline Keeper:** 3B, 3/3 flying. Creates a 2/2 Vampire token at end of turn; transforms into Lord of Lineage (all Vampires get +2/+2) when you control 5+ Vampires.
*   **Anowon, the Ruin Sage:** 3B, 3/3. At upkeep, each opponent sacrifices a non-Vampire. Asymmetric attrition that snowballs.
*   **Liesa, Forgotten Archangel:** 2WWB, 4/5 flying lifelink. Nontoken creatures you control that die return to hand at end of turn; opponents' creatures that die are exiled instead.
*   **Thalia, Heretic Cathar:** 2W, 3/2 first strike. Opponent creatures and nonbasic lands enter tapped.
*   **Necropolis Regent:** 3BBB, 6/5 flying. Whenever a creature you control deals combat damage to a player, put that many +1/+1 counters on it.
*   **Olivia, Crimson Bride:** 4BR, 6/5 flying haste. Attacking trigger: reanimate a Vampire from your graveyard (it gains haste; exiled if Olivia leaves).

### 🎯 Tribal Payoffs & Finishers (9 Cards)
*   **Cathars' Crusade:** 3W enchantment. Whenever a creature enters under your control, put a +1/+1 counter on each creature you control. With Eminence, each Vampire cast creates two entering creatures → two triggers → +2 counters on everything.
*   **Intangible Virtue:** 1W enchantment. Creature tokens get +1/+1 and vigilance.
*   **Stensia Masquerade:** 2R enchantment. Attacking creatures have first strike. Whenever a Vampire deals combat damage to a player, it gets a +1/+1 counter. Madness {2}{R}.
*   **Thirsting Bloodlord:** 2BB, 3/3. Other Vampires you control get +1/+1.
*   **All-Out Assault:** 2RWB enchantment. Creatures get +1/+1 and deathtouch. On ETB during your main phase, creates an additional combat phase (and untaps attackers before it).
*   **Windcrag Siege (Mardu mode):** 1RW enchantment. Choose Mardu: attacking-creature-triggered abilities trigger an additional time. Edgar's on-attack counter pump triggers twice; Sanctum Seeker and Sanctum Seeker-like drains double.
*   **Mass Hysteria:** R enchantment. All creatures have haste. Note: gives opponents' creatures haste too — use carefully.
*   **Exquisite Blood:** 4B enchantment. Whenever an opponent loses life, you gain that much life. Half of the 2-card infinite drain combo.
*   **Sanguine Bond:** 3BB enchantment. Whenever you gain life, target opponent loses that much life. The other half of the Exquisite Blood infinite.

### 🗺️ Lands (39 Cards)
*   **Command Tower:** Any color in Edgar's identity.
*   **Shattered Sanctum:** W/B slow land; enters untapped if you control a Plains or Swamp, otherwise pays 2 life.
*   **Vault of Champions:** W/B partner land; enters untapped in multiplayer.
*   **Inspiring Vantage:** W/R fast land; enters untapped if you control two or fewer other lands.
*   **Haunted Ridge:** B/R slow land; enters untapped if you control a Swamp or Mountain.
*   **Dragonskull Summit:** B/R check land; enters untapped if you control a Swamp or Mountain.
*   **Sundown Pass:** W/R slow land; enters untapped if you control a Plains or Mountain.
*   **Exotic Orchard:** Produces any color an opponent's land produces.
*   **Voldaren Estate:** Colorless + life for Vampire spells; late game creates Blood tokens at reduced cost.
*   **Nomad Outpost:** W/B/R tri-land; enters tapped.
*   **Restless Bivouac:** W/R land; enters tapped; animates into a 2/2 Ox on attack.
*   **Restless Fortress:** W/B land; enters tapped; animates into a 1/4 Nightmare that drains on attack.
*   **Akoum Refuge:** B/R gain land; enters tapped, gains 1 life.
*   **Scoured Barrens:** W/B gain land; enters tapped, gains 1 life.
*   **Wind-Scarred Crag:** W/R gain land; enters tapped, gains 1 life.
*   **Mountain ×8 / Plains ×8 / Swamp ×7:** Basics (23 total).
