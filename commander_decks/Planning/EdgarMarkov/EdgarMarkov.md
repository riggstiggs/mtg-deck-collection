# Deck Guide: Edgar Markov (Vampire Tribal)

![Edgar Markov](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/5/8/5831da5c-da05-49b8-8d74-b16d6b3bde62.jpg?1735288696)

## Commander Strategy
**Edgar Markov** (3RWB)
*   **Archetype:** Mardu (W/B/R) Vampire Tribal / Go-Wide Aggro-Drain
*   **Bracket:** 3
*   **Core Goal:** Leverage Edgar's Eminence to flood the board with 1/1 Vampire tokens every time you cast a Vampire, then win through combat backed by lord effects, Shared Animosity, and an incidental drain engine (Vito + Exquisite Blood, Sanctum Seeker, Blood Artist). The deck rarely needs to hard-cast Edgar — the 11-mana rate is almost never relevant.

### Key Notes
*   **3 Game Changers:** Vampiric Tutor, Necropotence, and Emeritus of Woe // Demonic Tutor. This is the Bracket 3 maximum — do not add a fourth without changing the bracket.
*   **2-Card Infinite:** Exquisite Blood + Vito, Thorn of the Dusk Rose create infinite life drain once any life is gained. Intentional combo package — acceptable at Bracket 3 as a late-game win line.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 👑 Commander (1 Card)
*   **Edgar Markov:** Passive token factory from the command zone; the +1/+1 counter on-attack is a secondary bonus. Plan is to cast cheap Vampires repeatedly and never pay the commander tax.

### ⚡ Ramp (2 Cards)
*   **Sol Ring:** Universal accelerant.
*   **Master of Dark Rites:** One-mana 1/1 that taps + sacrifices another creature to add BBB, spendable only on Vampire/Cleric/Demon spells. Bridges into a second wave of Vampires.

### 📖 Card Advantage (9 Cards)
*   **Skullclamp:** Edgar constantly makes 1/1 tokens — equip to a token, draw two cards, repeat. The most efficient draw engine in the deck.
*   **Night's Whisper:** Simple two-for-one.
*   **Dusk Legion Zealot:** 1/1 Vampire that replaces itself on ETB. Double-dips with Eminence.
*   **Vampire Gourmand:** Sacrifice another creature to draw a card and make this unblockable. Excellent with token fodder; activates Falkenrath Pit Fighter drain trigger as a bonus.
*   **Welcoming Vampire:** Draws a card the first time each turn a creature with power 2 or less enters. The 1/1 Eminence tokens keep this firing.
*   **Florian, Voldaren Scion:** First strike + impulse draw for each 1 damage dealt in combat. In a wide attack, this could draw 3–5 cards.
*   **Black Market Connections:** Three modes (token, card, mana) — the token and draw modes are default choices; the mana mode smooths tight hands.
*   **Necropotence** *(Game Changer)*: Skip your draw step, pay life to stock your hand. Extremely powerful with the life gain the deck generates.
*   **Twilight Prophet:** 3/4 flier that triggers Ascend (10+ permanents) easily given the token flood; at 10 permanents, reveals top card each upkeep, drains opponents for its CMC, and draws you the card.

### 🔍 Tutors (4 Cards)
*   **Vampiric Tutor** *(Game Changer)*: One-mana instant-speed tutor to top of library. Finds Exquisite Blood, Necropotence, or whatever the board needs.
*   **Diabolic Intent:** Sacrifice a token, tutor any card. Efficient and on-theme.
*   **Insatiable Avarice:** Flexible Spree sorcery — cheapest mode tutors to top of library (B+2); adding BB draws you three cards and drains 3. Both modes for 3BBB if needed.
*   **Forerunner of the Legion:** ETB tutors any Vampire to top of library; every Vampire entering from the library while Forerunner is in play gets a +1/+1 counter.

### ⚔️ Targeted Disruption (7 Cards)
*   **Path to Exile:** White's cleanest answer. The land-fetch drawback matters little in a deck racing to kill quickly.
*   **Swords to Plowshares:** Better than Path in most cases — don't care about opponent's life total in this deck.
*   **Despark:** Efficient removal for permanents with CMC 4+; hits commanders and most threats.
*   **Fracture:** Instant-speed permanent destruction for any color of permanent. Hits artifacts and enchantments too.
*   **Rite of Oblivion:** Sacrifice an artifact or creature (tokens!) to exile any nonland permanent. Great for problem enchantments.
*   **Ruthless Lawbringer:** On ETB, sacrifice another creature to destroy any nonland permanent. Repeatable threat removal on a Vampire body.
*   **Lethal Scheme:** Convoke instant that destroys a creature or planeswalker; creatures that convoked connive. Tap tokens to pay the cost and grow your board in the process.

### 💥 Mass Disruption (5 Cards)
*   **Winds of Abandon:** Path to Exile for all creatures you don't control — gives opponents basic lands but clears the board cleanly. White mana lets you turn off the land grant (for 1WW to path a single attacker).
*   **Crippling Fear:** Choose Vampire; all non-Vampires get -3/-3 until end of turn. Near-wrath for opponents; your team survives.
*   **Olivia's Wrath:** Destroy all non-Vampires. Hard-wrath for everything except your team.
*   **Taunt from the Rampart:** Goad all opponent creatures and make them unable to block until your next turn. Excellent before an alpha strike — they must attack each other and cannot block you.
*   **Everything Comes to Dust:** Convoke sorcery — exile all creatures except those sharing a type with a convoked creature, plus exile all artifacts and enchantments. With Vampires convoked, this exiles all non-Vampires, all artifacts, and all enchantments. The hardest reset in the deck at minimal cost.

### 🧛 Vampires — 1-Drops (5 Cards)
*   **Changeling Outcast:** B, 1/1, all creature types (including Vampire) — unblockable Vampire that can't be the target of spells or abilities. Triggers Eminence, gets token, slides through defenses.
*   **Vampire of the Dire Moon:** B, 1/1 deathtouch + lifelink. One mana, threatens blocks, gains life.
*   **Indulgent Aristocrat:** B, 1/1. Sacrifice a Vampire or token to put a +1/+1 counter on every Vampire you control. Scales absurdly with a wide board.
*   **Shadow Alley Denizen:** B, 1/1. Whenever another black creature enters, all black creatures gain intimidate until end of turn. Most of your Vampires are black — near-unblockable attack triggers.
*   **Falkenrath Pit Fighter:** R, 2/1. Activated ability: {1}{R}, discard a card, sacrifice a Vampire → draw two cards, activatable only when an opponent lost life this turn. With constant drain, this is almost always online.

### 🧛 Vampires — 2-Drops (11 Cards)
*   **Gifted Aetherborn:** 1B, 2/3 deathtouch + lifelink. A roadblock that gains life and enables Vito/Marauding Blight-Priest.
*   **Blood Artist:** 1B, 0/1. Whenever a creature dies (any creature), drain each opponent 1 and gain 1. Exceptional with the token sacrifice loops and board wipes.
*   **Cemetery Gatekeeper:** 1R, 2/1 first strike. Imprints a card type on ETB; opponents who play permanents of that type lose 2 life. Target lands to punish every land drop; combines with Urborg/Blood Crypt to ensure maximum coverage.
*   **Cordial Vampire:** 1B, 1/1. Whenever a creature dies, put a +1/+1 counter on every Vampire you control. Turns board wipes and sacrifice into board growth.
*   **Creeping Bloodsucker:** 1B, 1/2. At the beginning of your upkeep, deals 1 damage to each opponent and you gain that much life. In a 4-player pod, 3 damage + 3 life per turn — triggers Vito and Marauding Blight-Priest.
*   **Cruel Celebrant:** WB, 1/2. Whenever a creature or planeswalker you control dies, drain each opponent 1. Pairs with Indulgent Aristocrat and Blood Artist for a layered drain circuit.
*   **Ivora, Insatiable Heir:** 1R, 1/1 trample. Creates a Blood token on ETB and whenever she deals combat damage to a player. Grows with discards — pairs with Necropotence's end-step exile.
*   **Legion Lieutenant:** WB, 2/2. All other Vampires you control get +1/+1. One of the most efficient lords in the format at 2 mana.
*   **Vampire Socialite:** 1B, 2/2. Enters with a counter if an opponent lost life this turn (easy to ensure). If she has a counter, other Vampires entering get a counter too. Wide Eminence entry + Socialite = every new token arrives with a counter.
*   **Blood Seeker:** 1B, 1/1. Whenever a creature enters the battlefield under an opponent's control, that player loses 1 life. Punishes token strategies and creature ramp.
*   **Charismatic Conqueror:** 1W, 2/2 vigilance. Whenever an artifact or creature an opponent controls enters untapped, they may tap it. If they don't, you get a 1/1 Vampire token with lifelink. Forces opponents to tap down or give you free tokens.

### 🧛 Vampires — 3-Drops (8 Cards)
*   **Captivating Vampire:** 2B, 2/2. Other Vampires you control get +1/+1. Tap five Vampires: gain control of target creature, which becomes a Vampire. Anthem + board-state steal as a win condition.
*   **Marauding Blight-Priest:** 2B, 2/3. Whenever you gain life, each opponent loses 1 life. In this deck, life is gained constantly — this is a continuous drain multiplier.
*   **Preacher of the Schism:** 2B, 2/4 deathtouch. Attacking the player with most life creates a 1/1 Vampire token with lifelink. Attacking while YOU have most life draws a card and costs 1 life. Both modes are frequently active.
*   **Markov Baron:** 2B, 2/2 lifelink with Convoke and Madness. Static +1/+1 to all other Vampires you control. Can be cast for free by tapping Vampires; Madness cost of {2}{B} lets you cast it if discarded.
*   **Vito, Thorn of the Dusk Rose:** 2B, 1/3. Whenever you gain life, each opponent loses that much life. Half of the Exquisite Blood infinite — also just strong standalone drain doubling.
*   **Sanctum Seeker:** 3B, 3/4. Whenever a Vampire you control attacks, drain each opponent 1 and gain 1. With 10+ Vampires attacking, this is 10+ life lost per opponent per combat.
*   **Bloodletter of Aclazotz:** 3B, 2/4 flying. Each opponent's life loss from noncombat sources is doubled. Combined with Creeping Bloodsucker, Vito, Marauding Blight-Priest, and Sanctum Seeker, this turns constant small drains into lethal game-ending damage.
*   **Bloodline Keeper:** 3B, 3/3 flying. Creates a 2/2 Vampire token at end of turn; transforms when you control 5+ Vampires into Lord of Lineage (3/3 → 5/5, all Vampires get +2/+2). A reliable token generator that self-transforms.

### 🧛 Vampires — Top End (4 Cards)
*   **Vampire Nocturnus:** 3B, 3/3. If the top card of your library is black, all Vampires get +2/+1 and flying. With ~35 black-bordered cards in the deck, this is consistently active and often provides +2/+1 + flying to the whole team.
*   **Bloodthirsty Conqueror:** 3BB, 5/5 flying deathtouch. Whenever an opponent loses life, you gain that much life. An engine that turns all the opponent life loss into your own life gain, which re-triggers Vito and Marauding Blight-Priest.
*   **Emeritus of Woe // Demonic Tutor** *(Game Changer)*: {3}{B} — 5/4 Vampire Warlock creature (enters from the adventure exile zone). Adventure side: {1}{B} Demonic Tutor (search your library for any card, put it in your hand). A Vampire body stapled to the best tutor in Magic.
*   **Agadeem's Awakening // Agadeem, the Undercrypt:** MDFC — plays as a tapped B-producing land when needed early, or as an {X}{B}{B}{B} sorcery that reanimates up to X Vampire/Zombie creatures from the graveyard with CMC ≤ X. Late game, this refills the board from a wipe.

### 🎯 Tribal Payoffs & Finishers (5 Cards)
*   **Shared Animosity:** 2R enchantment. Each attacking creature of a shared type gets +1/+1 for each other attacking creature of that type. With 10 Vampires attacking, each gets +9. Turns a mediocre board into a lethal alpha strike.
*   **Banner of Kinship:** 5 artifact. Enters with fellowship counters equal to the number of Vampires you control. All Vampires you control get +1/+1 for each counter. Played when wide, this can provide +8 to +15 to the entire team.
*   **Akroma's Will:** 3W instant. Choose from four modes — give creatures flying, first strike, vigilance, and double strike. Typically cast during your combat step to one-shot the table.
*   **Cover of Darkness:** 1B enchantment. Choose Vampire — all Vampires have fear (only blockable by artifact creatures or black creatures). Near-unblockable evasion for the whole team.
*   **Exquisite Blood:** 4B enchantment. Whenever an opponent loses life, you gain that much life. Infinite combo with Vito; also just strong standalone life gain that fuels Necropotence draws.

### 🗺️ Lands (39 Cards)
*   **Command Tower:** Produces any color in Edgar's identity.
*   **Savai Triome:** W/B/R triome; fetchable.
*   **Sacred Foundry / Godless Shrine / Blood Crypt:** The three Mardu shock lands.
*   **Arid Mesa / Bloodstained Mire / Marsh Flats / Prismatic Vista:** Fetchlands that thin the deck and fix mana.
*   **Luxury Suite / Spectator Seating / Vault of Champions:** Partner-land cycle (Battlebond/Commander Legends) — typically enter untapped in multiplayer.
*   **Bleachbone Verge / Blazemire Verge / Sunbillow Verge:** Duskmourn/Aetherdrift Verge lands (W/B, B/R, W/R) — each taps for either color if you control the right land type.
*   **Shattered Sanctum / Silent Clearing / Sunbaked Canyon:** Spell-land cycle (W/B, W/B, W/R) — come in tapped or pay 2 life to enter untapped.
*   **Haunted Ridge / Sulfurous Springs / Sundown Pass / Battlefield Forge / Caves of Koilos:** Allied and enemy painlands covering B/R, W/R, W/B color pairs.
*   **Castle Locthwain:** B land that draws a card for 1BB when your hand is empty — late-game refuel.
*   **Exotic Orchard / Reflecting Pool / Mana Confluence:** Multi-color fixing; Confluence pays life for any color.
*   **Voldaren Estate:** Taps colorless or pays 1 life for any color (Vampire spells only); late game creates Blood tokens at reduced cost with many Vampires.
*   **Three Tree City:** Legendary land — choose Vampire; {2}{T} adds mana in any color equal to the number of Vampires you control. Explosive mana with a full board.
*   **Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth:** All lands tap for B. Fixes black mana across every land in the deck.
*   **Takenuma, Abandoned Mire:** MDFC Kamigawa channel land — pays 1B and sacrifices itself to mill 3 and return a legendary creature from your graveyard. Recovers a lost Edgar or key Vampire.
*   **Midgar, City of Mako:** Final Fantasy Universes Beyond MDFC — land side enters tapped; adventure side costs 2B for a sorcery effect.
*   **Minas Tirith:** LOTR Universes Beyond land — enters tapped, produces W, has a triggered ability.
*   **Cori Mountain Monastery:** Tarkir: Dragonstorm land — enters untapped if you control a Plains; taps for R; 3R to exile and play the top card of your library.
*   **Mountain / Plains / Swamp (×3):** Basic land package for fetch targets.

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
*   **Anointed Procession (3W):** Doubles every Eminence token. With the existing token-flooding strategy this is a near-must-include.
*   **Sorin, Lord of Innistrad / Sorin the Mirthless:** Planeswalkers with Vampire synergy — token generation and anthem effects.
*   **Reconnaissance (W):** Untap attackers after blockers are declared; lets you attack freely without fear of losing creatures while still triggering Sanctum Seeker drains.
*   **Cathar's Crusade (3WW):** Puts a +1/+1 counter on every creature you control when any creature enters. Obscene with Eminence token floods.
