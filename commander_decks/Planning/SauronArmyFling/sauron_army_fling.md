# Deck Guide: Sauron, the Dark Lord (Army & Fling Strategy)

## 🏹 Commander Strategy
**Sauron, the Dark Lord**
*   **Archetype:** Grixis Spellslinging / Amass / Fling
*   **Core Goal:** Utilize the high density of "Discard to Draw" spells (Rituals and Looting) to exponentially grow your Orc Army via **Saruman, the White Hand**. Once the Army is massive, use "Fling" effects like **Chandra's Ignition**, **Soul's Fire**, or **Gravitic Punch** to deal lethal damage to the entire table.

### 🏹 The "Fling" Win Condition
1.  **Chandra's Ignition:** Turns your massive Army into a board wipe that deals damage to each opponent.
2.  **Soul's Fire / Gravitic Punch:** Direct damage from the Army to a player's face.
3.  **Kess, Dissident Mage / Mission Briefing:** Provides the ability to re-cast these spells from the graveyard for a second attempt.

## 🛠️ Notable Synergies (Army & Fling)
*   **Mindsplice Apparatus:** Rapidly reduces the cost of your 20+ instants and sorceries, allowing for massive turns.
*   **Widespread Brutality:** A board wipe that scales with the size of your Army.
*   **Artful Dodge:** A cheap way to ensure your Army can connect for damage *before* you fling it.
*   **Galvanic Iteration:** Double your "Fling" spell for a guaranteed kill.
