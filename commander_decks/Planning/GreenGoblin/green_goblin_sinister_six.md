# Deck Guide: Norman Osborn / Green Goblin — "The Sinister Six"

![Norman Osborn / Green Goblin](https://cards.scryfall.io/normal/front/d/5/d5c53af9-7150-4e78-8771-2de7980aa307.jpg?1757433048)

## Commander Strategy
**Norman Osborn / Green Goblin**
*   **Archetype:** Grixis (Blue/Black/Red) Wheels / Discard Madness / Graveyard Value
*   **Bracket:** 3 (2 Game Changers: Cyclonic Rift, Fierce Guardianship)
*   **Core Goal:** Norman Osborn (1/1 {1}{U}) attacks unblocked to connive and fill the graveyard, then transforms into Green Goblin (3/3 flying/menace). Green Goblin's **Goblin Formula** gives every nonland card in your graveyard **Mayhem** — you may cast any card discarded *this turn* from your graveyard for its mana cost, reduced by {2} from Green Goblin's passive. Wheel an entire hand of 6–7 cards with Green Goblin in play, then recast every one of them from the graveyard at a steep discount in the same turn. Every graveyard cast triggers Secrets of the Dead, River Kelpie, Burning Vengeance, and Chainer Nightmare Adept for cascading damage and draws. A premium interaction suite (Mana Drain, Fierce Guardianship, Deflecting Swat, Cyclonic Rift) protects the engine until it goes off.

### 🧨 The Goblin Formula — Core Engine
The deck's primary combo line:
1.  **Deploy discard payoffs** (Bone Miser, Glint-Horn Buccaneer, Monument to Endurance) before wheeling.
2.  **Transform Norman into Green Goblin** — costs {1}{U}{B}{R} at sorcery speed.
3.  **Wheel** (Windfall, Tolarian Winds, etc.) — discard 5–7 cards. Each discard fires every payoff simultaneously.
4.  **Mayhem** — with Green Goblin on board, every card you discarded this turn can be recast from the graveyard for its mana cost minus {2}. A 4-mana sorcery becomes a 2-mana sorcery. A 2-mana instant is free.
5.  **Each graveyard cast** triggers Burning Vengeance (2 damage), Secrets of the Dead (draw), River Kelpie (draw), and Chainer (free hasted creature).

### 🏎️ How to Pilot the Sinister Six
1.  **Early (Turns 1–3):** Ramp with Sol Ring, Talismans, Dark Ritual. Land one discard payoff before Norman. Attack with Norman whenever possible — each unblocked swing connives, filtering toward combo pieces and growing him.
2.  **Mid (Turns 4–6):** Transform Norman into Green Goblin when payoffs are set up. Hold up Fierce Guardianship or Mana Drain. Begin smaller discard loops (Faithless Looting, Demand Answers) to fill the graveyard.
3.  **Late (Turn 7+):** Drop Windfall or Tolarian Winds into the Goblin Formula. Recast the discarded hand at discount. Doctor Octopus refills to 8 cards at your end step for another Mayhem cycle next turn.

## 🛠️ Card Explanations

### 🕷️ The Spider-Man Villain Package (6 Cards)
*Thematic Spider-Man Universes Beyond cards that drive and reward the discard engine.*

*   **Green Goblin, Nemesis:** 3/3 flying for {2}{B}{R}. Whenever you discard a nonland card, put a +1/+1 counter on target Goblin you control. Whenever you discard a land, create a tapped Treasure token. A standalone discard payoff that grows Norman/Green Goblin through nonland discards and generates Treasure from land discards — even wheel cards you'd normally consider "wasted" (lands) become Treasure here.
*   **Behold the Sinister Six!:** {6}{B} sorcery. Return up to six target creature cards with different names from your graveyard to the battlefield. The ultimate late-game payoff — after wheels load the graveyard with every creature in the deck, Behold the Sinister Six resurrects six of them simultaneously. With Anger in the graveyard, they all enter with haste.
*   **Carnage, Crimson Chaos:** 4/3 trample for {2}{B}{R}. ETB — return target creature card with mana value 3 or less from your graveyard to the battlefield; that creature gains "attacks each combat if able" and "when it deals combat damage to a player, sacrifice it." Mayhem {B}{R}. Reanimates a 3-or-less MV threat that self-sacrifices on damage — every combat trigger refuels the graveyard for more Mayhem casts. Serves as a target for its own ETB to create recursive sacrifice loops.
*   **Doctor Octopus, Master Planner:** 4/8 for {5}{U}{B}. Other Villains you control get +2/+2. Your maximum hand size is eight. At the beginning of your end step, draw cards until you have eight. A Villain lord and a permanent Reliquary Tower effect combined — after a wheel empties your hand, Doc Ock refills it to eight at end step, providing a full hand for the next Mayhem cycle. Norman Osborn and Green Goblin are Villains.
*   **Electro, Assaulting Battery:** 2/3 flying for {1}{R}{R}. You don't lose unspent red mana as steps and phases end. Whenever you cast an instant or sorcery, add {R}. When Electro leaves the battlefield, you may pay {X} — if you do, he deals X damage to target player. An Omnath for red mana that stockpiles {R} across every spell you cast. Each wheel spell and Mayhem recast generates additional {R} into the pool, then converts that mana into a damage burst on exit.
*   **Pumpkin Bombs:** {1}{R} artifact. {T}, Discard two cards: Draw three cards, then put a fuse counter on Pumpkin Bombs. It deals damage equal to the number of fuse counters to target opponent. They gain control of it. Net +1 card per activation with escalating ping damage — each use draws ahead, discards for discard payoffs, and deals more damage than the last. The opponent receiving it eventually has to decide whether to activate it themselves or pass it back.

### 🌀 Wheel & Discard Engine (9 Cards)
*The primary fuel source — spells that cycle the entire hand into the graveyard, triggering every payoff and loading Mayhem targets.*

*   **Windfall:** {2}{U} sorcery. Each player discards their hand then draws cards equal to the greatest number discarded. The premier wheel in Commander — with three opponents, Windfall draws 5–7 cards while triggering every discard payoff simultaneously. With Green Goblin in play, every card discarded immediately becomes a Mayhem target.
*   **Faithless Looting:** {R} sorcery — draw 2, discard 2. Flashback {2}{R}. The cheapest targeted discard in the deck. Flashback fires two separate sets of discard triggers across two turns — both uses load the graveyard for Chainer and Mayhem.
*   **Frantic Search:** {2}{U} instant — draw 2, discard 2, untap up to three lands. The untap effect makes this effectively free or mana-positive mid-game. Instant-speed, so it fires on opponents' end steps going into your Mayhem turn.
*   **Tolarian Winds:** {U} sorcery — discard your hand, then draw that many cards. A one-mana wheel that discards the entire hand at once. The single highest discard count per mana spent — a 7-card hand becomes 7 simultaneous discard triggers for {U}.
*   **Demand Answers:** {1}{R} instant — discard a card, draw 2. Cheap impulse that trades one discard trigger for net +1 card. Every payoff fires on the discard and the draw replaces the card.
*   **Unexpected Windfall:** {2}{R}{R} instant — discard a card, draw 2, create two Treasure tokens. Instant-speed Treasure generation on top of a discard and +1 draw. The two Treasures often fund the next Mayhem recast immediately.
*   **Big Score:** {4}{R} sorcery — discard a card, draw 2, create two Treasure tokens. The sorcery-speed sibling to Unexpected Windfall. The higher cost is offset by two immediate Treasures and a net +1 draw.
*   **Will of the Jeskai:** {3}{R} sorcery. Choose one. If you control a commander, you may choose both instead — • Each player may discard their hand and draw five cards. • Each instant and sorcery in your graveyard gains flashback until end of turn with mana cost equal to its mana cost. With the commander on board, this wheels AND grants flashback to every instant and sorcery in the graveyard simultaneously — a combined Windfall plus Yawgmoth's Will for spells on a single card.
*   **Into the Flood Maw:** {U} instant. Gift a tapped Fish (opponent creates a 1/1 Fish token). Return target creature an opponent controls to hand. If gift was promised, return any nonland permanent instead. One-mana bounce — gifting the Fish upgrades it to bounce any permanent, including enchantments and artifacts that can't otherwise be answered in Grixis. The Fish token is irrelevant at this power level.

### ⚡ Discard Payoffs (8 Cards)
*Permanents that convert every discard trigger into damage, Treasure, zombie tokens, or card advantage — the engine that makes wheels lethal before Mayhem even resolves.*

*   **Bone Miser:** {4}{B} creature. Whenever you discard a land, create a Swamp token. Whenever you discard a nonland, create a Treasure. Whenever you discard a creature, create a 2/2 Zombie. A Windfall discarding a mixed hand generates Treasures, Zombie tokens, and Swamps simultaneously — funding the Mayhem recasts in the same turn.
*   **Glint-Horn Buccaneer:** {2}{R}{R} creature. Whenever you discard a card, deal 1 damage to each opponent. Has haste and can tap to draw-discard. Each card discarded in a wheel pings all three opponents — Windfall discarding five cards deals 5 to each for 15 total damage. Haste means it fires the turn it enters.
*   **Brallin, Skyshark Rider:** {3}{R} creature. Whenever you discard a card, put a +1/+1 counter on Brallin and deal 1 damage to each opponent. A secondary Glint-Horn that also grows throughout the game — each discard makes it bigger and pings. After multiple wheels, Brallin becomes a serious combat threat on top of the passive damage.
*   **Burning Vengeance:** {2}{R} enchantment. Whenever you cast an instant or sorcery from your graveyard, deal 2 damage to any target. With Faithless Looting's flashback, Frantic Search into Mayhem recasts, and Will of the Jeskai's flashback mode, Burning Vengeance triggers repeatedly in the same turn — 2 damage per graveyard cast adds up to lethal across a full Mayhem chain.
*   **Monument to Endurance:** {3} artifact. Whenever you discard a card, choose one that hasn't been chosen this turn — draw a card; create a Treasure token; each opponent loses 3 life. The three modes rotate — the first discard draws, the second makes Treasure, the third pings 3 life from everyone. Resets each turn, meaning the first three discards always cycle through all three modes. Extraordinarily efficient in any wheel turn.
*   **Ghostly Pilferer:** {1}{U} creature. Whenever an opponent draws a card (except the first each turn), you may pay {1}{U} to draw a card. Whenever it's discarded, draw a card. Built-in safety net — if it's wheeled away you immediately draw a replacement. Generates passive card draw off opponent draw spells and triggers, and punishes Windfall-style symmetrical wheels.
*   **Surly Badgersaur:** {2}{R} creature. Whenever you discard a land, create a 2/2 Bear token. Whenever you discard a nonland, Surly Badgersaur deals 2 damage to any target. A Bone Miser variant focused on damage output — each nonland discarded deals 2 damage anywhere, distributing lethal damage across opponents over a sequence of wheels.
*   **Archfiend of Ifnir:** {3}{B}{B} creature with flying and cycling {2}{B}. Whenever you discard a card, put a -1/-1 counter on each creature an opponent controls. A wheel that discards five cards places five -1/-1 counters on every opposing creature — wipes tokens and small creatures entirely, and cripples mid-size threats. The cycling cost also triggers the ability.

### 🪦 Graveyard Synergies & Reanimation (7 Cards)
*Cards that reward a graveyard full of discarded threats or return them to the battlefield — the secondary engine that converts wheels into permanent board presence.*

*   **Chainer, Nightmare Adept:** {2}{B}{R} creature. Creatures you cast from your graveyard have haste. Once per turn when you discard a card, you may cast a creature from your graveyard. A one-card engine that converts every discard trigger into a free haste creature from the graveyard — and works in combination with Green Goblin's Mayhem to let you cast creatures and non-creatures both from the GY in the same turn. Graveyard creature cast also triggers Burning Vengeance if Chainer is somehow copied.
*   **Acererak the Archlich:** {2}{B} creature. When it ETBs, venture into the dungeon. Returns to hand unless all dungeons are completed. Repeatedly enters the battlefield, venturing deeper each time — Dungeon of the Mad Mage draws cards and creates Zombies before completion, then resets. Chains with the Mayhem engine since you can discard Acererak in a wheel and immediately recast it via Mayhem for {0} (costs {2}{B} minus {2}).
*   **Bringer of the Last Gift:** {6}{B}{B} 6/6 flying. When it enters *if cast*, each player sacrifices all other creatures they control, then each player returns all creature cards from their graveyard that weren't put there this way to the battlefield. An ETB board wipe followed by mass symmetric reanimation — your graveyard full of wheeled creatures all return simultaneously while opponents' boards are cleared. The sacrifice-first clause means your already-dead creatures (already in GY) survive the wipe and come back.
*   **River Kelpie:** {3}{U}{U} 3/3 with persist. Whenever any permanent enters from a graveyard, draw a card. Whenever any player casts a spell from a graveyard, draw a card. Triggers on any graveyard interaction — opponent's Animate Dead, your Chainer activation, your Mayhem casts. With persist, River Kelpie returns from its own death with a -1/-1 counter, drawing a card on re-entry. The backbone of the Mayhem draw engine.
*   **Secrets of the Dead:** {2}{U} enchantment. Whenever you cast a spell from a graveyard, draw a card. A second River Kelpie effect on an enchantment — each Mayhem cast or flashback activation draws an additional card, turning a full Mayhem chain into a massive refuel.
*   **Animate Dead:** {1}{B} enchantment aura. Return target creature card from any graveyard to the battlefield attached to Animate Dead. Two-mana reanimation of any creature from any graveyard — steals the best creature discarded from an opponent's wheel or returns your biggest discarded threat.
*   **Reanimate:** {B} sorcery. Put target creature from any graveyard onto the battlefield under your control; lose life equal to its converted mana cost. One-mana reanimation at the cost of life. With wheels loading every graveyard, Reanimate steals the best creature from any opponent's graveyard for one black mana.

### 🎭 Value Creatures & Draw Engines (10 Cards)
*Creatures that generate ongoing mana, card advantage, or additional discard triggers throughout the game.*

*   **Birgi, God of Storytelling / Harnfel, Horn of Bounty:** {2}{R}. Birgi — whenever you cast a spell, add {R}. Harnfel (back face) — discard a card to exile the top two cards of your library and cast them this turn. Birgi reduces the effective cost of every Mayhem recast and wheel spell. Harnfel flips the discard economy entirely — discards convert to impulsive draws from the top of the library without needing Green Goblin in play.
*   **Neheb, Dreadhorde Champion:** {3}{R} 5/4. Whenever Neheb deals combat damage to a player, discard any number of cards and draw that many. Add {R} to your mana pool for each card discarded this way. An attacking wheel on a 5/4 body — connecting Neheb draws a fresh hand, generates red mana equal to the hand size discarded, and triggers every discard payoff simultaneously. Anger in the graveyard gives it haste on entry.
*   **Vohar, Vodalian Desecrator:** {U}{B} legendary. ETB — mill two. {1}{U/B}: Exile an instant or sorcery from your graveyard, then cast a copy of it. Repeat-copies of Windfall, Will of the Jeskai, or Faithless Looting for {1}{U/B} — each activation re-fires an entire wheel chain from the graveyard without discarding the original.
*   **Rona, Herald of Invasion / Rona, Tolarian Obliterator:** {1}{U}. Herald side — whenever you cast a legendary spell, draw then discard. Tap to draw then discard. Fires a discard trigger on every legendary cast (Norman, Doc Ock, Neheb, Carnage, etc.) and as a free tap activation each turn — a reliable per-turn discard payoff that costs nothing beyond tapping.
*   **Likeness Looter:** {1}{U} creature. {1}{U}: Discard a card and draw a card. Can become a copy of a creature you discarded for {2}{U}{U}. Controlled on-demand discard triggers — activate before a Mayhem window to charge Glint-Horn, Bone Miser, and Monument to Endurance at will. The copy mode reanimates a discarded bomb as a permanent copy for {2}{U}{U}.
*   **Psychic Frog:** {U}{B} 1/1 flying. Whenever you discard a card, put a +1/+1 counter on Psychic Frog and you may draw a card. Grows with every discard while potentially replacing the discarded card — across a game of wheels, becomes a massive evasive threat that also fuels card draw.
*   **Bog Witch:** {2}{B} creature. {T}, Discard a card: Add {B}{B}{B}. Sacrifice to add {B}{B}{B}. Converts a discard into three black mana — activating Bog Witch before wheeling generates the mana to immediately cast Reanimate or Animate Dead on whatever hits the graveyard that same turn.
*   **Skirge Familiar:** {4}{B} creature. Whenever you discard a card, add {B}. A persistent mana engine — discarding five cards in a Windfall generates five black mana on the spot, often funding immediate Behold the Sinister Six! or Bringer of the Last Gift casts.
*   **Anger:** {3}{R} creature. As long as Anger is in your graveyard and you control a Mountain, all creatures you control have haste. The silent haste enabler — discard or mill Anger into the graveyard once and every creature you control or reanimate gains haste permanently. Essential for making Behold the Sinister Six! immediately lethal.
*   **High Fae Trickster:** {3}{U} 4/2. Flash. Flying. You may cast spells as though they had flash. Grants flash to *all* your spells while it's on the battlefield — Windfall becomes an instant-speed play, wheels fire on opponents' end steps, and Mayhem recasts can respond to threats at instant speed. A transformative tempo piece.

### 🆕 Aetherdrift Inclusions (4 Cards)
*Recent cards from the Aetherdrift (DFT) set that slot neatly into the discard engine.*

*   **Captain Howler, Sea Scourge:** {2}{U}{R} 5/4 legendary Shark Pirate. Ward — pay {2} and 2 life. Whenever you discard one or more cards, target creature gets +2/+0 until end of turn for each card discarded. Whenever that creature deals combat damage to a player, draw a card. Discarding 4 cards gives any creature +8/+0 until end of turn — pump Neheb or Glint-Horn into a lethal attacker, and a successful hit draws a card. Ward of 4 total makes it expensive to remove.
*   **Cryptcaller Chariot:** {3}{B} 5/5 Vehicle with menace. Whenever you discard one or more cards, create that many tapped 2/2 black Zombie tokens. Crew 2. A Windfall discarding five cards creates five Zombie tokens with a single trigger — instant board presence that also crews itself next turn into a 5/5 menace attacker. The tokens also feed Behold the Sinister Six! by stocking the graveyard with creatures.
*   **Magmakin Artillerist:** {2}{R} 1/4. Whenever you discard one or more cards, deal that much damage to each opponent. Cycling {1}{R} — discard this card: draw a card; deal 1 damage to each opponent. Every discard event deals damage equal to cards discarded to each opponent — discarding 5 in a wheel deals 5 to everyone. Can also be cycled to draw a card AND deal 1 to each opponent, triggering all other discard payoffs on the cycle discard.
*   **Monument to Endurance:** {3} artifact. Whenever you discard a card, choose one that hasn't been chosen this turn — draw a card; create a Treasure token; each opponent loses 3 life. Three modes that rotate each turn: first discard draws, second makes Treasure, third pings 3. Resets every turn. In a wheel that discards 6 cards, the first three trigger all three modes (draw + Treasure + 3 damage), and then the cycle continues — second set of three draws another card, makes another Treasure, deals 3 more.

### 🛡️ Premium Interaction Suite (9 Cards)
*A top-tier protection package that holds up free counterspells the moment Norman Osborn is in play.*

*   **Fierce Guardianship:** ⚠️ **Game Changer.** {2}{U} instant — if you control your commander, counter target noncreature spell without paying its mana cost. A free counterspell the moment Norman is on the battlefield. Protects wheels from being countered, protects the transformation activation, and taxes opponents into leaving mana up every turn.
*   **Cyclonic Rift:** ⚠️ **Game Changer.** {1}{U} instant — Overload {6}{U} to return all nonland permanents opponents control to their hands. The most powerful board reset in Commander. One-sided wipes the entire table's board while leaving all your discard payoffs intact — the clean setup for a lethal Mayhem turn.
*   **Mana Drain:** {U}{U} instant — counter target spell; at the beginning of your next main phase, add that spell's mana cost in {C}. Counter a key spell and immediately bank the mana into the next Windfall, Behold the Sinister Six!, or Bringer of the Last Gift cast.
*   **Deflecting Swat:** {2}{R} instant — if you control your commander, redirect target spell or ability without paying its mana cost. A free redirect the moment Norman is on the board. Turns targeted removal pointed at Norman mid-transformation into an opponent's key threat.
*   **Deadly Rollick:** {3}{B} instant — if you control your commander, exile target creature without paying its mana cost. A free, unconditional creature exile while the commander is in play. No mana cost means leaving all mana up for Mana Drain or the Mayhem chain.
*   **Swan Song:** {U} instant — counter target enchantment, instant, or sorcery; that player creates a 2/2 Bird token. The cheapest counterspell in the deck. Counters opponent's wheel hate (Narset, Notion Thief-style effects) or stops a lethal spell. The 2/2 Bird is irrelevant at this power level.
*   **Strix Serenade:** {U} instant — counter target artifact, creature, or planeswalker spell; its controller creates a 2/2 blue Bird with flying. One-mana targeted counter for permanents. Stops the most dangerous resolved threats (commanders, key artifacts) while leaving the opponent with a consolation 2/2.
*   **Toxic Deluge:** {2}{B} sorcery — pay X life; all creatures get -X/-X until end of turn. Scales to answer any board state by paying life. Hits indestructible creatures and tokens that other board wipes miss. Essential mass removal in Grixis.
*   **Lethal Scheme:** {3}{B} instant — creatures you control connive X (draw and discard), where X is the number of creatures tapped to pay the cost. Each connive trigger fires all discard payoffs — with several creatures on board this removes a threat, draws cards, and activates Glint-Horn, Bone Miser, and Monument simultaneously.

### 🗡️ Targeted Removal & Utility (4 Cards)
*   **Bitter Triumph:** {1}{B} instant — destroy target creature or planeswalker; unless you discard a card. The discard mode is always correct in this deck — a free discard trigger from every discard payoff in exchange for not losing 3 life. Destroys any creature or planeswalker at instant speed.
*   **Snap:** {1}{U} instant — return target creature to its owner's hand; untap two lands. A zero-net-mana tempo play — return a threat and untap two lands to hold up a counterspell or fire a Demand Answers in the same turn.
*   **Dark Ritual:** {B} instant — add {B}{B}{B}. The most explosive turn-1 accelerant in the format. Leads into a turn-1 Bone Miser or a turn-2 Windfall before any opponent is set up to respond.
*   **Withering Torment:** {2}{B} instant — destroy target creature or enchantment; you lose 2 life. Instant-speed removal that answers both creatures and enchantments — Grixis normally can't hit enchantments, making this a crucial utility slot. The 2-life cost is negligible.

### ⚙️ Permanents & Equipment (2 Cards)
*   **Lightning Greaves:** {2}, equip {0}. Equipped creature has shroud and haste. Free-equip the moment Norman lands — shroud prevents targeted removal and haste enables an immediate attack to start conniving and filling the graveyard the turn he enters.
*   **Brass's Tunnel-Grinder / Tecutlan, the Searing Rift:** {2}{R} artifact. ETB — discard any number of cards, then draw that many plus one. At each end step, if you descended, add a bore counter. Transform at 3 bore counters into Tecutlan, the Searing Rift — a legendary Cave land that taps for {R} and triggers Discover X (where X is the mana value of any permanent spell cast with its mana). The ETB is an immediate net +1 draw while discarding for payoffs. Tecutlan converts every permanent spell into a free cascade effect — Discovering off a {4} mana creature finds another 4-drop.

## 🚀 Future Roadmap: Upgrades & Considerations

### 💡 High-Impact Tech to Consider
*   **Waste Not ({1}{B}):** Whenever opponents discard a creature, create a 2/2 Zombie. Nonland = add {B}{B}. Land = draw a card. The ultimate wheel supplement — every card opponents discard in a Windfall becomes mana, a Zombie, or a card for you. Transforms every symmetrical wheel into a deeply asymmetrical advantage.
*   **Wheel of Fortune ({R}{R}):** The most efficient wheel in the format at two mana. Adds a third wheel at the same power level as Windfall.
*   **Narset, Parter of Veils ({1}{U}{U}):** ⚠️ **Game Changer** (would reach 3/3). Opponents can only draw one card per turn while Narset is on the battlefield. Combine with Windfall — you draw 7, opponents draw 0. The definitive wheel asymmetry piece.
*   **Notion Thief ({2}{U}{B}):** ⚠️ **Game Changer** (would reach 3/3). Flash — whenever an opponent would draw one or more cards, you draw that many instead. Cast at instant speed in response to an opponent's draw spell to steal their entire hand refill.
*   **Teferi's Ageless Insight ({2}{U}{U}):** Whenever you draw a card, draw an additional card. Doubles the output of every wheel — Windfall drawing 7 draws 14.

### ⚠️ Bracket Notes
*   **Current Game Changers (2/3):** Cyclonic Rift, Fierce Guardianship.
*   Adding Narset **or** Notion Thief fills the Bracket 3 maximum. Adding both (or adding a third alongside either) pushes to Bracket 4.
*   Mana Drain and Deflecting Swat are not on the Game Changers list but significantly elevate the power ceiling — worth discussing with your pod.
